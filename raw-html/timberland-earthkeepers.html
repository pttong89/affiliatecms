How do I properly care for my Earthkeepers?

Proper maintenance extends boot life significantly and maintains performance. Clean regularly with soft brush and mild soap, removing dirt and salt residue promptly. Allow complete drying at room temperature, avoiding direct heat sources that can crack leather. Apply quality leather conditioner every 3-6 months or when leather appears dry. I recommend Montana Pitch Blend or similar premium conditioners. Store in cool, dry locations with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape. Replace laces when frayed and consider professional resoling when outsoles show significant wear. Regular inspection catches small issues before they become major problems requiring expensive repairs or replacement.
