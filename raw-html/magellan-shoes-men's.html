Waterproofing Maintenance

The Hydroguard treatment on Magellan boots will eventually wear down with use. I've found that reapplying waterproofing treatment every 50-75 miles of use maintains optimal performance. Signs that your boots need waterproofing renewal include:

  • Water no longer beads on the surface
  • Material appears darker when wet
  • Increased drying time after exposure to moisture
  • Any moisture penetration during normal use

Pro Maintenance Tip

Stuff boots with newspaper after wet hikes to absorb moisture and maintain shape. Replace newspaper every few hours until boots are completely dry. Never use direct heat sources like hair dryers or heaters, as this can damage waterproof membranes and cause premature aging of materials.

Storage and Seasonal Care

Proper storage during off-seasons can significantly extend boot life. For Magellan mens boots, I recommend a climate-controlled environment away from direct sunlight. If storing for extended periods, apply a thorough waterproofing treatment before storage to prevent cracking and maintain material flexibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Magellan shoes men's true to size?

Based on my extensive testing and customer feedback from Academy Sports, Magellan shoes men's models run true to size for most foot types. However, I recommend sizing up a half size if you plan to wear thick hiking socks or have particularly wide feet. The boots have a medium width profile, so those with narrow or very wide feet may need to adjust accordingly. For the most accurate fit, try them on in-store during the afternoon when your feet are naturally swollen, which better simulates hiking conditions.

How waterproof are Magellan hiking boots?

Magellan's Hydroguard waterproofing technology has proven highly effective in my real-world testing. I've crossed over 20 streams and hiked through persistent rain without any water penetration. The waterproofing includes a breathable membrane that prevents the clammy feeling common in budget waterproof boots. However, like all waterproof treatments, effectiveness diminishes over time and requires reapplication every 50-75 miles of use. The gusseted tongue design also helps prevent water entry through the lacing area, which is often a weak point in other brands.

What's the break-in period for Magellan boots?

One of the standout features of Magellan shoes men's is their minimal break-in period. Most models can be worn on day hikes immediately after purchase without significant discomfort.
