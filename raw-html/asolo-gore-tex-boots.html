Professional Fitting Tips

1. Measure Your Feet Properly

  • Measure feet in the afternoon when they're naturally larger
  • Measure both feet - use the larger measurement
  • Wear your typical hiking socks during fitting

2. Test the Fit

  • Thumb's width space between longest toe and boot end
  • No heel slippage when walking downhill
  • Snug but not tight across the midfoot

3. Model-Specific Notes

  • Fugitive GTX: Runs true to size, medium width
  • TPS 520: Size up ½ for thick socks
  • Space GV: Consider wide fit if between sizes

Care & Maintenance: Maximizing Your Investment

Essential Care Routine

After Each Hike

  • Remove excess dirt with soft brush
  • Allow boots to air dry naturally (never direct heat)
  • Remove insoles to speed drying
  • Stuff with newspaper if very wet

Deep Cleaning (Monthly)

  • Use specialized boot cleaner or mild soap
  • Scrub gently with soft brush
  • Rinse thoroughly and air dry
  • Apply leather conditioner to leather models

Waterproofing (Every 3-6 months)

  • Clean boots thoroughly first
  • Apply DWR treatment to outer surface
  • Allow 24 hours to cure before use
  • Test water repellency before next hike

Extending Boot Lifespan

Storage Best Practices

  • Store in cool, dry place
  • Use cedar shoe trees to maintain shape
  • Avoid plastic bags or sealed containers
  • Keep away from direct sunlight

Usage Tips

  • Rotate between multiple pairs if hiking frequently
  • Use appropriate socks (avoid cotton)
  • Consider gaiters for extra protection
  • Break in gradually before major hikes

When to Resole

  • Vibram tread worn down to 2-3mm
  • Heel or toe showing excessive wear
  • Loss of traction on familiar terrain
  • Before sole separation begins

Pro Tip: Proper care can extend Asolo boots lifespan to 10+ years. My oldest pair has over 2,000 miles and is still going strong!

How do Asolo Gore Tex boots perform in extreme weather?

Which Asolo model is best for beginners?

The Asolo Fugitive GTX is perfect for beginners. It offers excellent versatility for day hiking and light backpacking, has minimal break-in requirements, and provides the classic Asolo comfort and durability at a more accessible price point.

How long is the typical break-in period?

Asolo boots have significantly shorter break-in periods compared to other premium brands. Synthetic models like the Fugitive GTX feel comfortable after 15-25 miles of hiking. Full leather models like the TPS 520 require 40-60 miles for complete break-in.

My recommendation: wear new Asolo hiking boots for 2-3 short hikes (3-5 miles each) before attempting longer adventures. The quality materials and construction minimize the painful break-in experience common with other brands.

Do Asolo boots work well for wide feet?

Yes, but with important considerations. Asolo offers dedicated wide-fit models that are 5mm wider in the forefoot area. However, wide options aren't available in every model. The standard fit tends to run narrow compared to American brands.

For wide feet, I recommend trying the wide-fit versions of popular models like the Fugitive GTX Wide or considering the women's models which sometimes offer better width accommodation depending on your foot shape.

Can Asolo Gore Tex boots be resoled?

Yes! Most Asolo models feature resoleable construction, which significantly extends their lifespan. The process typically costs $80-120 and can be done 2-3 times over the boot's lifetime, depending on upper condition.

Asolo offers official resoling services, and many local cobblers can work with Vibram soles. This feature alone justifies the higher initial cost, as you're essentially buying boots that can serve you for decades with proper maintenance and periodic resoling.
