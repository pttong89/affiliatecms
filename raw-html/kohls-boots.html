Seasonal Shopping Strategy

Through years of shopping at Kohl's, I've identified the optimal timing for boot purchases that can save you significant money while ensuring the best selection. Here's my insider knowledge on when to shop for different types of boots.

Spring Shopping (March-May)

Spring is clearance season for winter boots. I've found incredible deals on insulated hiking boots during this period, often 50-70% off regular prices. This is also when new hiking boot collections arrive, perfect for planning summer adventures.

Pro Tip: Stock up on next year's winter boots now. I bought my current Columbia snow boots for $45 (originally $120) during a March clearance event.

Summer Shopping (June-August)

Summer focuses on hiking boots and water-resistant styles. New fall collections start arriving in August, giving you early access to the latest styles. This is prime time for general footwear shopping at Kohl's.

Best For: Hiking boots, outdoor gear, and preparing for fall fashion trends.

Fall Shopping (September-November)

Peak boot season offers the largest selection but premium pricing. This is when I focus on fashion boots and work boots for the upcoming season. Black Friday sales can offer exceptional value on premium brands.

Strategy: Shop early September for best selection, wait until Black Friday for best prices.

Winter Shopping (December-February)

Focus shifts to immediate needs - snow boots, winter hiking gear, and weather-resistant options. January clearance events offer great opportunities for fashion boots and dress boots.

Emergency Buys: Don't wait if you need winter boots immediately - safety and comfort are worth full price.
