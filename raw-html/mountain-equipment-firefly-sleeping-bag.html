Technical Specifications and Construction

Understanding the technical specifications of the Mountain Equipment Firefly sleeping bag requires diving deep into the materials and construction methods that enable its exceptional performance. How much does the Mountain Equipment Firefly weigh?

The Mountain Equipment Firefly weighs just 560g (1lb 4oz) in the regular size and 600g (1lb 5oz) in the long version. What type of down insulation does the Firefly use?

The Firefly uses premium 800+ fill power goose down with a 93/7 down-to-feather ratio. This high fill power provides excellent loft and thermal efficiency while maintaining the ultralight design. Is the Mountain Equipment Firefly suitable for winter camping?

The Mountain Equipment Firefly is not designed for winter camping in cold climates. With its -3°C (27°F) lower limit rating, it's optimized for three-season use from late spring to early autumn. For winter conditions, you'd need a bag rated to much lower temperatures. The Firefly excels in mild to moderate conditions where its ultralight design and excellent packability shine.
