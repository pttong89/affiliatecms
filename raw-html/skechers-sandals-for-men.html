Frequently Asked Questions

Are Skechers sandals good for men with flat feet?

Yes, skechers sandals for men with Arch Fit technology are excellent for men with flat feet. The podiatrist-certified arch support provides proper foot alignment and reduces strain on flat arches. Models like the Arch Fit Motley series feature contoured footbeds that accommodate flat feet while providing cushioning and stability for all-day comfort. During my consultations with foot health professionals, they consistently recommend these sandals for patients with flat feet who need supportive yet comfortable footwear.

How do I choose the right size in Skechers sandals for men?

Skechers sandals typically run true to size, but I recommend ordering a half-size up if you have wide feet or plan to wear thick socks. The Relaxed Fit models offer more room in the toe box and may require going down a half-size for some feet. Always check the specific product measurements and read customer reviews for sizing insights, as some styles like the Slip-Ins may fit differently than traditional sandals. My personal testing shows that trying them on late in the day when your feet are naturally more swollen provides the most accurate fit assessment.

Can Skechers sandals for men be worn in water?

Many skechers sandals for men are water-friendly, particularly the Tresmen series and GO Walk models. These feature quick-dry materials and drainage systems that make them perfect for beach, pool, or river activities. However, avoid prolonged water exposure with leather models as it can damage the material and affect longevity. Always check the product specifications to confirm water resistance before purchasing. My river testing has shown that synthetic models dry completely within 30 minutes of removal from water.

How long do Skechers sandals for men typically last?

With regular use, quality skechers sandals for men typically last 12-18 months depending on usage frequency, terrain, and care. Outdoor models like the Tresmen series tend to last longer due to reinforced construction and durable materials. Proper care, including regular cleaning and avoiding extreme temperatures, can extend their lifespan significantly. The memory foam may compress slightly over time but usually maintains adequate comfort for the sandal's entire lifetime. My personal pairs have maintained their structural integrity and comfort well beyond the 18-month mark with proper rotation and care.

What's the difference between Skechers Relaxed Fit and regular fit sandals?

Relaxed Fit skechers sandals for men offer a roomier toe box and wider overall fit compared to regular models. They're designed for men with wider feet or those who prefer a less snug fit. The Relaxed Fit models typically add about 5mm of extra width and depth, making them ideal for extended wear or foot conditions that require additional space without compromising support. During my testing with men of various foot sizes, the Relaxed Fit consistently accommodated wider feet comfortably while still providing adequate heel security and arch support.
