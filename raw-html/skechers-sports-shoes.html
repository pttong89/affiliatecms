Do Skechers sports shoes run true to size?

Skechers sports shoes generally run true to size, though individual models may vary slightly. In my experience testing multiple styles, most align with standard US sizing. The GO RUN series tends to fit true to size with medium width, while some lifestyle-oriented models may run slightly narrow. The slip-in technology models often feel more accommodating due to the flexible heel design. I recommend trying shoes later in the day when feet are naturally more swollen for the most accurate fit assessment. For online purchases, check specific model reviews as some users report variations. The memory foam insoles do conform to foot shape over time, providing a more customized fit after breaking in. When in doubt, consider ordering half a size up, especially for longer runs or extended wear.

What makes Skechers memory foam special?

Skechers memory foam technology stands out due to its responsive temperature-sensitive formulation that molds to individual foot contours while maintaining supportive properties. Unlike basic foam insoles, Skechers memory foam provides targeted pressure relief in high-impact areas while maintaining arch support integrity. The material responds to body heat, creating a customized fit that improves over time. In my testing, this foam maintains its shape and support properties significantly longer than generic alternatives. The Arch Fit variant includes podiatrist-certified design elements that provide additional biomechanical benefits. The foam works synergistically with other technologies like HyperBurst midsoles to create a comprehensive comfort system. This combination addresses both immediate comfort and long-term foot health considerations.

Can I wash Skechers sports shoes in the machine?

Many Skechers sports shoes are machine washable, though specific care instructions vary by model. Models with mesh uppers and basic construction typically handle gentle machine washing well. Remove insoles and laces before washing, use cold water on gentle cycle, and air dry completely. Avoid putting shoes in the dryer as heat can damage adhesives and foam materials. Some premium models with leather components or specialized technologies may require hand cleaning only. I've successfully machine washed several pairs of GO WALK and basic athletic models without issues. Always check the specific model's care label or Skechers website for official recommendations. For best results and longevity, hand cleaning with mild soap and water remains the safest approach for maintaining shoe integrity and appearance.
