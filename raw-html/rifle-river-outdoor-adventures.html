How difficult is tubing on the Rifle River for beginners?

Rifle River tubing is exceptionally beginner-friendly, making it perfect for first-time river adventurers and families with children. During my experiences guiding newcomers, I've found that basic swimming ability is sufficient, and life jackets are provided for additional safety.
