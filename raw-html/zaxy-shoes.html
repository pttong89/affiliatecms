Brand History and Brazilian Heritage

The Grendene Legacy

Zaxy shoes are manufactured by Grendene, Brazil's largest footwear company, which also produces the internationally acclaimed Melissa shoes. Founded in 1971, Grendene has become synonymous with innovative plastic footwear that doesn't compromise on style or quality.

Launched in 2009, Zaxy shoes emerged as Grendene's answer to the growing demand for fashionable yet affordable jelly footwear. While Melissa focused on high-end designer collaborations, Zaxy was positioned to capture the hearts of younger, more price-conscious consumers who still desired quality and style. This strategic positioning allowed the brand to quickly establish itself in international markets.

Brazilian Craftsmanship Tradition

Brazil's footwear industry has a rich tradition dating back decades, with the country becoming a global leader in innovative shoe design and production. The tropical climate and beach culture of Brazil naturally led to the development of waterproof, comfortable footwear suitable for various weather conditions. Zaxy shoes embody this heritage, combining traditional Brazilian aesthetics with modern manufacturing techniques.

How should I care for my Zaxy shoes?
