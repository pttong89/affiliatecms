Understanding Sandro's Sizing Philosophy

Sandro boots typically run true to size in length but tend toward a narrower fit compared to many American and British boot brands. This reflects their European construction heritage and the slim, elegant silhouette that defines their aesthetic. I've found that individuals with average-width feet should order their normal size, while those with wider feet may benefit from sizing up half a size. The leather's natural stretch during the break-in period accommodates slight fit adjustments, but it's crucial to start with appropriate sizing.
