Sizing and Fit Recommendations

KEEN boots generally run about half a size large, but individual foot shape significantly influences fit. I recommend ordering your normal shoe size first, as the generous toe box design accommodates most foot shapes comfortably. Women with particularly narrow feet might consider going down half a size, while those with wider feet often find the standard sizing perfect. The Original Fit design philosophy prioritizes toe room over snug heel fit, which some users love and others find too loose.

Try boots on in the afternoon when your feet are slightly swollen to simulate hiking conditions. Wear the same thickness socks you plan to use for hiking – typically medium-weight merino wool hiking socks work best with these boots. The heel should feel secure without slipping, and you should have about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the boot's front. If possible, test them on an inclined surface to check for heel lift during uphill and downhill movement.
