Buying Guide and Sizing: Making the Right Choice

Critical Sizing Information

Order ONE FULL SIZE LARGER than your normal shoe size. This is not optional advice – it's essential for proper fit. I typically wear size 8 in athletic shoes but needed size 9 in the BearPaw Corsica boots.

The sizing recommendation exists because the boots are designed to accommodate the natural foot swelling that occurs during hiking, plus the suede material initially fits snugly before stretching to conform to your foot shape.

Sizing Guide and Fit Tips

How to Determine Your Size

  1. Measure your feet in the evening when they're naturally swollen
  2. Add one full size to your normal athletic shoe size
  3. If you're between sizes, round up rather than down
  4. Consider wide widths if you typically need them
  5. Account for hiking sock thickness in your decision

Initial Fit Expectations

When you first try on properly sized BearPaw Corsica hiking boots, they should feel:

  • Snug but not painfully tight
  • Secure around the heel with minimal slipping
  • Room for toe movement without cramping
  • Comfortable for 10-15 minutes of walking

Remember: The suede will stretch and the sheepskin lining will mold to your feet over the first few wears, creating a custom fit.
