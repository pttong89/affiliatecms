Can I wear UGG boots for hiking and outdoor activities?

How do outdoor UGG boots fit compared to regular shoes?

Most outdoor UGG boots run slightly large due to their generous sheepskin lining and relaxed fit design. Many users recommend ordering a half size smaller than your normal shoe size.
