Key Features and Technologies

Microguard Insulation

Jack Wolfskin's proprietary Microguard synthetic insulation represents a significant advance in non-down sleeping bag technology. This two-component system combines high-loft fibers for insulation with a stabilizing outer layer that prevents compression and migration.

  • Maintains loft when compressed repeatedly
  • Performs well in humid conditions
  • Machine washable without performance loss

Thermozone Technology

This intelligent insulation mapping system places extra warmth precisely where your body loses heat most rapidly. After studying thermal imaging data, Jack Wolfskin strategically adds insulation layers to the head, core, and foot areas.

  • Reduces overall weight while improving warmth
  • Eliminates cold spots in critical areas
