Sizing and Fit Guide: Getting the Perfect Match

Achieving the perfect fit with bershka shoes requires understanding their unique sizing characteristics. After fitting dozens of styles and consulting with store associates across three countries, I've developed a comprehensive sizing strategy that minimizes return hassles and maximizes comfort.

Bershka uses European sizing as their primary standard, which can create confusion for US customers. Their size 40 generally corresponds to US women's 9 or men's 7, but this varies by style and intended fit. Platform boots tend to run larger due to their chunky construction, while sleek sneakers often feel tighter through the midfoot area.

My Personal Sizing Recommendations

Chunky Sneakers

Size up 0.5 size from your normal US size. Wide feet should consider a full size up. The thick sole design accommodates slightly loose fitting.

Platform Boots

True to size or size up 0.5 if between sizes. The ankle construction is less forgiving than their sneaker lines.

Dress Shoes

Order your exact European size. These styles prioritize sleek silhouettes over roomy comfort.

Width considerations are crucial, as Bershka primarily caters to medium-width feet. Those with narrow feet often find their chunky styles too roomy, while wide-footed customers struggle with their sleeker designs. Unlike brands such as Aetrex shoes that offer width variations, Bershka maintains a one-size-fits-all width approach.

Seasonal fitting variations also affect comfort. During summer months, I size up an additional 0.5 size to account for foot swelling during long wear periods. This strategy proved invaluable during my hiking adventures that required more walking than typical fashion footwear encounters. For serious outdoor activities, however, I still prefer dedicated hiking footwear with proper arch support and moisture management.

Sizing Warning: Always check recent customer reviews for specific styles before ordering, as Bershka occasionally updates their lasts (foot-shaped forms used in shoe construction) without changing the style name, resulting in fit variations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Bershka shoes true to size?

Bershka shoes generally run small compared to standard US sizing. I recommend sizing up by half a size, especially for their chunky sneakers and platform boots. Their European sizing tends to be more accurate, but always check their size guide and read recent customer reviews for specific styles before ordering. Wide-footed customers should consider sizing up a full size for optimal comfort.

How long do Bershka shoes typically last?

Based on my experience and user feedback, Bershka shoes typically last 6-12 months with regular wear. While they're not built for long-term durability like premium brands, they offer excellent value for trend-focused footwear. Proper care, rotation with other shoes, and avoiding harsh weather conditions can extend their lifespan significantly. Styles with STARFIT® technology tend to maintain comfort longer.

What's the return policy for Bershka shoes?

Bershka offers a 30-day return policy for unworn items with original packaging and tags. Online purchases can be returned to physical stores or via mail. I recommend trying shoes immediately upon arrival and keeping all packaging until you're certain about the fit and comfort. International returns may have longer processing times, so factor this into your ordering timeline when purchasing for specific events.

Are Bershka shoes comfortable for all-day wear?

Comfort varies significantly by style. Their chunky sneakers with foam insoles are surprisingly comfortable for 6-8 hours of wear, making them suitable for casual day activities and light urban exploration. However, their platform boots and high heels are better suited for shorter periods. I always recommend breaking them in gradually and considering gel insoles for extended wear situations like shopping trips or social events.
