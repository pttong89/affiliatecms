Understanding La Sportiva's Unique Sizing System

La Sportiva uses European sizing with their own last specifications, which can differ significantly from other brands and even between their own models. When shopping for rei la sportiva climbing shoes, it's essential to understand that a size 42 in one model may feel completely different in another. During my extensive testing, I've found that most climbers need to size down 1-1.5 sizes from their street shoe size, but this varies considerably based on the specific model and desired fit.

The Tarantulace, being a comfort-oriented shoe, typically requires only a half-size to full-size reduction from street shoes. In contrast, aggressive models like the Solution or Skwama may require sizing down 1.5 to 2 full sizes. My personal experience with Solutions required going from a size 44 street shoe to a size 42 in the Solution, while my Tarantulace fit perfectly at size 43.
