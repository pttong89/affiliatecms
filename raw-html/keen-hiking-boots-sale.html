What maintenance do KEEN hiking boots require?

KEEN boots require minimal maintenance but benefit from regular care to maximize their lifespan. Clean them after muddy hikes using a soft brush and mild soap, allow them to air dry completely before storage, and reapply waterproofing treatment every 6-12 months depending on usage. Remove insoles periodically to air dry and prevent odor buildup. Store boots in a cool, dry place away from direct heat sources. Proper maintenance can extend the life of boots purchased during keen hiking boots sale events, ensuring you get maximum value from your investment.
