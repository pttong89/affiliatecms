The Magellan Outdoors boots lineup features several distinct waterproof approaches. How should I care for my Magellan waterproof boots?

Clean boots after each use with mild soap and water, allowing them to air dry completely. Apply waterproofing treatment every 3-6 months or after heavy use. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct heat. Remove insoles periodically for cleaning and drying. Proper care extends the life and waterproof performance significantly. For rubber models, simple hose-down cleaning is sufficient, while leather boots benefit from conditioning treatments to prevent cracking.
