Best Places to Find Clearance Camping Gear

REI Garage Sales

REI's legendary Garage Sales offer the deepest discounts on returned and gently used gear. I've personally scored a $300 sleeping bag for $89 at their Seattle location. These quarterly events require early arrival – I recommend lining up 2 hours before opening for the best selection.

Pro Tip: Sign up for REI emails to get advance notice of garage sale dates and locations.

Steep & Cheap

As Backcountry.com's outlet store, Steep & Cheap consistently offers 40-60% discounts on name-brand gear. Their daily deals feature premium brands like Patagonia, Arc'teryx, and Black Diamond at fraction of retail prices.

Best Strategy: Check their "Gear Cache" section for extended-term deals beyond daily specials.

Major sporting goods retailers like Dick's Sporting Goods, Sportsman's Warehouse, and Bass Pro Shops maintain dedicated clearance sections year-round. Dick's consistently marks down end-of-season camping gear by 40-70%, while Sportsman's Warehouse offers excellent deals on hunting and camping equipment. I've found that visiting physical locations often reveals deeper markdowns than their online clearance sections.

Online outlet stores deserve special attention for clearance outdoor gear hunters. CampSaver Outlet regularly offers up to 97% off retail prices on last year's models, while Enwild provides deep discounts with their "Awesome Gear, Amazing Service" guarantee. These specialized outlets often carry technical gear that mainstream retailers don't stock.

Understanding retail cycles revolutionizes clearance camping gear shopping. The sweetest deals emerge during transition periods when retailers clear seasonal inventory. Late summer through early fall represents the golden window for camping gear, as stores make room for winter sports equipment. I've consistently found 50-70% discounts during these periods on everything from camping tent clearance sales to sleeping bags and outdoor cooking equipment.

Holiday shopping periods offer unexpected opportunities beyond Black Friday. The week between Christmas and New Year's consistently delivers exceptional clearance deals as retailers clear holiday inventory. Presidents Day weekend often features outdoor gear sales, while Memorial Day marks the beginning of summer gear clearance at many retailers.

Weather patterns significantly impact clearance timing. Unseasonably warm falls extend summer gear clearance periods, while early winters accelerate markdowns. Monitor local weather forecasts and retail inventory levels – a sudden cold snap can trigger immediate clearance pricing on remaining summer camping gear.

Expert Timing Tip

Set up price tracking alerts on key items using tools like Honey or Keepa. Many clearance items cycle through multiple markdowns before reaching rock-bottom prices. Patience often rewards shoppers with an additional 20-30% savings beyond initial clearance pricing.

Shelter Systems

Tents represent the highest-value clearance opportunities. Premium 3-season tents from Big Agnes, REI Co-op, and Sierra Designs frequently appear at 40-60% off retail. Focus on established models rather than first-year releases for proven reliability.

Sleep Systems

Sleeping bags and pads offer excellent clearance value. Down bags maintain performance regardless of color or previous season's features. Look for established temperature ratings and reputable fill power specifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to buy clearance camping gear?

The optimal times for clearance camping gear are late summer through fall (August-October), post-holiday season (January-February), and spring transitions (March-April). Retailers clear seasonal inventory during these periods, offering discounts of 40-70% off regular prices. End-of-season sales typically provide the deepest discounts as stores make room for new inventory. Weather patterns can also trigger additional clearance events – an early winter often accelerates summer gear markdowns, while warm fall weather extends clearance periods.

Where can I find the best clearance camping gear deals?

Top destinations include REI Garage Sales (up to 50% off returned items), Steep & Cheap (daily deals up to 60% off), major retailers like Dick's Sporting Goods and Sportsman's Warehouse clearance sections, and online outlets like CampSaver and Enwild. Amazon's warehouse deals and manufacturer direct sales also offer significant savings on quality camping equipment. Local outdoor retailers often provide the best personal service and early access to clearance events through customer relationships and email lists.

How can I ensure clearance camping gear is still good quality?

Check return policies, read customer reviews thoroughly, inspect product descriptions for defects, verify warranty coverage, and stick to reputable brands even on clearance. Many clearance items are simply last season's colors or models with identical functionality. REI's satisfaction guarantee and established retailer return policies provide additional protection for clearance purchases. Focus on core performance specifications rather than cosmetic updates, as technical performance remains constant regardless of clearance status.

What camping gear should I prioritize when shopping clearance sales?

Focus on high-ticket items like tents, sleeping bags, backpacks, and cookware where savings are substantial. Quality base layers, rain gear, and technical clothing also provide excellent value on clearance. Women's hiking shoes clearance and walking boots clearance opportunities should be seized when proper fit is confirmed. Avoid clearance electronics or items requiring warranties, and prioritize durable goods from established outdoor brands that maintain quality standards across all price points.

Are there specific brands that offer the best clearance camping gear values?

REI Co-op, Coleman, Big Agnes, ALPS Mountaineering, and Kelty frequently appear in clearance sales with excellent value retention. These brands maintain quality standards while offering competitive clearance pricing. Patagonia and North Face clearance items, while less frequent, provide premium quality at reduced prices. Timberland hiking boots clearance and Merrell women's hiking shoes clearance events offer exceptional value from trusted footwear specialists. Focus on brands with strong warranty support and established reputations in the outdoor industry.
