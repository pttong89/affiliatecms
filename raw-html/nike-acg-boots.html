Nike ACG boots generally run true to size, but hiking demands differ from casual wear. I recommend sizing up by half a size to accommodate hiking socks and natural foot swelling during long hikes. Care and Maintenance: Extending Your Nike ACG Boots' Lifespan

Proper maintenance transforms good boots into lifelong companions. After maintaining multiple pairs of Nike ACG boots through thousands of trail miles, I've developed a care regimen that maximizes durability and performance.

Post-Hike Cleaning Protocol

Immediate post-hike care prevents long-term damage. Remove mud and debris while wet—dried mud becomes abrasive and damages materials. I use a soft brush and lukewarm water, avoiding harsh soaps that can compromise waterproof treatments. For stubborn stains, a mild solution of water and gentle detergent works effectively.

Drying technique matters significantly. Never use direct heat sources like radiators or hair dryers, which can crack leather and damage synthetic materials. Instead, remove insoles and laces, stuff boots with newspaper, and air dry in a well-ventilated area. This method typically requires 12-24 hours but preserves boot integrity.

Waterproofing Maintenance

Gore-Tex membranes require periodic treatment to maintain optimal performance. I recommend reapplying DWR (Durable Water Repellent) coating every 40-50 hiking days or when water stops beading on the surface. Quality waterproofing products specifically designed for technical footwear provide the best results.

Storage Best Practices

Long-term storage requires attention to prevent material degradation. Store boots in a cool, dry location away from direct sunlight. Cedar shoe trees help maintain shape and absorb moisture. For extended storage periods, apply a light coating of leather conditioner to prevent cracking.

Maintenance Schedule: Clean after each use, deep clean monthly, waterproof treatment every 50 hiking days, professional inspection annually. This schedule has kept my oldest pair of Nike ACG boots performing optimally after three years of regular use.
