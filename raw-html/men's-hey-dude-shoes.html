Hey Dude shoes are manufactured exclusively in whole sizes, which initially concerned me as a half-size wearer. Frequently Asked Questions

Are men's Hey Dude shoes good for walking?

Men's Hey Dude shoes are excellent for short walks and casual daily activities, but they're not ideal for long-distance walking or hiking. The lightweight design and flexible sole provide comfort for everyday wear, but lack the structured support needed for extended walking sessions. Based on my experience, they're perfect for walking around towns, exploring parks on paved trails, and general daily activities. For serious hiking or long-distance walking, I recommend dedicated walking or hiking shoes with more substantial support and traction.

During my testing, I found them comfortable for walks up to 3-4 miles on relatively flat terrain. Beyond that distance, the lack of arch support and cushioning becomes noticeable. They're ideal for travel, sightseeing, and casual outdoor activities where comfort and convenience are priorities over performance.

Do men's Hey Dude shoes run true to size?

Men's Hey Dude shoes typically run true to size, but since they only come in full sizes, it's recommended to size up if you normally wear a half-size. The stretchy materials accommodate slight size variations, and most users find their regular shoe size works well. However, foot shape plays a significant role in fit satisfaction.

From my extensive testing with different foot types, I recommend ordering your regular size first if you have average width feet. Wide-footed individuals should definitely consider sizing up, especially in the Wally series. The Paul collection tends to run slightly more true to traditional sizing due to its more structured design.

The elastic components in most models provide significant accommodation for different foot shapes. I suggest ordering from retailers with good return policies to ensure the perfect fit, as individual foot characteristics can significantly impact comfort and satisfaction.

How long do men's Hey Dude shoes last?

The durability of men's Hey Dude shoes varies depending on usage. With regular casual wear, they typically last 6-12 months. However, heavy daily use may require replacement every 2-3 months. The lightweight construction prioritizes comfort over long-term durability, which is an important consideration for potential buyers.

In my experience, the sole and upper materials begin showing wear after about 200-300 miles of walking. For occasional wear and travel, I've had pairs last over a year while maintaining good comfort and appearance. Daily work wear, especially in demanding environments, accelerates wear significantly.

The machine washable feature helps maintain appearance and hygiene throughout the shoe's lifespan. While they may not last as long as traditional leather shoes, the comfort benefits and reasonable pricing make replacement costs manageable for most users. I typically budget for replacement every 8-10 months with regular use.

Can you wear men's Hey Dude shoes without socks?

Yes, men's Hey Dude shoes are designed to be worn comfortably without socks, thanks to their soft interior materials and moisture-wicking properties. Many users prefer the barefoot feel, especially during warm weather. However, wearing thin socks can extend the shoe's lifespan and improve hygiene.

I personally prefer wearing them with no-show or ankle socks for better moisture management and reduced odor. The breathable materials work well for sockless wear during short periods, but for all-day use or in humid conditions, thin socks provide better comfort and foot health. The machine washable feature makes maintenance easy regardless of your sock preference.

Are Hey Dude shoes good for people with flat feet?

Men's Hey Dude shoes provide minimal arch support, which can be problematic for people with flat feet who require structured support. The flexible design and memory foam insole offer comfort for mild foot issues, but they're not recommended for those with significant flat feet or other foot conditions requiring orthotic support.

Based on feedback from users with flat feet, some find them comfortable for short periods, while others experience discomfort during extended wear. If you have flat feet, I recommend consulting with a podiatrist and possibly using custom insoles with Hey Dude shoes. The removable insole design makes it possible to accommodate custom orthotics in some models.
