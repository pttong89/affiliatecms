Sizing and Fit Considerations

Salomon sizing tends to run slightly narrow compared to American brands like Merrell or Keen. I recommend trying on shoes in the afternoon when feet are naturally swollen, simulating hiking conditions. For outlet purchases, consider ordering half a size up if you're between sizes, especially for models intended for winter hiking with thicker socks.

Fit Testing Checklist:

  • Ensure 1/2 inch space between longest toe and shoe front
  • Check for heel slippage during walking motion
  • Verify adequate width accommodation without pinching
  • Test lacing system adjustment range
  • Confirm comfortable arch support placement

Technology and Feature Priorities

Focus on core technologies that align with your hiking style. Gore-Tex waterproofing adds $30-50 to retail prices but provides essential protection for wet conditions. The Contagrip outsole system offers superior traction across diverse terrain types, justifying the premium over basic rubber compounds.
