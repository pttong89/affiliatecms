What is the best way to clean Ozelia shoes?

Cleaning Ozelia shoes requires a careful approach due to their mixed-material construction. Start by removing loose dirt with a soft-bristled brush, then create a mild cleaning solution using warm water and gentle detergent. Use a microfiber cloth or soft brush to clean synthetic leather areas, avoiding excessive water saturation. The textile portions can handle slightly more moisture but should still be cleaned gently. Regular maintenance with appropriate leather/synthetic cleaners will preserve their appearance longer.
