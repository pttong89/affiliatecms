Can you machine wash Skechers waterproof boots?

Most Skechers walking boots are machine washable, but this can compromise waterproofing over time. For waterproof models, hand cleaning is recommended to preserve the protective membrane. Use lukewarm water, mild soap, and a soft brush to clean the exterior. Remove insoles and laces for separate washing. Avoid direct heat when drying; instead, stuff boots with newspaper and air dry at room temperature. For tough stains, specialized boot cleaners work better than household detergents. If machine washing is necessary, use cold water on gentle cycle and skip the spin cycle to prevent damage to waterproof seals.
