Sizing Guide and Fit Tips

Getting the right fit with INC shoes requires understanding their unique sizing characteristics. Through extensive testing and customer feedback analysis, I've developed a comprehensive sizing strategy that will save you time and return hassles.

General Sizing Guidelines

Most INC shoes run true to size in length, but many styles tend to run narrow in width. This is particularly noticeable in their pointed-toe pumps and boots. If you typically wear a medium width, I recommend trying on both regular and wide width options when available.

For heels above 3 inches, consider going up half a size. The elevated heel naturally pushes your foot forward, and the extra room prevents pinching at the toe box. I learned this lesson the hard way with my first pair of Zitah pumps - stunning shoes, but initially too tight until I sized up.

Professional Fitting Tips

I always recommend shopping for shoes in the afternoon or evening when your feet are naturally more swollen - this ensures a comfortable fit throughout the day. When trying on INC shoes at Macy's, walk around the store for at least 5 minutes to test comfort levels.

For online purchases, take advantage of Macy's generous return policy. Order two sizes if you're uncertain - you can always return the pair that doesn't fit. I've found this strategy particularly helpful with new INC styles that don't have established reviews yet.
