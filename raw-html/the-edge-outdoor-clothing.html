What temperature range is The Edge clothing suitable for?

The Edge outdoor clothing is designed for typical skiing conditions, generally comfortable in temperatures ranging from -10°C to +5°C (14°F to 41°F). For extremely cold conditions below -15°C, additional layering might be necessary, as the edge outdoor clothing prioritizes versatility over extreme weather performance. Most users find the temperature range perfectly suited for UK winter conditions and European ski resorts during peak season.
