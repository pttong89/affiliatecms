LOWA Sizing Fundamentals

LOWA uses European sizing with some unique characteristics that differ from other manufacturers. Understanding these differences prevents costly sizing mistakes.

The 15mm Rule

LOWA recommends 15mm extra length beyond your longest toe. This accounts for foot expansion during long hikes and proper downhill positioning.

Width Considerations

Available in medium and wide widths. Most hikers need medium width, but those with high-volume feet or wide forefeet benefit from wide options.

Break-in Requirements

Allow 20-40 miles for full break-in. Leather models require more time than synthetic alternatives.
