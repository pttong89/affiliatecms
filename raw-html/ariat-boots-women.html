How should Ariat boots fit when you first buy them?

Ariat boots women purchase should fit snugly but not painfully tight when new. Your heel should sit securely in the heel cup with minimal slipping, and there should be about a thumb's width of space at the toe. The boots will feel tighter initially as the leather needs 20-40 hours of wear to break in and conform to your foot shape. Expect some initial stiffness in the shaft and around the ankle area, which will soften over time. If you experience significant pain or pressure points, consider trying a different size or width.
