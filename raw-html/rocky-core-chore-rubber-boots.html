How should I clean and maintain Rocky Core Chore rubber boots?

Proper maintenance extends the life of Rocky Core Chore rubber boots significantly. After each use, rinse off mud and debris with clean water, paying attention to the sole's tread pattern. For deeper cleaning, use mild soap and a soft brush to scrub the rubber surfaces. Always allow boots to air dry completely before storage, preferably in a well-ventilated area away from direct heat sources.
