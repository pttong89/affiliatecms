What's the best way to care for and maintain LL Bean snow pants?

Proper care significantly extends the lifespan and performance of LL Bean women's snow pants. I recommend washing them in cold water with technical fabric detergent, avoiding fabric softeners that compromise waterproof coatings. Tumble dry on low heat or air dry to maintain insulation loft and membrane integrity. After approximately 20 wash cycles, consider applying DWR (Durable Water Repellent) treatment to restore surface water resistance. Store pants in a dry location with good air circulation, avoiding compression in storage bags. For minor repairs, LL Bean's repair services handle zipper replacements and seam repairs professionally. During ski season, I brush off snow before entering heated areas to prevent rapid temperature changes that stress materials. Regular inspection of high-wear areas like ankle cuffs and pocket zippers allows early intervention before problems become serious.
