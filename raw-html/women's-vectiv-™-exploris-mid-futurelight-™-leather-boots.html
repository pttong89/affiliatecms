Care and Maintenance Tips

Proper care extends the life and performance of your women's VECTIV™ Exploris Mid FUTURELIGHT™ leather boots significantly. The nubuck leather benefits from regular brushing with a soft bristle brush to maintain its texture and appearance. Apply a quality leather conditioner every 50-75 miles of use or when the leather appears dry.

The FUTURELIGHT™ membrane maintains its waterproof properties without additional treatment, but cleaning the exterior regularly prevents dirt buildup that can compromise breathability. Remove insoles after each use to allow proper drying, and stuff boots with newspaper if they become thoroughly soaked. Avoid direct heat sources, which can damage both the leather and membrane technology.
