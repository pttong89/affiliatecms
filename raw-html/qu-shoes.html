CloudTec Technology: The Science Behind the Comfort

The revolutionary CloudTec technology that defines qu shoes began with an unconventional inspiration: a garden hose. Founder Olivier Bernhard experimented with the curved shape of a hose, exploring how it could create a new type of cushioning system. When Nike rejected his prototype, Bernhard and his partners decided to develop the technology independently, leading to the creation of On and the birth of qu shoes. CloudTec technology operates on a simple yet brilliant principle: provide cushioning during landing while creating a solid platform for push-off. The hollow "clouds" underneath each shoe compress individually when your foot strikes the ground, absorbing impact and reducing stress on joints and muscles. This compression phase cushions your landing with remarkable efficiency, distributing forces across multiple contact points rather than concentrating them in a single area like traditional foam midsoles. The magic happens during the transition from landing to push-off. As you move through your gait cycle, the compressed clouds lock together to form a solid foundation for propulsion. This locking mechanism, achieved through precise engineering tolerances, eliminates the energy loss typically associated with soft foam midsoles. Instead of sinking into cushioning material during push-off, your foot gains leverage from a firm, responsive surface that maximizes energy return. Different qu shoes models utilize varying CloudTec configurations optimized for specific activities. Racing models feature aggressive cloud patterns designed for speed and responsiveness, while comfort-focused versions employ larger, softer clouds for maximum cushioning. The newest CloudTec Phase technology found in premium qu shoes models creates an even smoother transition by allowing clouds to collapse in sequence, creating a rolling motion that propels you forward naturally. The materials used in CloudTec construction have evolved significantly since the first qu shoes launched. Modern versions incorporate Helion superfoam, a proprietary material that maintains its properties across temperature ranges while providing consistent performance over thousands of miles. This advanced foam retains its shape and responsiveness longer than traditional EVA foam, ensuring your qu shoes maintain their performance characteristics throughout their lifespan. Environmental considerations also play a role in CloudTec technology development. Recent qu shoes models incorporate recycled materials in their CloudTec construction, demonstrating that performance and sustainability can coexist.
