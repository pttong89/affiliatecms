Sizing Guide: Finding Your Perfect Fit

Getting the right size in ugg ultra mini platform boots is crucial for both comfort and style. After helping numerous friends find their perfect fit and analyzing hundreds of reviews, I've developed a comprehensive sizing strategy that takes the guesswork out of ordering online.

The Golden Rule: Size Down

The most important thing to understand about UGG platform boots is that they typically run large. I recommend sizing down half a size from your normal shoe size for the best fit. If you're between sizes, always choose the smaller option.

Example: If you normally wear a size 8, order a size 7.5. If you normally wear a 7.5, order a size 7.

The reason for sizing down comes down to the UGGplush™ lining. When new, this lining is quite thick and plush, making the boots feel tight initially. However, the lining compresses and molds to your foot shape over the first few weeks of wear. What feels snug on day one becomes perfectly fitted by week three. I learned this the hard way with my first pair – ordering my true size resulted in boots that became too loose after break-in.

When trying on or receiving your ultra mini UGG platform boots, they should feel snug but not painfully tight. Your toes should lightly touch the front of the boot, and your heel should feel secure without slipping. If you can easily slip your finger between your heel and the back of the boot, you likely need to go down another half size.

Common Sizing Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ordering your true shoe size (usually results in loose fit after break-in)
  • Expecting them to feel comfortable immediately (they need a break-in period)
  • Not accounting for the platform when considering heel fit
  • Trying them on with thick socks (use regular or thin socks for accurate fit)
  • Assuming all UGG styles fit the same (platforms fit differently than regular boots)

Width considerations are also important. UGG boots are designed for medium-width feet. If you have particularly wide feet, you might need to go with your true size rather than sizing down, but be prepared for some looseness after break-in. For narrow feet, definitely size down and consider using heel grips if needed for the perfect fit.

Smart Shopping: Order from retailers with free returns like Amazon so you can try multiple sizes if needed. Many people order two sizes and return the one that doesn't fit.

The platform aspect does affect fit slightly. The 2-inch platform can make the boot feel different when walking compared to flat boots. Some people experience slight heel lifting initially, which usually resolves as the boots break in and conform to your foot shape. If heel lifting persists after two weeks of regular wear, you likely need a smaller size.

International sizing can be tricky, so always refer to the specific measurements rather than size conversions. UGG provides detailed size charts with foot length measurements, and I highly recommend measuring your feet at home and comparing to their charts rather than relying on size conversions between US, UK, and EU sizing systems.

Care and Maintenance Tips for Long-Lasting Boots

Proper care is essential for maintaining the appearance and longevity of your ugg ultra mini platform boots. After nearly two years of wearing mine regularly, I've developed a comprehensive care routine that keeps them looking almost new. Here's everything I've learned about preserving these investment pieces.

Protection is Prevention: Before wearing your new boots, invest in quality suede protector spray. This single step will save you hours of cleaning later and significantly extend the life of your boots.

Pre-Treatment Protection

The most important step happens before you ever wear your boots outside. Apply a high-quality suede and nubuck protector spray in a well-ventilated area, holding the bottle 6 inches away from the boot. Cover evenly but don't oversaturate – you want protection, not a color change. Allow 24 hours to dry completely before first wear. This creates an invisible barrier that repels water and prevents stains from setting in.

I learned this lesson the hard way with my first pair of UGG boots ultra mini style. Without pre-treatment, they picked up stains and water marks that were much harder to remove later. Don't skip this step – it's the most cost-effective maintenance you can do.

Daily Maintenance Routine

After each wear, especially in wet or dirty conditions, brush your boots with a suede brush while they're still slightly damp. Use light strokes in one direction to lift the nap and remove surface dirt. This prevents dirt from setting in and maintains the suede's texture. Store them in a cool, dry place with proper ventilation – avoid plastic bags or sealed containers that can trap moisture.

Essential Care Kit

  • Suede brush - For daily maintenance and texture restoration
  • Suede eraser - For removing scuff marks and light stains
  • Protector spray - Apply every 4-6 weeks or after cleaning
  • Gentle suede cleaner - For deeper cleaning when needed
  • Boot trees or paper - To maintain shape while drying
  • Clean cloth - For applying cleaners and general maintenance

Deep Cleaning Process

When your boots need more than surface maintenance, follow this deep cleaning process I've perfected over time. Remove excess dirt with a dry brush, then use a specialized suede cleaner following the manufacturer's instructions. Work in small sections, applying cleaner with a clean cloth and gently working it in circular motions. Remove excess cleaner with a clean, damp cloth, then brush the entire surface to restore texture.

The drying process is crucial – stuff boots with paper towels or boot trees to maintain shape, and allow them to air dry naturally away from direct heat or sunlight. This can take 24-48 hours, but patience here prevents cracking and shrinkage. Once completely dry, brush again to restore the suede nap, then reapply protector spray.

What NOT to Do

  • Never machine wash or dry UGG boots
  • Avoid using regular leather cleaners on suede
  • Don't use direct heat sources for drying (radiators, hair dryers, etc.)
  • Never submerge boots completely in water
  • Avoid wearing in heavy rain or snow without proper protection

Seasonal Storage Tips

Proper storage during off-seasons extends the life of your boots significantly. Clean them thoroughly before storage, ensuring they're completely dry. Stuff with acid-free tissue paper to maintain shape and absorb any residual moisture. Store in breathable dust bags or boxes in a cool, dry place with good air circulation.

Before bringing them out of storage, check for any signs of mold or mildew (especially in humid climates), brush to restore texture, and consider reapplying protector spray if it's been more than six months. This preparation ensures your UGG ultra mini boots are ready for another season of wear.

Maintenance Schedule: Daily brushing after wear, weekly inspection for damage, monthly deep cleaning if needed, and seasonal protection spray reapplication. This routine has kept my boots looking great for nearly two years of regular wear.

Remember that proper care is an investment in the longevity of your boots. The few extra minutes spent on daily maintenance and the occasional deeper cleaning session will keep your UGG Ultra Mini Platform boots looking and feeling like new for years to come. Quality care products may seem expensive initially, but they're much more cost-effective than replacing boots prematurely due to neglect.
