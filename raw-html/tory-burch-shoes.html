Complete Sizing Guide & Fit Tips

Getting the right size in Tory Burch shoes can be tricky because different styles fit differently. Here's my comprehensive sizing guide based on extensive testing:

Miller Sandals

Recommendation: Size up 0.5

These run narrow and slightly small. I'm normally a size 8 but wear 8.5 in Millers for optimal comfort during long wear.

Minnie Ballet Flats

Recommendation: True to size

These fit perfectly in your regular size. The leather is soft and stretches slightly with wear.

Good Luck Sneakers

Recommendation: Size up 0.5

The platform design runs small, especially in the toe box. Half size up ensures comfort.

Eleanor Pumps

Recommendation: True to size

Well-constructed with memory foam insole. Standard sizing works perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Tory Burch shoes run true to size?

Most Tory Burch shoes, particularly the Miller sandals, tend to run slightly small and narrow. I recommend sizing up half a size for most styles, especially if you have wide feet or plan to wear them for extended periods. The Minnie ballet flats are generally true to size, which is why they're so popular for travel and everyday wear. Always check specific style reviews before purchasing.

Are Tory Burch shoes worth the investment?

Yes, Tory Burch shoes are worth the investment for their quality construction, timeless designs, and durability. I've owned Miller sandals for over 5 years that still look great with regular wear. When you calculate cost-per-wear, the investment makes sense for frequently worn styles. The brand uses premium materials and construction techniques that justify the price point, especially for classic styles that won't go out of fashion.

How do I care for my Tory Burch shoes?

Proper care extends the life of your shoes significantly. For leather styles, use a quality leather protector before first wear and condition regularly with appropriate leather cream. Patent leather can be cleaned with a damp cloth and baking soda for scuff marks. Store shoes with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape, and rotate wear to allow materials to rest between uses. Avoid extreme temperatures and moisture when possible.

Which Tory Burch shoe style is most comfortable?

The Minnie Travel Ballet Flats are consistently rated as the most comfortable Tory Burch shoes, designed specifically for long wear and travel. They feature a cushioned insole and flexible construction. The Miller sandals are also very comfortable for a sandal, with a padded footbed. For those needing more arch support, the Good Luck Trainers offer athletic-inspired comfort with fashion-forward styling.

Where can I find the best deals on Tory Burch shoes?

The best deals are typically found during end-of-season sales at department stores like Nordstrom and Bloomingdale's. Amazon occasionally offers competitive pricing on select styles with authentic merchandise. Outlet stores carry previous seasons at reduced prices. I recommend signing up for newsletters from authorized retailers to be notified of sales. However, be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true, as counterfeit products are common with this brand.

After a decade of wearing and testing Tory Burch shoes, I can confidently say they represent excellent value for quality-conscious shoppers who appreciate timeless design. While the initial investment is significant, the durability and versatility make them a smart long-term purchase.
