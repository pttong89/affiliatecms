How do Sidi Adventure boots fit compared to regular shoes?

Sidi boots typically run narrow and small. Most riders need to size up 1-1.5 sizes from their regular shoe size. For example, if you wear US size 10 shoes, you'll likely need EU size 45-46 in Sidi boots. The narrow footbed can be problematic for riders with wide feet, though some find removing the insoles helps create more room. I recommend ordering from retailers with good return policies to try multiple sizes. The Sidi Adventure 2 Gore-Tex motorcycle boots fit guide suggests measuring your foot length and consulting size charts rather than assuming normal shoe sizing applies.

During my testing, the squeak was most noticeable during the first 1,000 miles but became much less prominent afterward.
