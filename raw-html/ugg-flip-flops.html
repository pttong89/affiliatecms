Care and Maintenance Instructions

Proper maintenance of UGG flip flops extends their lifespan significantly while preserving their luxurious appearance and comfort. The sheepskin construction requires different care approaches compared to synthetic materials, but with consistent attention, these sandals can provide years of reliable service.

Daily Care Routine

After each wear, allow your UGG flip flops to air dry naturally away from direct heat sources. Sheepskin naturally resists odor, but proper ventilation prevents moisture buildup that could affect the material's integrity. I learned this lesson during a humid summer in Yellowstone when I initially stored my damp flip flops in a closed bag, leading to temporary stiffness that required extra conditioning.

Never machine wash UGG flip flops - the delicate sheepskin and leather components can be permanently damaged by water immersion and agitation.

Deep Cleaning Process

For thorough cleaning, UGG recommends their specialized cleaning kit containing sheepskin-safe products. Begin by removing surface dirt with a soft brush, working in one direction to maintain the wool's natural lay. Apply UGG cleaner sparingly to stained areas, using circular motions with minimal pressure. Allow the product to work for several minutes before wiping away residue with a damp cloth.

The conditioning step is crucial for maintaining sheepskin suppleness. UGG's conditioner restores natural oils that keep the material flexible and comfortable. Apply conditioner evenly using a clean cloth, allowing complete absorption before the next wear. This process differs significantly from maintaining synthetic alternatives like OOFOS flip flops, which simply require soap and water.

Seasonal Storage Tips

During off-season storage, stuff UGG flip flops with tissue paper to maintain their shape and store them in breathable cloth bags rather than plastic containers. Cedar shoe trees work excellently for maintaining form while providing natural moisture absorption. Before storing, ensure they're completely clean and dry to prevent mold or mildew development during extended storage periods.

Professional restoration services exist for severely worn or damaged UGG products, though proper preventive care typically eliminates this necessity. Regular conditioning every 3-4 months maintains optimal appearance and performance, making UGG flip flops a worthwhile investment compared to frequently replacing cheaper alternatives.
