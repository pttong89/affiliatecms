Sizing and Fit Considerations

Hey Dude shoes typically run slightly large due to their elastic construction and roomy toe box design. Most users recommend ordering a half-size down from your normal athletic shoe size, especially if you have narrow feet. The elastic laces accommodate some size variation, but proper initial fit is crucial for optimal performance during hiking activities.

For hiking applications, ensure you have adequate toe room to prevent crowding during downhill descents, but avoid excessive heel slip that can cause blisters. The elastic construction means the shoes will stretch and conform to your feet over time, so a snug initial fit often works better than loose-fitting shoes.
