Break-in and Maintenance Tips

Proper Break-in Process:

  1. Wear for 2-3 hours daily during first week
  2. Gradually increase wearing time each day
  3. Use thick socks initially to stretch material gently
  4. Walk on different surfaces to condition the outsole
  5. Allow 24-hour rest periods between extended wear

Maintenance Best Practices:

  • Machine wash monthly with gentle cycle
  • Air dry completely before wearing
  • Rotate between two pairs when possible
  • Replace insoles every 4-6 months
  • Monitor outsole wear patterns for alignment issues
