Sizing and Fit Considerations

Proper sizing is crucial for optimal performance and comfort. HOKA shoes generally run true to size, but individual models may vary slightly. I recommend these sizing strategies based on extensive testing experience:

  • Order your normal athletic shoe size as a starting point
  • Consider going up a half size for long-distance hiking to accommodate foot swelling
  • Try shoes on with the same socks you'll use for hiking
  • Test fit in the afternoon when feet are naturally larger
  • Ensure adequate thumb-width space between longest toe and shoe front
