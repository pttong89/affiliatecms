What's the best way to care for Mountain Equipment down insulation?

Proper care of Mountain Equipment down products ensures optimal performance for years. I wash my down jackets 2-3 times per year using specialized down detergent, machine washing on gentle cycles with extra rinse cycles to remove all soap residue. Drying requires patience – low heat settings with tennis balls or down-specific dryer balls to restore loft. Storage should be uncompressed in breathable garment bags rather than stuff sacks. Most importantly, address moisture exposure immediately by air-drying before compression storage. Following these practices, my five-year-old Lightline jacket maintains original loft and warmth despite extensive use.
