Complete Product Line Analysis

Drive Series: Lightweight Performance

The Drive Series represents Element outdoor clothing's entry-level offering, but "entry-level" doesn't mean basic performance. These lightweight garments feature moisture-wicking polyester construction with DWR (Durable Water Repellent) coating and integrated scent control technology. The Drive Series quarter-zip shirts and lightweight pants are perfect for early season hunting when temperatures are moderate and mobility is crucial. During my testing, the Drive Series pants impressed with their quiet fabric and comfortable fit. The lightweight construction makes them ideal for active hunting styles, while the water-resistant treatment provides adequate protection against morning dew and light precipitation. For hunters seeking affordable alternatives to premium brands, the Drive Series offers exceptional value.

Prime Series: Mid-Weight Versatility

The Prime Series strikes the perfect balance between warmth and breathability, making it Element outdoor clothing's most versatile line. The Prime Series jacket features bonded fabric construction with EO Grid lining for superior moisture management and insulation. Multiple pocket configurations, including chest pockets and hand warmers, provide practical storage without bulk. What sets the Prime Series apart is its three-layer construction that provides windproof and water-resistant protection while maintaining breathability. The jacket's waist cinch and wrist cinches help seal out wind and retain body heat without restricting movement. YKK zippers throughout ensure reliability in harsh conditions, while the quiet shell fabric won't spook game during critical moments.

Infinity Series: Heavy-Duty Protection

For extreme weather conditions, Element outdoor clothing's Infinity Series delivers maximum protection. The heavy waterproof bibs and jackets in this line feature fully seam-sealed construction with reinforced knees and seat areas. The Infinity Series is designed for hunters who face the harshest conditions, from late-season whitetail hunting to waterfowl hunting in driving rain. The Infinity Series bibs include adjustable suspenders, zippered leg openings for easy on/off over boots, and reinforced scuff guards. The jacket features a full-zip design with storm flap, adjustable hood, and extended tail for complete coverage. While heavier than other series, the Infinity line provides uncompromising protection when weather conditions deteriorate.

Flexlite Series: Insect Shield Technology

Element outdoor clothing's newest innovation, the Flexlite Series, incorporates Insect Shield technology that converts clothing into effective insect repellent. This EPA-registered treatment repels mosquitoes, ticks, flies, chiggers, and other biting insects without chemicals on your skin. For hunters pursuing spring turkey or fishing in bug-heavy environments, the Flexlite Series offers game-changing protection. The Flexlite garments maintain the lightweight, breathable characteristics hunters need for warm-weather activities while providing built-in insect protection that lasts through 70+ washes. This technology eliminates the need for topical repellents, reducing scent contamination concerns while providing more consistent protection throughout long days outdoors.
