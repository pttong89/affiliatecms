Maintenance and Care Protocols

Proper care extends the life and performance of technical ski pants significantly. I wash my Mammut pants after every 5-7 ski days using technical gear detergent and avoiding fabric softeners that can clog the membrane pores. The key is using cool water and ensuring thorough rinsing to remove all soap residue.

DWR (Durable Water Repellent) treatment renewal is crucial for maintaining water-shedding performance. I retreat my pants every 20-25 ski days using spray-on DWR treatment, following this with heat activation using a hair dryer on low setting. This simple maintenance step has kept my three-year-old pants performing like new.

Storage between seasons requires attention to detail. I ensure the pants are completely clean and dry before storage, then hang them in a cool, dry location. Avoiding compression storage prevents membrane damage and extends the life of the DRYtechnology Pro system. These care practices have kept my original pair of Mammut womens ski pants performing at peak level through multiple seasons.
