Professional Fitting Tips

  • Measure feet in the afternoon when they're naturally slightly swollen
  • Allow thumb-width space between longest toe and boot front
  • Consider the thickness of winter socks you'll typically wear
  • Test fit with both feet as sizes can vary slightly
  • Walk around to ensure no pressure points or heel slippage
