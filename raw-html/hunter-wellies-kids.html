Professional Fitting Process I Recommend

After making sizing mistakes with my first purchase, I developed a foolproof fitting process that I now recommend to all parents. First, measure your child's feet in the afternoon when natural swelling reaches its peak. Children's feet can expand up to a half-size throughout the day, making afternoon measurements most accurate for boot fitting.

Hunter wellies kids boots should provide approximately half-inch of growing room at the toe, which I measure by having my child stand while I press gently above their longest toe. This growing room accommodates both foot growth and thick socks during cold weather adventures. However, avoid excessive room as this creates blisters and instability on uneven terrain.

The calf width deserves special attention, particularly for children with fuller legs. Hunter wellies kids models feature different calf measurements across their range, with First models offering the most generous fit for toddlers still wearing diapers or snow pants underneath winter clothing.

Care, Maintenance and Seasonal Usage

Proper care and maintenance significantly extends the lifespan of Hunter wellies kids boots while preserving their appearance and performance characteristics. Through three years of intensive use, I've developed maintenance routines that keep our boots looking nearly new despite extensive outdoor adventures.

Daily Care and Cleaning Routine

Immediate post-adventure cleaning prevents permanent staining and maintains rubber flexibility. I rinse boots with cool water immediately after use, paying special attention to removing dirt and debris from sole treads. Warm water can damage rubber composition over time, so I consistently use cool to lukewarm temperatures.

For stubborn mud or stains, gentle scrubbing with soft brushes and mild soap effectively removes buildup without damaging surface texture. I avoid harsh chemicals, petroleum-based products, or abrasive cleaners that can degrade natural rubber composition and compromise waterproof integrity.

Storage and Seasonal Considerations

Proper storage prevents cracking and extends boot longevity significantly. I store our Hunter wellies kids boots in cool, dry locations away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Excessive heat causes rubber to deteriorate rapidly, while prolonged sunlight exposure leads to fading and surface cracking.

During summer storage periods, I stuff boots with newspaper or boot forms to maintain shape and prevent creasing. Cedar shoe inserts help control odor while maintaining interior freshness for the next wearing season.
