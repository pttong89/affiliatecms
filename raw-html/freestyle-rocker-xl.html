Frequently Asked Questions

What is the weight capacity of the GCI Freestyle Rocker XL?

The GCI freestyle rocker xl has a 400-pound weight capacity, making it suitable for larger individuals and providing exceptional durability. The heavy-duty powder-coated steel frame ensures long-lasting reliability even under maximum load conditions. This represents a 60% increase over the standard freestyle rocker, indicating significantly more robust construction that benefits users of all sizes through improved stability and longevity.

How does the Spring-Action Rocking Technology work?

The patented Spring-Action Rocking Technology uses built-in shock absorbers that create a smooth, balanced rocking motion on any flat surface. This innovative system allows you to rock gently without requiring a traditional rocking base, making it perfect for camping on various terrains. The shocks compress and extend in a controlled manner, providing therapeutic motion that can help reduce muscle tension and improve circulation during extended sitting periods.

What are the dimensions when folded and opened?

When opened, the freestyle rocker xl measures 25.6 x 26.4 x 34.8 inches with a seat height of 20 inches. When folded, it compacts to 25 x 4.9 x 34.8 inches, making it relatively portable for car camping though it's bulkier than standard camping chairs due to the rocking mechanism. The chair weighs 13.4 pounds, which is manageable for short carries but may be heavy for extended backpacking trips. Consider the pod rocker for lighter alternatives.

Is the chair comfortable for extended use?

Yes, the chair features padded armrests, a breathable mesh backrest, and a wider seat design that promotes comfort during extended use. The ergonomic design helps maintain proper posture, while the gentle rocking motion adds to the relaxation experience. The mesh panel prevents overheating during warm weather, and the 25.6-inch width accommodates various body types comfortably. Many users report sitting comfortably for hours during camping trips, sporting events, and outdoor gatherings.

What maintenance does the chair require?

Regular cleaning and occasional lubrication of the shock mechanisms will keep your chair in optimal condition. Some users report squeaking after extended use, which can be resolved with proper lubrication using a light machine oil or silicone spray. The powder-coated frame resists rust and corrosion with minimal care, though storing the chair in a dry location when not in use will extend its lifespan. Clean fabric components with mild soap and water as needed.
