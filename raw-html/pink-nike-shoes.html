How do I clean and maintain pink Nike shoes to prevent discoloration?

Maintaining pink Nike shoes requires gentle but consistent care. For leather models like Air Force 1, use a soft brush and mild soap solution, followed by leather conditioner every few months. Mesh and fabric shoes benefit from gentle machine washing in cold water with color-safe detergent, air-dried away from direct sunlight. Avoid bleach or harsh chemicals that can fade pink tones. Pre-treating stains immediately prevents permanent discoloration. I recommend waterproofing sprays for protection against stains and moisture. Regular cleaning every 2-3 weeks maintains appearance and prevents deep-set stains that are harder to remove.
