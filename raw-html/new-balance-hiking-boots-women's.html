Sizing and Fit Guidelines

Proper fit is crucial for hiking boot performance and comfort. New Balance's extensive width options make them particularly suitable for women with hard-to-fit feet. Based on fitting hundreds of hikers over the years, here's my recommended approach:

  1. Measure feet in the afternoon when they're naturally swollen from daily activity
  2. Try on with hiking socks – the same thickness you'll wear on trails
  3. Check for thumb-width space between longest toe and boot front
  4. Ensure heel stays planted when walking – no slipping or lifting
  5. Consider width options – don't just go longer if boots feel tight on the sides
