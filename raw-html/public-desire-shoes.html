Sizing Guide & Fit Tips from Real Experience

One of the most challenging aspects of ordering public desire shoes online is getting the sizing right. After years of trial and error, I've developed a system that works 90% of the time. Here's everything I wish I knew before my first order.

Understanding Public Desire Sizing

Public Desire uses UK sizing as their standard, which can be confusing for US customers. Their size conversion isn't always consistent with other brands, so relying solely on their size chart can lead to disappointment. I've found that public desire shoes typically run about half a size smaller than brands like Nike or Adidas, but close to true-to-size compared to other fashion shoe brands.

The fit also varies significantly between shoe styles. Pointed-toe heels tend to run smaller and narrower, while their platform boots often have more generous sizing. Their boot collection generally offers more forgiveness in fit compared to their precision-cut heels.

Sizing Reality Check

Don't assume your usual size will work across all Public Desire styles. I wear a US 8 in most brands, but my Public Desire collection ranges from UK 6 to UK 7 depending on the specific shoe design and intended fit.

My Proven Sizing Strategy

For Heels & Sandals

  • Order your true UK size for strappy styles
  • Size up 0.5 for pointed toes
  • Consider foot width – go up if you have wider feet
  • Check recent reviews for style-specific feedback

For Boots & Closed Shoes

  • Go with your normal UK size for ankle boots
  • Consider calf width for knee-high styles
  • Allow room for thicker socks in winter boots
  • Wide-fit options are genuinely wider, not just longer

For those new to public desire shoes, I recommend starting with their wide-fit options if you're between sizes. The additional width often provides the comfort needed for longer wear, and you can always add heel grips or toe pads for minor adjustments. Their wide-fit shoe selection has expanded significantly over the past year.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Public Desire shoes true to size?

Public Desire sizing tends to run about half a size smaller than typical US brands. Based on my extensive testing, I recommend ordering half a size up for pointed-toe styles and sticking to your usual UK size for boots and block heels. Their wide-fit options are genuinely wider, not just longer, making them excellent for those with broader feet. Always check recent customer reviews for specific styles, as sizing can vary between different shoe designs within their collection.

How is the quality compared to other affordable shoe brands?

Public Desire offers solid quality for the price point, particularly excelling in aesthetic details and construction finishing. While they primarily use synthetic materials, the stitching is generally reliable and hardware feels substantial. Compared to similar fast-fashion shoe brands, they invest more in design details that matter for photos and special occasions. However, don't expect the same durability as leather shoes from established footwear brands. For occasional wear and trendy styles, the quality justifies the price.

Can you wear Public Desire shoes for extended periods?

Comfort varies significantly by style within the Public Desire range. Their block heels and platform styles can handle 6-8 hours of wear with minimal discomfort, while stilettos are best reserved for special occasions lasting 2-4 hours. The boots generally offer the most comfort for extended wear. I recommend breaking in new shoes gradually and consider adding cushioned insoles for all-day events. Their wide-fit options provide additional comfort for longer wear periods.

What's the return policy and international shipping like?

Public Desire offers a 30-day return window, though customers are responsible for return shipping costs from the US, which can be expensive (around $25-30). They do process refunds relatively quickly once items are received. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days and costs $10-15 depending on order value. During sales periods, shipping may take longer. I recommend ordering multiple sizes for important events and returning what doesn't fit, factoring in the return shipping cost.

Are there any celebrity endorsements or collaborations?

Public Desire has been spotted on numerous celebrities and influencers, including Bella Hadid, Katy Perry, and various Love Island contestants. They regularly collaborate with social media influencers for limited collections and styling partnerships. While they don't have major celebrity endorsement deals like luxury brands, their organic celebrity wear has significantly boosted their credibility and social media presence. Many of their viral styles originate from celebrity sightings or influencer collaborations.

How do Public Desire shoes hold up over time?

Longevity depends heavily on usage frequency and shoe style. With occasional wear (1-2 times monthly), most Public Desire shoes maintain good condition for 1-2 years. Daily wear will show signs after 6-9 months, particularly in high-stress areas like heel tips and toe boxes. Their boots generally outlast sandals and heels due to sturdier construction. For the price point, durability is reasonable, but consider them fashion pieces rather than investment footwear. Regular maintenance like heel tip replacement can extend their lifespan significantly.
