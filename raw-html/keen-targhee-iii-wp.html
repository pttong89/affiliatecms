What sets the Targhee III apart is its balance of traditional hiking boot durability with contemporary comfort features.

At its core, the KEEN Targhee III WP features a waterproof leather and performance mesh upper that's built to withstand years of trail abuse. The KEEN Targhee III WP runs true to size according to 165 verified customer votes, but fit considerations extend beyond simple length measurements.
