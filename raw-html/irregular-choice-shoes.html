The Brand Story & Dan Sullivan's Vision

The story of irregular choice shoes begins in 1999 when Dan Sullivan, a visionary from Brighton, England, decided to revolutionize the footwear industry. Growing up on London's King's Road during the punk era of the 1970s, Sullivan witnessed firsthand how fashion could be a powerful form of self-expression. His parents, who developed their own unique footwear collections including Transport and Chaos Creators, inspired his unconventional approach to design.

By the late 1990s, Sullivan observed that the British footwear market had become increasingly "neutral and black," lacking the creativity and individuality that consumers craved. At just 18, he had already opened his first shoe shop in Covent Garden, which expanded to six stores before he decided to close them at 23 to travel the world and deepen his understanding of global fashion trends. This journey through Asia, particularly China, exposed him to manufacturing techniques that would later enable his intricate, affordable designs.

The cultural influences from his travels became the foundation of Irregular Choice's first collection. The iconic split-toe shoe design, inspired by traditional Japanese footwear, remains a signature element in today's collections. Sullivan's fearless philosophy has remained unchanged throughout the brand's 25-year journey: create original, trend-resistant designs that help wearers "stand out from the crowd." This commitment to authenticity means that alternative footwear enthusiasts and collectors worldwide recognize Irregular Choice as genuinely unique.

Today, Irregular Choice creates over 600 different styles annually, with Sullivan's ever-expanding creative process resulting in more than 10,000 design variations. The brand has evolved from a small Brighton-based company to a global phenomenon, sold through hundreds of stockists worldwide. What started as one man's rebellion against boring footwear has become a beloved brand that transforms shoes into wearable art, proving that fashion can be both functional and fantastical.

Comfort, Fit & Sizing Guide

Important Comfort Considerations

Irregular Choice prioritizes artistic design over comfort. These shoes are best suited for special occasions, photo opportunities, and shorter wearing periods rather than all-day comfort.

Understanding the fit and comfort profile of irregular choice shoes is crucial for making informed purchasing decisions. The brand's sizing generally runs true to standard UK/EU measurements, but individual styles can vary significantly due to their unique construction methods. Character heels, platform designs, and heavily embellished styles may fit differently than simpler designs within the same size range.

Community feedback consistently highlights sizing inconsistencies between different styles. While some customers find their usual size perfect, others report needing to size up or down depending on the specific design. The brand now offers wide-fit options for select styles, addressing concerns from customers with broader feet. For international buyers, conversion charts help navigate the UK-based sizing, though trying on similar styles in person when possible remains the best approach for determining proper fit.

Comfort levels vary dramatically between style categories. Flat designs and low-heeled options (under 2 inches) provide the best comfort for extended wear, often featuring cushioned insoles and stable bases. Mid-heel styles (2-3 inches) offer reasonable comfort for moderate wearing periods but may cause pressure on the ball of the foot. Character heels and high platforms (4+ inches) prioritize visual impact over comfort and are best reserved for special occasions, photo shoots, or events where seating is readily available.

Professional tips for maximizing comfort include applying sole protectors immediately upon purchase, using gel insoles for additional cushioning, and gradually breaking in new pairs with short wearing sessions. Many enthusiasts recommend keeping backup flats for long events and choosing styles based on the occasion's requirements. Remember that performance footwear serves different purposes than artistic statement pieces – embrace Irregular Choice for what they do best: making unforgettable fashion statements.

Irregular choice shoes prioritize artistic design over comfort. The brand focuses on creating wearable art rather than practical everyday footwear. For maximum comfort, consider flat styles, apply gel insoles, and limit wearing time for high-heeled designs. Many enthusiasts recommend having backup flats available for extended events.

Irregular choice shoes sizing can be inconsistent between styles. Generally, they fit true to size, but some customers report variations.
