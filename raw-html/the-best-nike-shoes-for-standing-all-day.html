Should I buy Nike shoes in a larger size for standing all day?

Yes, consider going up half a size for extended standing. Your feet naturally swell throughout the day, especially during long periods of standing. Nike shoes typically run true to size, but the extra room prevents tightness and pressure points that develop as feet expand. This is particularly important for models like the Vomero 18 or Structure 25 that you'll wear for 8+ hours daily. However, avoid going too large as this can cause instability and blisters. The best approach is to try on shoes in the evening when your feet are naturally more swollen.
