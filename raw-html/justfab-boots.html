Frequently Asked Questions

Are JustFab boots true to size?

JustFab boots generally run true to size for most styles, but ankle boots and booties tend to run slightly small. I recommend ordering a half-size up for ankle boot styles, especially if you have wider feet or prefer to wear thicker socks. Knee-high and over-the-knee styles typically fit true to size. The brand provides detailed size charts and customer reviews that include sizing feedback, which are invaluable for making the right choice. If you're unsure, take advantage of their generous return policy to try multiple sizes.

How do I cancel my JustFab VIP membership?

Canceling your JustFab VIP membership is straightforward but requires attention to timing. You can cancel online through your account dashboard, by calling customer service, or through live chat. The key is to cancel before the 5th of the month to avoid being charged for the current month. You can also "skip" a month if you don't want to purchase anything but want to maintain your membership benefits. I recommend setting a monthly calendar reminder to either shop, skip, or cancel to avoid unexpected charges. Customer service is generally helpful and understanding if you're charged accidentally.

What is the quality like compared to more expensive boots?

JustFab boots offer impressive quality for their price point, though they don't match luxury brands in materials or longevity. The faux leather is well-made and looks authentic, stitching is generally reliable, and comfort features like cushioned insoles are standard. With proper care, expect 12-18 months of regular wear from most styles. While they won't last as long as $200+ boots, they're significantly better quality than many fast-fashion alternatives. The construction is solid enough for daily wear, making them excellent value for fashion-conscious consumers who like to update their style regularly.

Can JustFab boots be resoled or repaired?

Most JustFab boots are not designed for resoling due to their construction method and materials. The soles are typically attached using adhesive and molding processes rather than traditional stitching methods that allow for resoling. However, basic repairs like heel replacement or minor stitching fixes are possible at most shoe repair shops. For the price point, JustFab boots are designed to be replaced rather than extensively repaired. To extend their life, focus on preventive care like regular cleaning, conditioning, and proper storage with shoe trees or stuffing to maintain shape.

What styles work best for professional settings?

For professional environments, JustFab's heeled ankle boots and knee-high boots in neutral colors work excellently. Look for styles with clean lines, minimal hardware, and heel heights between 1.5-3 inches for comfort during long workdays. Black, brown, and navy options are most versatile for business settings. Avoid heavily embellished styles, extreme heel heights, or overly casual designs like combat boots. Their Chelsea boot styles are particularly workplace-appropriate and can transition from office to after-work events seamlessly. Always consider your specific workplace culture and dress code requirements when selecting professional footwear.
