Frequently Asked Questions

Are Skechers walking boots good for long distance walking?

Yes, Skechers walking boots are excellent for long distance walking, especially models with Arch Fit technology and Memory Foam insoles. The Ultra Go cushioning system provides responsive support that reduces fatigue during extended walks. However, for serious hiking over 10 miles, consider their dedicated hiking boot models like the Trego series which offer enhanced ankle support and more aggressive tread patterns for challenging terrain. In my experience, they excel for distances up to 8-10 miles on established trails, after which you might notice some limitations in support compared to dedicated backpacking boots.

Do Skechers walking boots run true to size?

Skechers walking boots generally run true to size, but there can be variations between models. The Go Walk series tends to fit slightly loose for comfort, while Arch Fit models may feel snug initially as they're designed to mold to your foot shape. I recommend ordering your normal shoe size and allowing 24-48 hours for the Memory Foam to adjust. If you have wide feet, consider ordering a half size up or look for their wide-width options. Always check the specific model's reviews for sizing feedback, as manufacturing variations can occur.

What's the difference between Skechers Go Walk and Arch Fit technologies?

Go Walk technology focuses on lightweight comfort and flexibility, featuring responsive Ultra Go cushioning and breathable designs perfect for casual walking. Arch Fit technology, however, provides podiatrist-certified arch support with a patented insole design that offers superior stability and alignment. Go Walk is ideal for everyday comfort walking, while Arch Fit is better for those needing orthotic-level support or dealing with foot conditions like plantar fasciitis. Many newer models combine both technologies for comprehensive comfort and support.

Are Skechers walking boots waterproof?

Many Skechers walking boots offer water-resistant or waterproof protection, but it varies by model. Look for boots with 3M Scotchgard treatment for basic water resistance, or models specifically labeled as 'waterproof' for full protection. The Trego Alpine Trail and certain Go Walk boot models feature waterproof membranes. However, for heavy rain or stream crossing, dedicated waterproof hiking boots might be more suitable than general walking boots. Always check the product specifications for specific waterproof ratings and care instructions.

How long do Skechers walking boots typically last?

With regular use, quality Skechers walking boots typically last 300-500 miles of walking, or about 6-12 months depending on usage frequency and terrain. The Memory Foam insoles maintain their cushioning for approximately 6 months of daily wear. Signs it's time to replace them include worn tread patterns, compressed foam that doesn't bounce back, or loss of arch support. Proper care, rotation with other shoes, and avoiding extreme conditions can extend their lifespan significantly. For occasional use, they can last 2-3 years while maintaining their comfort properties.
