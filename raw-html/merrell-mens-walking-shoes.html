Proper sizing proves critical for merrell mens walking shoes performance and longevity. Merrell's generous width options deserve special consideration. Their standard D width fits most users comfortably, while EE options accommodate significantly wider feet without compromising support. The toe box design allows natural toe movement, reducing hot spot formation during long walks. However, users with narrow feet may find the fit too loose, requiring alternative brands or models.
