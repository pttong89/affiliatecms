Sizing, Fit, and Comfort Guide

Proper fit is critical for maximizing the performance of your KEEN Explore Waterproof shoes. Based on extensive testing with multiple foot types and input from outdoor specialty retailers, I've developed comprehensive sizing recommendations to ensure optimal comfort and performance.

The KEEN Explore Waterproof runs true to size for most foot shapes, but there are important considerations for optimal fit. Unlike the generous toe box of KEEN Explore Mid boots, these low-cut shoes have a more athletic fit that accommodates average to narrow feet exceptionally well. Wide-footed hikers should consider sizing up a half size or exploring KEEN's wide-width options.

The heel cup dimensions are critical for proper performance. I recommend trying on shoes in the afternoon when feet are naturally swollen to simulate hiking conditions. The heel should feel snug without pressure points, and there should be approximately thumb-width space between your longest toe and the shoe front when standing on an incline.

Do KEEN Explore Waterproof shoes run true to size?

Yes, the KEEN Explore Waterproof generally runs true to size for most foot shapes.
