How should I care for my cloud shoes for women?

Proper care extends the lifespan and performance of cloud shoes for women significantly. Regular cleaning involves removing debris from the CloudTec pods using a soft brush or toothpick, as small stones and dirt can accumulate in the hollow areas. The upper materials generally clean well with mild soap and water, but avoid harsh chemicals that might damage the engineered mesh or recycled materials. Allow shoes to air dry naturally away from direct heat sources, which can degrade the Helion foam and pod materials. Rotate between multiple pairs when possible to allow complete drying and foam recovery between uses. The speed-lacing system requires occasional adjustment as the elastic materials may stretch over time. Store shoes in cool, dry locations away from extreme temperatures to maintain material integrity and CloudTec responsiveness.
