What is YOOX and Why Buy Shoes There

Are YOOX shoes authentic?

Why are YOOX shoes so cheap compared to retail?

YOOX shoes are discounted because they primarily sell end-of-season inventory, overstock, and previous collections from luxury brands. This clearance model allows them to offer genuine designer shoes at 30-80% off retail prices. The company works directly with fashion houses to clear their excess inventory, creating a win-win situation. Additionally, YOOX operates with lower overhead costs than traditional luxury retail stores, passing savings to customers. The sparse product descriptions and limited photos also reduce operational costs, contributing to lower prices while maintaining product authenticity.

How does YOOX sizing compare to regular shoe sizes?

YOOX sizing can be tricky because different designers have varying size standards. Italian brands like Marni and Bottega Veneta often run small, while some contemporary designers run true to size. I always check the product measurements provided rather than relying solely on size labels. European sizes (IT/EU) are most common on YOOX and typically run smaller than US sizes. For example, a US size 8 usually corresponds to IT 38-39. Many users report sizing inconsistencies even within the same brand, so I recommend studying reviews and measurements carefully before purchasing.

What is YOOX return policy for shoes?

YOOX offers returns within 14 days of delivery for a full refund, minus a $6.95 return shipping fee. Items must be unworn and in original packaging with all tags attached. The return process involves printing a prepaid shipping label and sending items back via UPS. Processing typically takes 5-7 business days after YOOX receives the return. Some customers report keeping sales tax as a 'return fee,' though this varies by location. PayPal purchases can sometimes get return shipping reimbursed through PayPal's buyer protection, though this program has limitations.
