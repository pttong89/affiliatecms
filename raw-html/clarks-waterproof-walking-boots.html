Sizing and Fit Considerations

Proper sizing is crucial for outdoor footwear performance and comfort. Clarks waterproof walking boots generally run true to size, but I recommend trying them on with the socks you plan to wear during activities. Your feet naturally swell during extended physical activity, so consider trying boots on later in the day when your feet are at their largest. Allow for approximately a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the boot's front to prevent pressure during descents.

Sizing Quick Reference:

  • Width Options: Most models available in standard and wide fits
  • Size Range: Men's 7-13, Women's 5-11 (varies by model)
  • Half Sizes: Available for most popular models
  • Fit Characteristics: True to size with adequate toe room

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Clarks waterproof walking boots really waterproof?

Yes, clarks waterproof walking boots feature advanced waterproof technologies including GORE-TEX membranes that provide 100% waterproof protection. The GORE-TEX lining creates a barrier that prevents water from entering while allowing moisture to escape, keeping your feet dry and comfortable. However, proper care and maintenance are essential to maintain their waterproof capabilities over time. Regular cleaning and avoiding harsh chemicals will preserve the membrane's effectiveness for years of reliable use.

What's the difference between Clarks ATL Trek and Morris Peak boots?

The ATL Trek series is designed for more rugged outdoor activities with enhanced grip and durability, featuring a higher ankle support and more aggressive tread pattern perfect for trail hiking. The Morris Peak series offers a more refined look suitable for both casual wear and light hiking, with a sleek chukka boot design that works well in urban environments. Both feature waterproof technology but the ATL Trek provides superior protection for challenging terrain, while Morris Peak excels in versatility and style for everyday wear.

How do I maintain my Clarks waterproof boots?

To maintain your clarks waterproof walking boots, clean them with lukewarm water and a soft brush to remove dirt and debris after each significant use. Allow them to air dry naturally away from direct heat sources, which can damage the waterproof membrane. Avoid using wax, grease, or silicon-based products as these can interfere with the GORE-TEX technology. Store them in a well-ventilated area and consider applying waterproofing treatments annually to maintain optimal performance and extend the life of your investment.

Are Clarks waterproof walking boots suitable for serious hiking?

Clarks waterproof walking boots are excellent for light to moderate hiking, day walks, and everyday outdoor activities. They perform admirably on well-maintained trails and provide reliable waterproof protection for most recreational hiking scenarios. However, for serious mountaineering, technical rock climbing, or extended backpacking on extremely rugged terrain, you may want to consider boots with more rigid construction and specialized hiking features. The ATL Trek series offers the best performance for more challenging terrain among Clarks' offerings.

What size should I choose for Clarks waterproof walking boots?

Clarks waterproof walking boots generally run true to size, but it's recommended to try them on with the hiking socks you plan to wear during outdoor activities. Allow for slight room (about thumb-width) at the toe for comfort during long walks and to prevent pressure during descents. Consider that feet tend to swell during extended activity, so trying them on later in the day can help ensure a proper fit. Most models are available in both standard and wide widths to accommodate different foot shapes and preferences.
