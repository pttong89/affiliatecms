Sizing Guide and Fit Analysis

Sizing the ugg platform ultra mini correctly is crucial for both comfort and the intended aesthetic. After trying on multiple sizes and speaking with numerous customers during my research trips to various retailers, I've compiled the most comprehensive sizing guide available.

The overwhelming consensus from users and my personal testing is that these boots run approximately one full size large. I typically wear a size 8 in most footwear, but found that a size 7 in the ultra mini ugg platform provided the perfect snug fit that UGG recommends.

Critical Sizing Insights

One of the most common issues I've observed is heel lifting while walking. This occurs when the boots are too large and can make the platform feel unstable. During my field testing in various outdoor settings, I noticed that properly sized boots eliminate this problem entirely.

For narrow feet: Size down 1 full size

For regular width feet: Size down 0.5 to 1 size

For wide feet: Consider sizing down 0.5 size or staying true to size

Most users recommend sizing down half to one full size from your regular shoe size. The boots tend to run large, and the sheepskin lining will compress and mold to your feet over time. I personally sized down from 8 to 7 and found it perfect. Users with narrow feet should definitely size down one full size, while those with wider feet might only need to go down half a size. The snug initial fit is intentional and will become more comfortable after a few days of wear as the materials adjust to your foot shape.

Are UGG Platform Ultra Mini boots worth the investment?

For many users, yes. They offer excellent warmth, comfort, and style versatility that justifies the $160-170 price point. Yes, but with limitations. The pretreated suede upper repels water and light snow effectively, and the platform height provides protection from ground moisture.
