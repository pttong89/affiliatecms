How do Salomon running shoes fit compared to other brands?

What makes Salomon Contagrip outsoles special?

Salomon's Contagrip outsoles use specially formulated rubber compounds optimized for different terrains. Advanced models like the Speedcross 6 measure 93.9 HC durometer hardness, providing exceptional durability while maintaining grip. The multi-directional lug patterns enhance traction during climbing, descending, and lateral movements across varied terrain conditions.

How long do Salomon running shoes typically last?
