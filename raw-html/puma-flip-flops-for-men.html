What's the difference between Puma slides and flip flops?

Puma slides feature a wide strap across the top of the foot, while flip flops have a Y-shaped strap that goes between the toes. Slides are easier to slip on and off, making them ideal for poolside use, locker rooms, and quick trips. They also accommodate socks better for cooler weather camping. Flip flops provide a more secure fit during walking and activities, with the toe post preventing the foot from sliding forward. Based on my experience, slides work better for stationary activities and relaxation, while flip flops excel for walking and light recreation. Both styles offer excellent ventilation and quick-drying capabilities. The choice often comes down to personal preference and intended use – I keep both styles for different situations during outdoor adventures.
