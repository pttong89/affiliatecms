Step 2: Fit and Sizing

OutdoorGearLab's Fit Guidelines

Torso Length Measurement
  1. Find C7 vertebra (base of neck)
  2. Locate iliac crest (top of hip bones)
  3. Measure distance between points
  4. Match to manufacturer sizing chart
Hip Belt Sizing
  • Measure at iliac crest level
  • Add 2-4 inches for clothing layers
  • Ensure belt sits on hips, not waist
  • Test with typical load weight
