Best Daily Training Shoes of 2022

After 400+ miles in the ASICS Novablast 3, I can confidently say it redefined my expectations for daily trainers. The FlyteFoam Blast+ midsole delivers an almost trampoline-like bounce that makes easy runs feel effortless, yet it's stable enough for tempo efforts.

What impressed me most was its versatility. During my Chicago Marathon training, I used these for everything from recovery runs to 20-mile long runs. The shoe weighs just 8.8 ounces but feels substantial underfoot, and the engineered mesh upper provides excellent breathability during hot summer months.

Top Race Day Shoes for 2022

Saucony Endorphin Pro 3 - Race Day Champion

Carbon fiber supershoes revolutionized racing in 2022

The Saucony Endorphin Pro 3 earned its place as my go-to racing shoe after setting a 10-minute marathon PR wearing them at the Boston Marathon. What separates this shoe from the competition is its perfect balance of cushioning and responsiveness—40mm of PWRRUN PB foam that feels protective yet springy.

Unlike some carbon-plated shoes that feel harsh or unstable, the Pro 3's SpeedRoll geometry creates a natural rocking motion that actually helps maintain form during the late miles of a marathon. The breathable upper proved crucial during my summer races, keeping my feet comfortable even in 85°F temperatures.

The $225 price point positions it $25-50 below most competitors while delivering equal or superior performance. For runners serious about race day performance, the investment pays dividends in both comfort and speed.

Speed Work Specialist: Nike Streakfly

For tempo runs and 5K to 10K races, the Nike Streakfly became my secret weapon. This shoe embodies everything great about ZoomX foam in a lower-profile package designed for speed. At just 6.2 ounces, it feels like an extension of your foot rather than a shoe.

What impressed me most during track workouts was its grip and stability through turns. The Continental rubber outsole provides exceptional traction on both wet and dry surfaces, while the ZoomX midsole delivers the perfect amount of energy return for maintaining fast paces without fatigue.

The Streakfly bridges the gap between racing flats and super shoes, making it ideal for runners who want speed without the full carbon plate experience. When comparing men's running shoes for 2022, it stands out for its pure focus on tempo and speed work.

Most Innovative Running Shoes of 2022

New Balance SuperComp Trainer - Revolutionary Daily Trainer

When I first saw the New Balance SC Trainer's 47mm stack height, I was skeptical. How could a shoe that looked like a marshmallow on steroids actually be functional? After 300 miles of testing, including a 125-mile training week, I understand why it earned "Most Innovative" honors from multiple publications.

The genius lies in its Energy Arc carbon fiber plate system. Rather than creating a rigid platform like traditional racing shoes, it provides just enough structure to prevent the massive FuelCell stack from feeling unstable. The result is a shoe that delivers maximum cushioning while maintaining energy return—something I never thought possible.

During my Cascade Range training camp, where daily runs often exceeded 15 miles on technical terrain, the SC Trainer became indispensable. My legs felt fresher after back-to-back long runs than they had in years, and the durability proved excellent despite the challenging conditions.

Innovation Impact: The SC Trainer proved that maximum cushioning and performance aren't mutually exclusive, paving the way for a new category of "super trainers" that combine race day foam with daily training durability.

Surprise Hit: On Cloudmonster

On's reputation among serious runners has been... complicated. Their shoes looked great but often felt gimmicky during actual runs. The Cloudmonster changed my perception entirely. This isn't just marketing—it's a legitimate performance trainer that happens to look incredible.

The softer Helion foam combined with their refined CloudTec system creates a unique sensation that's both bouncy and smooth. During my European racing tour, where I ran everything from London's cobblestones to Berlin's smooth asphalt, the Cloudmonster adapted beautifully to different surfaces.

What sealed the deal was its versatility. I've worn these for everything from easy recovery runs to uptempo efforts, and they never felt out of place. For runners exploring women's running shoes in 2022, the Cloudmonster represents a perfect blend of performance and style.

Best Stability and Support Shoes

Saucony Tempus - Stability Reimagined

As a mild overpronator who's struggled with traditional stability shoes, the Saucony Tempus was a revelation. Instead of dense foam or plastic posts that often feel intrusive, Saucony used their PWRRUN PB super foam (the same as their racing shoes) with a subtle guidance system.

The horseshoe-shaped TPU rim around the heel provides just enough guidance without feeling controlling. During my Boston Marathon buildup, where I logged 70+ mile weeks, the Tempus became my go-to shoe for long runs and recovery days. It offers the performance benefits of neutral shoes while providing the stability I need.

Perfect For:

  • • Those seeking premium foam in stability shoes

Key Features:
