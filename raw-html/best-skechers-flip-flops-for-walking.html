What's the typical lifespan of Skechers walking flip flops?

With regular walking use (3-5 miles, 4-5 times per week), Skechers flip flops for walking typically last 12-18 months before showing significant wear. The foam cushioning may compress slightly after 6-8 months of heavy use, but remains functional. Outsole wear depends heavily on walking surfaces – concrete and asphalt cause faster wear than dirt trails or indoor surfaces. Proper care, including regular cleaning and allowing them to air dry, extends lifespan significantly. Compared to budget flip flops that last 3-6 months, Skechers provides excellent durability value. Signs for replacement include visible compression of the footbed, worn tread patterns, or loss of arch support comfort.
