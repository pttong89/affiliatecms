Sizing Guide & Fit Tips

One of the most common questions I receive about jimmy choo shoes concerns sizing, and rightfully soâ€”getting the perfect fit is crucial for both comfort and longevity. After purchasing twelve pairs over the years and helping friends navigate their first purchases, I've developed a comprehensive understanding of how different Jimmy Choo styles fit across various foot types.

Generally, jimmy choo shoes run true to size, but there are important nuances to consider. The brand uses Italian sizing, which can be confusing for American buyers. I typically wear a US size 8, which translates to a 38 in Jimmy Choo sizing. However, certain styles like the narrow-toed Romy collection may require sizing up half a size if you have wider feet. According to Jimmy Choo's official sizing guide, their shoes are designed for a European medium width.

Style-Specific Sizing Recommendations

Different Jimmy Choo collections fit differently due to their unique construction and design elements. The Bing mule collection tends to run slightly large due to its open-back design, so I recommend going down half a size for a secure fit. The jimmy choo eshe boot and other boot styles typically run true to size but consider the thickness of socks or tights you'll be wearing. For sandal styles like the Saeda, I've found that the ankle strap provides adjustability, making true-to-size the safest choice.

Professional fitting advice suggests trying on jimmy choo shoes later in the day when your feet are naturally slightly swollen, mimicking how they'll feel during extended wear. Many Jimmy Choo boutiques offer professional fitting services, and their staff are incredibly knowledgeable about the nuances of each style. This personalized service is one of the reasons why shopping directly from Jimmy Choo, whether online or in-store, provides such a superior experience.

How should I care for my Jimmy Choo shoes?

Proper care is essential for maintaining the beauty and longevity of jimmy choo shoes. Always use cedar shoe trees immediately after wearing to maintain shape and absorb moisture.
