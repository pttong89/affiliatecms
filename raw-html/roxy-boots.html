Frequently Asked Questions

Are Roxy boots good quality?

Yes, roxy boots are generally well-made with quality materials and construction. The brand focuses on durability and comfort, especially in their winter and work boot collections. Most models feature water-resistant materials, comfortable insoles, and sturdy construction that can withstand daily wear. Based on my personal experience and extensive testing, they offer excellent value for the price point, though they're positioned as mid-range rather than luxury footwear. The quality-to-price ratio is particularly strong when compared to similar offerings from competing brands in the surf and lifestyle footwear space.

What sizes do Roxy boots come in?

Roxy boots typically come in US women's sizes 5-11, including half sizes for better fit customization. Some popular styles may extend to size 12, depending on availability and demand. The brand generally runs true to size, but it's recommended to check specific product reviews as some styles may run slightly large or small. During my experience with multiple pairs, I found that the athletic-inspired styles tend to run slightly larger, while the more fashion-forward ankle boots tend to fit true to size or slightly smaller.

How do you care for Roxy boots?

Proper care depends on the material composition of your specific roxy boots. For leather boots, use a damp cloth to clean surface dirt and apply leather conditioner every few months to maintain suppleness. Suede boots should be brushed with a suede brush and protected with waterproofing spray before first wear. For synthetic materials, mild soap and water cleaning usually suffices. Always air dry boots naturally away from direct heat sources and store them in a cool, dry place. I personally use cedar shoe trees to help maintain shape and absorb moisture, which has significantly extended the life of my collection.

Where can I buy authentic Roxy boots?

Authentic roxy boots are available through several trusted retailers including the official Roxy website, Zappos, Macy's, Amazon, and specialized footwear stores. Always verify seller authenticity when shopping online to ensure you're getting genuine products with proper warranty coverage. Be cautious of significantly discounted prices from unknown sellers, as these may indicate counterfeit products. Major retailers like those mentioned typically offer return policies and customer service support that provide additional peace of mind. For the best selection and latest styles, I recommend checking multiple retailers as inventory can vary significantly.
