Proper sizing is critical for maximizing the benefits of Gaerne Dakar boots. Gore-Tex vs. Aquatech Versions

Gaerne offers the Dakar in both Gore-Tex and Aquatech (DryTech) membrane versions, with significant performance differences. Having tested both extensively, I strongly recommend the Gore-Tex version for serious adventure touring. The Gore-Tex membrane provides superior breathability and more durable waterproofing, justifying the modest price premium for riders who encounter diverse weather conditions.

The Aquatech version, while more affordable, works well for riders in consistently dry climates or those primarily riding paved roads. However, for the versatility that defines adventure touring, the Gore-Tex version's all-weather capability makes it the clear choice. This aligns with the philosophy behind other premium models in the Gaerne Adventure lineup.

Can you resole Gaerne Dakar boots?

Yes, Gaerne Dakar boots feature traditional welt construction that allows skilled cobblers to replace the sole, extending the boot's lifespan significantly. The resoling process typically costs $80-120 and can be performed multiple times throughout the boot's life. I've seen riders get 10+ years from a single pair with proper care and resoling. This serviceability sets them apart from most modern motorcycle boots that must be completely replaced when the sole wears out.
