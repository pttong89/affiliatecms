Buying Guide and Sizing Tips

Sizing Recommendations

Based on extensive user feedback and personal testing, adidas mid gore tex walking shoes typically run about half a size small. I recommend ordering your normal hiking shoe size plus 0.5 for optimal comfort with medium-thickness hiking socks. If you prefer thick wool socks or have wider feet, consider going up a full size or choosing the wide version where available.

The toe box is notably tapered compared to brands like Merrell or Salomon. During my fitting sessions, I noticed this affects comfort significantly for those with wider forefoot spreads. Those exploring comprehensive footwear options should consider adidas terrex gore tex boots for additional ankle support and protection.

Professional Sizing Tip

Measure your feet late in the day when they're naturally swollen. This ensures proper fit during long hikes when feet expand. Leave about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the shoe front.
