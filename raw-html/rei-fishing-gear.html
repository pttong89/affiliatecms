Pairing your waders with the right boots is crucial for both safety and comfort. REI's selection of Simms wading boots offers excellent traction systems and durability. Safety First: Boot Sole Selection

Choose felt soles for maximum grip on wet rocks, but check local regulations first. Some waters prohibit felt to prevent invasive species transfer. Rubber soles with studs offer a good compromise and are accepted everywhere.
