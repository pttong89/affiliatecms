How to Measure Your Feet Correctly

  1. Stand on a flat surface with your heel against a wall
  2. Place a ruler beside your foot, touching the same wall as your heel
  3. Measure from your heel to the tip of your longest toe in millimeters
  4. Repeat for both feet and use the larger measurement
  5. If between sizes, always size up for Vagabond Marja boots

Pro Tip from the Trail: I always measure my feet in the evening when they're slightly swollen from daily activity - this ensures a comfortable fit during long hiking days when feet naturally expand.
