Comfort and Sizing Guide

Sizing Recommendations

One of the most frequent questions I receive about Aquatalia boots concerns sizing. Based on my experience and feedback from numerous customers, most people find that Aquatalia runs true to size, though those with wider feet may benefit from ordering a half-size up. The brand offers excellent support for wide feet, making them a popular choice among those who struggle with narrow boot designs.

Sizing Tips from Personal Experience:

  • Standard width feet: Order true to size
  • Wide feet: Consider half-size up for optimal comfort
  • Thick socks: Go up half-size if you plan to wear heavy socks
  • Narrow feet: May want to size down slightly or use insoles
