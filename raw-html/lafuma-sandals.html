Lafuma sandals generally run true to size, but the open design requires different considerations than traditional hiking boots. I recommend ordering your normal athletic shoe size for most foot types. However, if you have narrow feet or plan to wear them primarily without socks, consider sizing down a half size. The Z-strap system accommodates some size variation, but proper initial sizing prevents discomfort and strap wear.

Width considerations are particularly important with sandals. Lafuma sandals work well for normal to wide feet, but those with very narrow feet may find the strap system loose even when fully tightened. Conversely, those with extra-wide feet should ensure adequate toe clearance and comfortable strap positioning without excessive tension.
