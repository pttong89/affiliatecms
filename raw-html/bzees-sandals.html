Proper sizing and care are essential for maximizing the lifespan and comfort of your BZees sandals. Unlike traditional sandals that may require complex maintenance routines, BZees' innovative design simplifies care while delivering superior performance when properly maintained.

Sizing Guide

Based on extensive customer feedback and my personal testing across multiple styles:

  • Stretch Fabric Styles: Order 1/2 size smaller than normal
  • Standard Styles: True to size for most foot shapes
  • Wide Feet: Stretch styles accommodate naturally
  • Narrow Feet: Consider sizing down 1/2 size
  • Between Sizes: Choose smaller size for secure fit

Pro Tip: The dynamic stretch uppers will adapt to your foot shape within 2-3 wears, so a snug initial fit is preferable to a loose one.

Machine Washing Guide

The revolutionary machine washable feature requires specific care steps:

  1. Remove any debris from soles and straps
  2. Use cold water cycle (30°C/86°F maximum)
  3. Add mild detergent, avoid bleach or fabric softener
  4. Wash with similar colors to prevent dye transfer
  5. Air dry completely before wearing
  6. Never use heat drying or direct sunlight

Frequency: Machine wash after every 15-20 wears or when visibly soiled for optimal hygiene and appearance.
