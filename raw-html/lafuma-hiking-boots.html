Always measure your feet in the afternoon when they're naturally swollen to ensure the best fit for long hiking days. The anatomical footbed design accommodates most foot shapes well, but those with high arches may want to consider custom insoles for optimal comfort during extended use.

How waterproof are Lafuma hiking boots really?

Lafuma hiking boots with Climactive or Gore-Tex membranes provide excellent waterproof protection in real-world conditions.
