Frequently Asked Questions

Are Mezlan shoes worth the investment for luxury footwear?

Yes, mezlan shoes offer exceptional value for luxury footwear enthusiasts. With over 50 years of Spanish craftsmanship tradition, each pair features handmade construction, premium materials including exotic leathers, and memory foam comfort technology. How do Mezlan shoes fit compared to other brands?

Mezlan shoes typically run slightly larger than standard US sizing. Most customers recommend ordering a half-size down from your regular shoe size. The Spanish last used in construction tends to be more generous, particularly in length. I always advise trying them on or ordering from retailers with good return policies, especially for your first pair. Some users report needing to go down a full size, while others find a half-size sufficient, so individual foot shape plays a role in the optimal fit.

What makes Mezlan's exotic leather shoes special?

Mezlan's exotic leather collection features genuine crocodile, ostrich, lizard, and snake skins sourced ethically and crafted by master Spanish artisans. Each exotic skin has unique characteristics - crocodile offers durability and prestige, ostrich provides distinctive texture and flexibility, while lizard skins offer refined elegance. The handcrafted construction ensures each pair showcases the natural beauty of these premium materials. Unlike printed or embossed patterns, these are authentic exotic skins that develop character and patina over time, making each pair truly unique.

Where can I find the best deals on Mezlan shoes?

The best mezlan shoes deals can be found at the official Mezlan Warehouse, authorized retailers like Nordstrom during sales events, and specialty stores like Sherman Brothers. End-of-season clearances often offer 40-60% discounts. Amazon occasionally stocks certain models, though selection is limited. Always verify authenticity when purchasing from third-party sellers, as the craftsmanship and materials are key to the Mezlan experience. Consider checking outlets like discount shoe retailers for occasional deals.

How should I care for my Mezlan shoes to maintain their quality?

Proper care is essential for mezlan shoes. Use cedar shoe trees immediately after wearing to maintain shape. Clean with appropriate leather cleaners - saddle soap for regular leather, specialized exotic leather cleaners for crocodile or ostrich. Condition monthly with quality leather conditioner. Rotate pairs to allow 24-hour rest between wears. Store in dust bags away from direct sunlight. Professional resoling extends the life significantly due to the quality construction. For exotic leathers, avoid excessive moisture and consider professional cleaning annually to maintain their unique characteristics.
