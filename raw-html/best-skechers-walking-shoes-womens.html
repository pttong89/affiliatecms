Fitting and Sizing Guidelines

Proper fit is crucial for comfort and performance. Most skechers walking shoes womens run true to size, but individual models may vary. I recommend trying shoes in the afternoon when feet are naturally more swollen, similar to how they'll feel during long walks. Consider these fitting tips based on my extensive testing experience:

  • Allow thumb-width space between longest toe and shoe front
  • Ensure heel doesn't slip when walking
  • Check for adequate width without pinching at widest foot part
  • Test with your typical walking socks thickness
