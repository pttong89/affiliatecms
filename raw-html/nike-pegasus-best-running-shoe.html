Sizing and Fit Considerations

Proper sizing is crucial for experiencing why Nike Pegasus earns best running shoe recognition. I recommend visiting a specialty running store for professional fitting, especially if transitioning from other brands. Nike's sizing tends to run true-to-size, but individual foot shape variations can affect optimal fit.

Consider the following fit factors:

  • Allow thumb-width space between longest toe and shoe front
  • Ensure secure heel lockdown without pressure points
  • Test fit in the afternoon when feet are naturally swollen
  • Consider wide or narrow variants if available for your foot type
  • Account for sock thickness and seasonal swelling variations

For runners with specific fit challenges, explore Nike's expanding size range including wide and extra-wide options. The brand has improved inclusivity in recent years, making the Nike Pegasus best running shoe experience accessible to more foot types than ever before.
