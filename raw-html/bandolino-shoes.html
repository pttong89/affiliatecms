Complete Buying Guide

Sizing and Fit Considerations

Proper sizing represents the most crucial aspect of purchasing bandolino shoes online or in-store. Based on extensive testing across multiple styles, I've found that Bandolino generally runs true to size, but with some important nuances. Their pumps tend to fit slightly narrow, particularly in the toe box area, so women with wider feet should consider ordering a half-size larger. Conversely, their loafers and flats typically offer more generous width, making them suitable for various foot shapes.

When measuring your feet for Bandolino purchases, I recommend measuring both feet in the afternoon when natural swelling has occurred – this ensures optimal fit for extended wear. The brand's size chart proves remarkably accurate, but remember that different styles may fit differently due to varying construction methods. For those unsure between sizes, I generally recommend sizing up rather than down, as quality insoles can compensate for slightly loose fit better than tight shoes can be stretched.
