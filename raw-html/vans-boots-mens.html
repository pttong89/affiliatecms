Sizing and Fit Guide

After fitting dozens of customers and testing multiple sizes personally, I've developed specific recommendations for vans boots mens sizing. Unlike traditional skateboard shoes, MTE boots require more precise fitting due to their technical construction and intended use cases.

General Sizing Recommendations

Most Vans MTE boots run true to standard US sizing, but I recommend ordering a half-size up if you plan to wear thick hiking socks or orthotics. During my Colorado winter testing, the additional room proved essential for proper circulation and comfort during extended cold exposure.

Important Sizing Note

Chelsea boot models tend to run slightly narrow. Consider wide-width options if you have broader feet or plan extended wear periods.

The Vans shoes for men collection offers various width options, though they're not always clearly advertised. I strongly recommend trying boots on in-store when possible, especially for work or hiking applications where improper fit can cause significant discomfort.

Breaking In Your Vans Boots

Unlike traditional work boots, most MTE models require minimal break-in periods. However, I recommend gradually increasing wear time over the first week to allow the footbed to conform to your specific foot shape.
