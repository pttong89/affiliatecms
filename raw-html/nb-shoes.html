Frequently Asked Questions

What does NB mean in shoes?

NB stands for New Balance, the renowned athletic footwear company founded in 1906 in Boston. The brand has become synonymous with quality running and lifestyle shoes, offering exceptional arch support and comfort. New Balance pioneered the concept of offering shoes in multiple width sizes, making them a favorite among people with specific foot needs. The company's medical heritage, starting as an arch support manufacturer, continues to influence their shoe designs today, which is why podiatrists frequently recommend nb shoes for various foot conditions.

Which NB shoes are best for beginners?

For beginners, I highly recommend the New Balance 574 or Fresh Foam Roav series. These models offer excellent cushioning, stability, and versatility at an affordable price point. The 574 is perfect for casual wear and light activities, while the Fresh Foam Roav provides superior comfort for walking and light jogging. Are NB shoes good for people with flat feet?

Yes, many nb shoes are excellent for flat feet, particularly models with motion control features. The New Balance 860 v12 and Fresh Foam X Vongo v6 are specifically designed with enhanced arch support and stability features. These shoes help control overpronation common in flat-footed individuals. I always recommend consulting with a podiatrist for personalized advice, as foot structure varies significantly between individuals. Based on my experience and research, New Balance consistently ranks among the top recommendations from medical professionals for flat foot conditions due to their superior arch support technology and motion control systems.

How do NB shoes compare to Nike and Adidas?

NB shoes excel in comfort, arch support, and width options compared to Nike and Adidas. While Nike focuses on performance innovation and Adidas emphasizes style, New Balance prioritizes foot health and comfort. NB shoes typically offer superior cushioning for long-distance walking and running. However, Nike and Adidas may have more trendy designs and advanced performance technologies for competitive athletes. How often should I replace my NB shoes?

Replace your nb shoes every 300-500 miles for running shoes, or every 6-8 months for daily wear shoes. Signs it's time to replace include worn tread patterns, compressed midsole cushioning, uneven wear patterns, or discomfort during wear. High-quality NB shoes like the 990 series may last longer with proper care. I track my mileage and inspect my shoes monthly to ensure optimal performance and foot health. The superior construction quality of New Balance shoes often extends their lifespan compared to cheaper alternatives, but replacing them before excessive wear prevents injury and maintains the therapeutic benefits that make New Balance shoes special.
