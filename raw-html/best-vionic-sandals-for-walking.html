Proper fit is crucial for orthotic effectiveness. Unlike fashion sandals where loose fit might be acceptable, Vionic sandals require precise sizing to function correctly. During my testing, I discovered that foot size can fluctuate throughout the day and across seasons, affecting how sandals fit and perform.

The arch support should feel snug but not painful – it should support your arch without creating pressure points. Adjustable straps are invaluable for fine-tuning fit, especially for those with foot conditions that cause swelling. Remember that proper orthotic support requires a secure fit – sandals that slide around can't provide effective biomechanical correction.
