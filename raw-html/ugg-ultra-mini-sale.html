Sizing and Care Tips for Maximum Longevity

Proper sizing represents the most critical factor in enjoying your UGG Ultra Mini sale investment for years to come. Through extensive testing across different UGG styles and personal consultations with UGG store associates, I've learned that Ultra Mini boots require different sizing considerations than traditional UGG boots due to their shorter shaft and snugger fit around the ankle.

The general rule for UGG boots Ultra Mini sizing is to order a half size down from your regular shoe size, particularly if you typically wear whole sizes. This accounts for the sheepskin lining's natural compression over time and ensures a snug initial fit that will relax to perfect comfort within the first few wears.

My Sizing Guide by Foot Type

Narrow Feet

Size down a full size for optimal fit. The snugger construction works well with narrow foot shapes.

Wide Feet

Order true to size or only half size down. Allow extra break-in time for comfortable fit.

High Arches

True to size usually works best. The ankle height accommodates higher arches well.

Essential Care Routine for Sale UGGs

Pre-Treatment

Apply UGG protector spray before first wear and monthly thereafter. This prevents staining and water damage.

Weekly Cleaning

Brush with suede brush in one direction to maintain nap. Remove surface dirt before it sets in.

Drying Protocol

Always air dry away from direct heat. Stuff with newspaper to maintain shape during drying.

Storage

Store in breathable bags with cedar shoe trees. Avoid plastic containers that trap moisture.
