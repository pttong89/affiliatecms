Do I need waterproof Adidas hiking shoes?

Waterproof versus breathable is one of the most important decisions when selecting hiking footwear. During my testing, I found non-waterproof Adidas models excel in dry conditions and warm weather due to superior breathability and faster drying times. Gore-Tex versions provide reliable waterproofing but sacrifice some breathability and cost more. I recommend non-waterproof models for most three-season hiking, especially in dry climates. Choose waterproof versions if you frequently hike in wet conditions, during early/late season, or in consistently damp environments. Remember that waterproof shoes will eventually wet out from internal moisture during strenuous activities.
