What should I bring on a local walking adventure?

Essential items depend on walk length, terrain, and weather conditions, but core items include water, navigation tools, and emergency supplies. For walks under two hours, carry at least 16 ounces of water per person, a fully charged cell phone with offline maps downloaded, and basic first aid supplies including bandages and pain relievers. Weather-appropriate clothing layers allow adaptation to changing conditions without overheating or getting chilled. A small backpack or waist pack carries essentials without creating burden. For longer walks or challenging terrain, add extra water, snacks, emergency whistle, flashlight, and backup navigation tools. Always inform someone of your planned route and expected return time, regardless of walk difficulty or duration.
