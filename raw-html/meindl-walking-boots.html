The ComfortFit models may run even larger, so careful attention to sizing is essential. Always check Meindl's sizing guide and consider their generous return policy when ordering online.

What makes Meindl ComfortFit boots different?

ComfortFit technology specifically addresses the needs of hikers with wider feet through an expanded toe box and modified last construction.
