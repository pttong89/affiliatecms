On Cloud shoes generally run true to size, but I recommend sizing up half a size if you have wider feet or plan to wear thick socks. During long shifts, feet naturally swell, and that extra room prevents discomfort during peak swelling hours. I've found that most professionals prefer a thumb's width of space between their longest toe and the shoe front.

Different work environments require different shoe characteristics. For hospital settings with potential spill hazards, I recommend models with better traction patterns like the Cloudrunner 2.
