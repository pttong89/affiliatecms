The Ultimate Sizing Guide

Sizing for Rick Owens shoes can be notoriously tricky, and I've learned this lesson through both personal experience and conversations with other collectors. The brand's Italian sizing system, combined with the unique construction methods and materials, means that sizing can vary significantly between different models and even between colorways of the same shoe. After trying on dozens of pairs across various retailers, I've developed a comprehensive understanding of how different Rick Owens models fit.

For Geobaskets, the general consensus among experienced buyers is to size down by half a size from your normal sneaker size. These shoes are built on a wider last and tend to run large, especially in length. However, individuals with wider feet may find their true size more comfortable. The leather construction means the shoes will stretch and mold to your feet over time, so a snug initial fit is preferable to a loose one. I personally wear size 42 in most sneakers but find size 41.5 ideal for Geobaskets.

Pro Sizing Tips from Real Users

  • Always try on both feet when possible, as Rick Owens shoes can fit asymmetrically
  • Consider the thickness of socks you'll wear with different models
  • Leather models will stretch more than canvas or synthetic options
  • Visit authorized retailers during the winter months when feet are naturally smaller

The sizing philosophy for Ramones differs slightly from Geobaskets due to their more streamlined construction. These shoes typically run true to size or slightly large, making them more predictable for online purchases. However, the DRKSHDW line uses different lasts than the mainline Rick Owens shoes, so experience with one doesn't necessarily translate to the other. The relationship between Dr Martens Rick Owens collaborations and regular Rick Owens sizing also varies, as Dr. Martens brings their own sizing conventions to the partnership.

For boots and more structured designs, I recommend sticking closer to your normal shoe size while accounting for the specific last used. Rick Owens hiking boots and similar outdoor-inspired designs often fit more conventionally due to their functional requirements. The key is understanding that Rick Owens prioritizes aesthetic impact over traditional comfort considerations, which affects both fit and sizing across the range.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do Rick Owens shoes typically cost?

Rick Owens shoes typically range from $800 to $1,500 for standard models, with special editions and collaborations commanding higher prices. Geobaskets usually retail between $1,000-$1,200, while boots and more complex designs can exceed $1,500. Seasonal sales at authorized retailers occasionally offer 20-30% discounts, making timing crucial for budget-conscious buyers. The investment is significant, but the combination of Italian craftsmanship, unique design, and strong resale value helps justify the cost for serious fashion enthusiasts seeking distinctive footwear pieces.

Do Rick Owens shoes run true to size?

Rick Owens shoes sizing varies significantly between models, with most requiring adjustments from your normal shoe size. Geobaskets typically run large and require sizing down by half a size, while Ramones tend to fit closer to true size. The Italian sizing system, combined with unique lasts for different models, creates inconsistency that makes trying on essential when possible. Leather models stretch over time, so a snug initial fit is preferable. I recommend consulting size guides specific to each model and reading user reviews before purchasing, especially for online orders where returns may be complicated.

Are Rick Owens shoes comfortable for daily wear?

Rick Owens shoes prioritize aesthetic impact over traditional comfort, resulting in a unique wearing experience that requires adjustment. The break-in period typically lasts 1-2 weeks, during which the substantial construction and premium materials conform to your feet. Once properly broken in, most models become quite comfortable for extended wear, though the architectural soles and unconventional proportions feel different from mainstream sneakers. The weight and silhouette require minor adjustments to walking patterns initially. Daily comfort ultimately depends on individual foot shape, sizing accuracy, and adaptation to the brand's distinctive construction methods and design philosophy.

Where can I buy authentic Rick Owens shoes?

Authentic Rick Owens shoes are available through the official Rick Owens website, authorized luxury retailers like SSENSE, Farfetch, MR PORTER, and physical boutiques in major fashion cities.
