Pro Setup Tips from My Experience

  • Always practice setup at home before your trip – familiarity prevents frustration in challenging weather
  • Carry backup stakes suitable for your terrain – standard stakes fail in sand and hard clay
  • Use a footprint or tarp to extend floor life and improve insulation properties
  • Position doors away from prevailing winds to reduce interior drafts and rain intrusion
  • Allow adequate clearance from trees and hazards when using instant pop-up models
