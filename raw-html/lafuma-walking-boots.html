Complete Buying Guide

Sizing and Fit Considerations

Proper sizing is crucial when purchasing Lafuma walking boots. Based on my experience fitting dozens of hikers, I recommend trying boots on in the afternoon when feet are naturally swollen. Wear the same sock thickness you'll use for hiking – typically merino wool or synthetic hiking socks.

For Lafuma walking boots, I've found that most models run true to European size but may feel narrow compared to American brands. If you have wider feet or high arches, consider going up half a size. The toe box should allow comfortable wiggle room, as your feet will swell during long hikes.

Choosing the Right Model

Your hiking style and typical terrain should dictate which Lafuma walking boots model you choose. For casual day hiking on established trails, the Access Clim series provides excellent value and comfort.
