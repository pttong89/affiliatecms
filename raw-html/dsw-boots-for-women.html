Frequently Asked Questions

What makes DSW boots different from other retailers?

DSW specializes exclusively in footwear, allowing them to offer extensive brand variety, competitive pricing, and expert fitting services. Their VIP rewards program, frequent sales events, and focus on customer education through knowledgeable staff set them apart from general retailers. Additionally, their ability to carry both budget-friendly and premium brands under one roof makes comparison shopping convenient and efficient.

How do I know which size to order when shopping online?

DSW provides detailed size charts for each brand, as sizing varies significantly between manufacturers. I recommend measuring your feet at home using their measurement guide, reading customer reviews for fit feedback, and taking advantage of their free returns if the fit isn't perfect. For new brands, consider ordering two sizes to compare, returning the one that doesn't fit as well.

What's the best time of year to shop for boots at DSW?

The best deals occur during end-of-season clearance events: late February through March for winter boots, and late August for summer styles. However, for the newest styles and best selection, shop early in the season – September for fall boots and November for winter styles. Black Friday and end-of-year sales also offer significant discounts on current season inventory.

Does DSW offer wide width boots for women?

Yes, DSW carries wide width options from several brands including Naturalizer, Easy Spirit, and Dr. Scholl's. Their website allows filtering by width, and store associates can help identify brands that run wider or offer extended width options. Many customers with wide feet find success with brands like Clarks and Easy Spirit, which tend to have more accommodating fits.

Can I return boots to DSW if they don't fit properly?

DSW offers a generous return policy allowing returns within 60 days of purchase with original receipt and unworn condition. VIP members get extended return periods and additional benefits. Returns can be made to any DSW store location or shipped back using their prepaid return labels. Sale items and clearance merchandise may have different return policies, so check your receipt for specific terms.

Are DSW's private label boots worth buying?

Kelly & Katie, DSW's private label, offers excellent value for money with current styles at accessible price points. While they may not have the prestige or longevity of premium brands, they're perfect for trendy styles, building a diverse collection on a budget, or trying new looks without major investment. The quality has improved significantly over the years, making them a smart choice for fashion-forward shoppers.
