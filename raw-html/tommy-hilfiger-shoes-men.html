How should I care for my Tommy Hilfiger leather shoes?

Proper care significantly extends the life and appearance of your Tommy Hilfiger leather shoes. I recommend cleaning them weekly with a soft brush to remove surface dirt, followed by a damp cloth for deeper cleaning when needed. Apply quality leather conditioner every 2-3 months to maintain suppleness and prevent cracking. For dress shoes, regular polishing with matching color polish maintains their professional appearance. Always allow shoes to dry completely between wears, and consider using cedar shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture. Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Waterproofing spray can help protect against stains and moisture, especially for lighter colored shoes. With proper care, Tommy Hilfiger leather shoes can maintain their appearance and comfort for several years of regular wear.
