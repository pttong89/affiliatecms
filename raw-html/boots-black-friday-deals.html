Which boot brands offer the biggest Black Friday discounts?

Timberland, Columbia, Merrell, and UGG consistently offer 30-50% discounts during Black Friday. Work boot brands like Caterpillar and Red Wing typically provide 25-40% savings, while premium hiking boot manufacturers like Salomon and KEEN offer 20-35% discounts. Lesser-known brands often provide deeper discounts of 40-70% to compete with established names. The key is timing your purchase with each brand's specific sale periods. Are boots Black Friday deals better online or in-store?

Online deals typically offer better selection and convenience, while in-store shopping allows for trying on boots for proper fit. Many retailers offer price matching between their online and physical stores. Online exclusive deals often provide deeper discounts, especially on Amazon and direct-to-consumer brands. However, popular sizes sell out quickly online, making in-store shopping valuable for hard-to-find sizes. The best strategy combines both approaches: research and compare prices online, but visit physical stores for fitting and immediate availability. Some retailers offer "buy online, pickup in store" options that combine the best of both worlds.
