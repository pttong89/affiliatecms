What Are Rick Owens Kiss Boots?

Rick Owens Kiss boots are the designer's most controversial and recognizable footwear creation. First introduced in the early 2010s, these platform boots feature a distinctive clear acrylic heel that typically measures 5+ inches in height, combined with a 2+ inch platform sole.

The boots come in various iterations including:

  • Grilled Kiss: Featuring metal-look grilles on the heel
  • Clear Kiss: Transparent acrylic heel with gradient effects
  • Solid Kiss: All-black leather construction
  • Taco Kiss: Over-the-knee variations
  • Beveled Kiss: Angular heel construction

The boots are crafted in Rick Owens' Italian facilities using premium materials including soft grain leather, stretch lamb leather, and the signature acrylic heel components. Each pair represents Rick's vision of futuristic gothic luxury.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Rick Owens Kiss boots worth the investment?

How do I determine the right size for Kiss boots?

Sizing is crucial and differs from standard footwear. For leather versions, size down 0.5 to 1 full size from your typical shoe size, as the leather will stretch with wear. Suede versions require sizing down a full size due to less stretch. If you have wide feet, consider staying closer to your true size, while narrow feet should definitely size down. The boots should feel snug initiallyâ€”they will mold to your feet over the first few weeks of wear. When possible, try them on in person or purchase from retailers with good return policies to ensure proper fit.

How do I maintain and care for Kiss boots?

Proper maintenance is essential for longevity. Clean the acrylic heel regularly with a soft microfiber clothâ€”never use harsh chemicals or abrasive materials. Condition the leather upper every 3-4 months with high-quality leather conditioner. Store the boots with heel protectors when not in use to prevent scratches and chips. Avoid wearing them in wet conditions or on rough surfaces. Replace heel tips before they wear completely through the acrylic. Consider professional cleaning and conditioning annually if you wear them frequently. The investment in proper care will significantly extend their lifespan.

What's the best way to style Kiss boots for beginners?

Start with the minimalist approach: well-fitted black pants and a simple black top. This combination allows the boots to be the focal point without competing elements. Avoid busy patterns, bright colors, or multiple statement pieces in your first attempts. Focus on clean lines and monochromatic palettes. As you gain confidence, you can experiment with different textures and silhouettes, but always maintain visual balance. Remember that Kiss boots add significant presence to your lower half, so your upper body styling should complement rather than compete with this impact.

Are Kiss boots comfortable for extended wear?

Comfort is subjective and improves significantly after the break-in period. The initial 2-3 weeks require adjustment as your feet adapt to the height and your walking gait changes. Once broken in, many wearers find them surprisingly comfortable for special occasions and moderate walking. However, they're not suitable for extensive walking, standing for long periods, or athletic activities. The platform design does provide some stability compared to traditional high heels. If you're accustomed to wearing heels or platforms, the transition will be easier. Consider them more appropriate for fashion events, photo shoots, and social occasions rather than daily practical wear.
