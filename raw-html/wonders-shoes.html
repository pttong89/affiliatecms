How should I care for my Wonders leather shoes?

Proper care extends the life of your wonders shoes significantly. Clean them regularly with a soft, damp cloth and allow them to air dry naturally. Use a high-quality leather conditioner every 2-3 months to maintain the soft leather's suppleness. Store them with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture. Rotate between pairs when possible to allow the leather to rest and breathe between wears. Avoid harsh chemicals or machine washing, and if they get wet, stuff them with newspaper and let them dry slowly at room temperature. With proper care, these shoes will develop a beautiful patina and provide years of comfortable wear.
