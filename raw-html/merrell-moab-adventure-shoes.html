Sizing, Fit, and Buying Guide

Getting the right fit in merrell moab adventure shoes is crucial for comfort and performance. Based on fitting dozens of pairs and consulting with specialty footwear retailers, here's everything you need to know about sizing and selection.

Critical Sizing Information

Merrell sizes run approximately 0.5 sizes large compared to most athletic shoes. This is particularly true for the Adventure 3 models. However, the waterproof versions tend to fit more snugly due to the additional membrane lining.

Wide width options are dramatically different from standard widths, not just slightly roomier. If you normally wear wide shoes, the Merrell wide versions are essential. Standard width wearers should avoid wide versions as they'll be too loose.

Sizing Recommendations

  • Standard feet: Order 0.5 size down from your athletic shoe size
  • Wide feet: Order true size in wide width models
  • Thick socks: Consider true size for heavy hiking socks
  • Waterproof models: May need true size due to snugger fit
  • Mid-height boots: Often feel smaller than low shoes in same size

Fit Testing Tips

  • Afternoon fitting: Try shoes when feet are slightly swollen
  • Hiking socks: Wear your intended sock thickness
  • Toe room: 1/2 inch space between longest toe and shoe end
  • Heel slip: Minimal movement when laced properly
  • Width comfort: No pinching at widest part of foot
