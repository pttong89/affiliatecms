What's the best way to care for Crane outdoor clothing to maintain performance?

Proper care significantly extends the life and performance of crane outdoor clothing. Wash items in cold water using technical fabric detergent, avoiding fabric softeners that can compromise DWR coatings and breathability. Air dry when possible, or use low heat settings if machine drying becomes necessary. Reapply DWR treatment annually or when water no longer beads on the fabric surface. Store items hanging or folded loosely to prevent compression damage to insulation. Following these guidelines ensures your crane outdoor clothing maintains its technical performance characteristics for years of reliable use in challenging conditions.
