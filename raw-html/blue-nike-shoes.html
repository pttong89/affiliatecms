How do I clean and maintain blue Nike shoes?

Cleaning blue Nike shoes requires gentle care to preserve both color vibrancy and material integrity. Start by removing loose dirt with a soft brush, then use a mixture of mild detergent and warm water applied with a clean cloth. For leather blue Nike shoes, use specialized leather cleaner to prevent discoloration. Avoid harsh chemicals or bleach that can cause blue dyes to fade or change color. Air dry completely away from direct sunlight to prevent color alteration. For deep cleaning, consider professional shoe cleaning services. Different blue materials may require specific care approaches, so always check Nike's care instructions for your specific model. For winter conditions, blue Uggs might be more appropriate.
