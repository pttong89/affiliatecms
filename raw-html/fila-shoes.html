What is the best way to clean and maintain FILA shoes?

Proper maintenance significantly extends FILA shoe lifespan, especially for their popular white models. I've found that regular cleaning with a soft brush and mild soap solution works best for synthetic and leather uppers. For stubborn stains on white models, magic erasers are remarkably effective without damaging the material. Always air dry FILA shoes away from direct heat or sunlight, as excessive heat can damage the Memory Foam cushioning and adhesive bonds. For athletic models used in outdoor environments, I recommend rotating between multiple pairs to allow complete drying between uses. This rotation strategy has helped my FILA shoes maintain their comfort and appearance for years of regular use.
