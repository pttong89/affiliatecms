What's the difference between ninjalines and traditional slacklines?

Ninjalines are specifically designed obstacle courses featuring attachment points every few feet for hanging obstacles like monkey bars, rope climbs, and balance challenges. Traditional slacklines are flat webbing used primarily for balance training. Ninjalines from Slackers outdoor adventures combine the balance element with upper body strength training through various obstacles. The professional-grade webbing in ninjalines is typically wider and stronger, designed to handle dynamic movements and multiple attachment points while maintaining safety standards. Ninjalines offer more versatile training opportunities compared to single-purpose slacklines.
