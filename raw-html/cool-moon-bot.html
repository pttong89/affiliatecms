How should I care for my Cool Moon boots?

Proper care significantly extends Cool Moon boots' lifespan and maintains their appearance, based on my three-year testing experience. Clean boots after each use with a soft brush to remove dirt and salt deposits before they can damage the leather. Apply high-quality leather conditioner monthly during active wearing seasons to maintain flexibility and water resistance. Store boots with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture between wears. For deep cleaning, use saddle soap followed by leather conditioner, allowing 24 hours for complete drying. Never use direct heat sources like radiators, which can crack the leather and damage the sheepskin lining.
