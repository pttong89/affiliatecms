Key Features and Technology

Waterproof TEX Membrane Technology

The standout feature of crivit walking boots is their integrated TEX membrane system. This technology creates a barrier that prevents water penetration while allowing moisture vapor to escape. During my testing, this system proved effective in various wet conditions, from morning dew-soaked grass to stream crossings and light rain showers. The breathability aspect works well during moderate activity levels, though intensive hiking in warm conditions can lead to some interior moisture buildup.

Construction and Materials

The boots feature a combination of synthetic materials and textile components designed to balance durability with affordability. The upper construction uses eco-friendly leather production methods, as highlighted in product descriptions. The toe and heel areas incorporate reinforced synthetic materials to withstand abrasion from rocks and rough terrain. Internal mesh lining provides additional comfort and helps prevent pressure points during extended wear.

Sole Design and Grip Performance

The outsole design incorporates a deep lug pattern optimized for varied terrain conditions. The rubber compound provides reasonable grip on most surfaces, though performance on wet rocks and smooth surfaces is moderate. The midsole construction includes phylon cushioning material that offers decent shock absorption for day hiking activities. Ankle support is adequate for most hiking conditions, though serious backpackers might prefer boots with more robust support systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Crivit walking boots waterproof?

Yes, most crivit walking boots feature waterproof technology with TEX membrane construction. This integrated membrane provides water resistance while maintaining breathability. However, the level of waterproofing varies between models, with some offering basic water resistance and others providing more robust protection suitable for wet conditions and stream crossings. During my testing, the waterproof performance proved reliable for typical hiking conditions, though extended exposure to heavy rain or deep puddles may eventually lead to moisture penetration.

How do Crivit walking boots compare to premium brands?

Crivit walking boots offer excellent value for money but lack some advanced features of premium brands. They provide adequate durability and comfort for casual hiking and day walks. However, premium brands typically offer superior materials, advanced cushioning systems, better ankle support, and longer warranties. For occasional hikers, Crivit boots represent a cost-effective entry point into outdoor footwear, while serious hikers might benefit from investing in higher-end alternatives for better long-term performance and specialized features.

Where can I buy Crivit walking boots?

Crivit walking boots are primarily available through Lidl stores and their online platform during special outdoor gear promotions. Availability is often seasonal, with the best selection typically appearing during spring and autumn outdoor gear sales at Lidl stores across Europe. Online marketplaces offer more consistent availability but often at higher prices than direct Lidl purchases.

What sizes do Crivit walking boots come in?

Crivit walking boots are available in European sizing, typically ranging from EU 36 to EU 46, which covers UK sizes 3.5 to 11.5. The boots are designed with German shoe strips for optimal fit and come in both men's and women's specific models. Some unisex models are also available. It's recommended to try them on or carefully check size charts, as sizing can vary slightly between different Crivit models and production batches.

How long do Crivit walking boots typically last?

With moderate use, crivit walking boots typically last 1-2 years or approximately 300-500 miles of hiking. Their durability depends heavily on usage patterns and terrain. For casual day hiking and walking, they can provide excellent service for their price point. However, for intensive hiking or challenging terrain, users may experience faster wear on the sole and upper materials compared to premium hiking boots. Regular maintenance and appropriate care can extend their lifespan significantly.

Pro Tips for Crivit Boot Care

  • Waterproof Treatment: Apply DWR (Durable Water Repellent) treatment annually to maintain water resistance
  • Proper Drying: Remove insoles and allow boots to air dry completely after wet conditions
  • Regular Cleaning: Clean mud and debris promptly to prevent material degradation
  • Storage: Store in cool, dry conditions away from direct sunlight when not in use
