Merrell hiking boots generally run true to size but tend to have a wider fit than brands like Salomon or La Sportiva. Should I choose waterproof or non-waterproof Merrell boots?

The choice depends on your hiking environment and personal preferences. From my experience, waterproof versions excel during creek crossings, muddy trails, and unpredictable weather conditions. I've kept my feet dry through multiple river crossings in my waterproof Moab 3s. However, non-waterproof models offer superior breathability, making them ideal for hot, dry climates and summer hiking. If you primarily hike in wet conditions or during shoulder seasons, invest in waterproof models. For desert hiking or hot summer trails, non-waterproof versions provide better temperature regulation and faster drying times.
