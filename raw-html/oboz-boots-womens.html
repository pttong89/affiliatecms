Frequently Asked Questions

How do Oboz boots for women fit compared to regular shoe sizes?

Oboz boots typically run about half a size smaller than your regular shoe size, especially in hiking models. The brand's narrower heel design provides excellent support but requires proper sizing to avoid discomfort. This sizing approach prevents common issues like black toenails and hot spots during extended hikes.
