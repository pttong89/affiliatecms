What's the difference between steel toe and composite toe options?

Steel toe caps provide traditional protection with proven durability and are generally less expensive. They offer excellent impact resistance and are preferred in many industrial environments. Composite toe caps are lighter, non-metallic (airport friendly), and provide better insulation in extreme temperatures. They meet the same ASTM safety standards but may have a slightly larger toe box profile. Choose steel toe for maximum durability and cost-effectiveness, or composite toe if you need non-metallic footwear, work in extreme temperatures, or frequently pass through metal detectors.
