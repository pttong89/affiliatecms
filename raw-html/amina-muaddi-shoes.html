Complete Sizing and Fit Guide: My Personal Experience

Getting the perfect fit in amina muaddi shoes can be tricky, and I learned this the hard way with my first purchase. After trying on dozens of pairs across five different styles, I've cracked the code on Amina Muaddi sizing – and I'm sharing all my secrets with you.

Slingback Styles (Begum, Camelia, Holli)

These pointed-toe beauties run notoriously small, especially the PVC versions. I normally wear a size 38 EU, but I need a 38.5 or even 39 in the Begum slingbacks. The crystal embellishment sits right at the widest part of your foot, so if you have wider feet like I do, definitely size up. My crystal Begums in size 38.5 fit perfectly with just enough room to prevent the straps from cutting in during long wear.

Mules and Slides (Gilda, Lupita, Alexa)

These styles are much more forgiving and typically run true to size. My Gilda glass sandals in size 38 are perfect – secure but not tight. The open-back design naturally accommodates different foot widths, making them ideal for those between sizes. I actually prefer the 70mm heel height for everyday wear; it's substantial enough to make a statement but comfortable for extended walking.

Boots and Closed-Toe Pumps

The boot collection runs closest to true size in my experience. My Veneda stretch boots in size 38 fit like a dream, hugging my legs perfectly without being restrictive. For the closed-toe pumps like the Camelia, I recommend sticking with your normal size unless you have particularly wide feet.

Pro Sizing Tips From My Experience

  • Order from retailers with free returns when trying new styles
  • PVC materials don't stretch, so err on the larger side
  • Consider foot swelling – I size up 0.5 for evening events
  • The pyramid heel distributes weight beautifully, making higher heels surprisingly stable

Speaking of comfort, I cannot emphasize enough how remarkable these shoes are for their heel height. My 105mm Begum slingbacks feel more comfortable than many 85mm heels from other brands. The secret is in the heel construction and the precise angle of the foot bed. During a recent museum opening that required four hours of standing, my feet felt significantly better than they do in comparable luxury heels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Amina Muaddi shoes run true to size?

Based on my experience with multiple pairs, Amina Muaddi shoes typically run slightly small, especially the slingback styles. I recommend sizing up half a size for pointed-toe styles like the Begum, particularly in PVC materials that don't stretch. For mules and boots, true to size usually works well, but if you're between sizes, go with the larger option for optimal comfort during extended wear.

Are Amina Muaddi shoes worth the investment?

Absolutely. The craftsmanship is exceptional with handmade construction in Italy using premium materials. The signature pyramid heel and crystal embellishments are trademarked designs you won't find elsewhere. They're surprisingly comfortable for luxury heels and hold their value well in the resale market. Consider them an investment in both style and quality – I've worn my pairs extensively and they still look pristine after years of use.

How comfortable are Amina Muaddi shoes for all-day wear?

I've worn my Begum slingbacks for 8+ hour events and they're remarkably comfortable compared to other luxury heels. The lightweight construction, strategic weight distribution, and engineered heel angle make them one of the most comfortable designer heels I own. There's a brief break-in period of about 3 wears, but once broken in, they're surprisingly supportive. The pyramid heel provides excellent stability that traditional stilettos can't match.

What's the best way to care for crystal-embellished styles?

Store them in dust bags with individual crystal protection, avoid contact with rough surfaces, and clean crystals gently with a soft brush. For PVC styles, use mild soap and water, then dry thoroughly. I recommend professional cleaning for major maintenance. The crystals are securely attached, but gentle handling extends their lifespan significantly. Avoid wearing them in rain or on rough pavement to maintain the pristine crystal appearance.

Where can I find authentic Amina Muaddi shoes on sale?

End-of-season sales at authorized retailers like MyTheresa, Saks, and Farfetch typically offer 20-40% discounts. The best sales occur in January-February and July-August. Sign up for notifications at multiple retailers since stock varies. Authenticated resale platforms like Vestiaire Collective and The RealReal also offer excellent deals on previous seasons. Avoid unauthorized sellers as counterfeits are increasingly common – stick to verified retailers for guaranteed authenticity.
