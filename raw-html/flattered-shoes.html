About Flattered: Swedish Elegance Meets Artisan Craftsmanship

Founded in Stockholm in 2013, Flattered has established itself as a premier Scandinavian footwear brand that embodies the essence of minimalist design and exceptional quality. The brand's name derives from their original signature collection of ballet flats, which continue to be their most beloved and sought-after products.

What sets Flattered apart is their commitment to ethical production and sustainability. All Flattered shoes are designed in the heart of Stockholm and handcrafted by skilled artisans in Southern Europe, specifically in Italy, Portugal, and Spain. This European production ensures not only exceptional quality but also supports traditional craftsmanship techniques that have been perfected over generations.

Brand Philosophy

Flattered creates "a new realm of luxury for the conscious individual, where form and function unite." Their designs are guided by minimalism and crafted to echo the Scandinavian tradition of timelessness, contributing to reshaping consumption habits toward quality over quantity.

The brand has also made significant social impact commitments, partnering with organizations like Camfed to donate to young women's education in Sub-Saharan Africa. As of mid-2025, their contribution equals 20,947 weeks of education, providing four years of secondary school for 145 students.

Sizing & Fit Guide

Official Sizing Information

According to Flattered's official sizing guide, their shoes are true to size unless the product description specifically states otherwise under Size Information. However, real customer experiences reveal some important nuances.

Important Sizing Notes

  • • Flattered uses European (EU) sizing exclusively
  • • No half sizes available
  • • Pointed toe styles may fit smaller
  • • Real leather stretches approximately half a size in width

Customer Experiences

Pointed Toe Styles

Customers recommend sizing up for pointed toe heels, as they tend to run small, especially in the toe box area.

Wide Feet Considerations

Some styles accommodate wider feet better than others. The flats may have gaping issues for narrow feet but work well for wider feet.

Break-in Period

Genuine leather shoes require a break-in period and will stretch about half a size with regular wear.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Flattered shoes true to size?

Flattered officially states their shoes are true to size, but customer experiences vary. Many recommend sizing up for pointed toe styles, as they tend to run small. Since Flattered doesn't offer half sizes, consider your foot width and the specific shoe style. Real leather shoes will stretch about half a size in width with wear, so factor this into your decision. For the most accurate fit, measure your feet and consult the size chart on each product page.

What makes Flattered shoes special compared to other brands?

Flattered distinguishes itself through Scandinavian design principles, ethical European production, and exceptional comfort. Unlike mass-produced shoes, each pair is handcrafted in Italy, Portugal, or Spain using premium materials. The brand focuses on timeless designs rather than fast fashion trends, ensuring your investment lasts years. Additionally, Flattered commits to social responsibility through educational donations, making each purchase contribute to meaningful causes while supporting sustainable fashion practices.

How do I care for my Flattered shoes?

Proper care extends your Flattered shoes' lifespan significantly. Use Flattered's own leather care products or high-quality alternatives. Regular conditioning prevents cracking and maintains suppleness. For everyday maintenance, brush off dirt and allow shoes to air dry completely between wears. Store with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape. For deeper cleaning, use specialized leather cleaners and always test on an inconspicuous area first. Professional cleaning is recommended for serious stains or damage.

Can I return Flattered shoes if they don't fit?

Flattered offers exchanges and returns, though policies may vary by region and retailer. When purchasing directly from Flattered, they typically provide free exchanges within a specified timeframe. International customers should carefully review return policies as shipping costs may apply. To ensure smooth returns, keep original packaging and avoid wearing shoes outdoors until you're certain of the fit. Consider ordering multiple sizes if unsure, as this often proves more cost-effective than multiple shipping cycles.

Are Flattered shoes worth the investment?

For those prioritizing quality, comfort, and ethical production, Flattered shoes represent excellent value. The premium pricing reflects genuine leather construction, artisan craftsmanship, and timeless designs that won't quickly go out of style. Many customers report wearing their Flattered shoes for years with proper care. However, if you're budget-conscious or prefer trendy styles that change frequently, consider whether the investment aligns with your priorities. The cost-per-wear often justifies the initial expense for quality-focused consumers.
