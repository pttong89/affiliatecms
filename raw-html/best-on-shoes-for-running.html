Technical Specifications Breakdown

Understanding On's technologies helps identify which models align with your biomechanical needs and performance goals. Each technology serves specific purposes and creates distinct ride characteristics:

Hollow pods compress vertically on impact and lock horizontally during toe-off. Creates soft landing with firm takeoff sensation. Best for efficient runners who appreciate ground connection.

Models: All On shoes feature some variation of CloudTec cushioning

Speedboard Technology

What makes On's CloudTec technology special?

CloudTec technology features hollow pods in the midsole that compress vertically on impact and lock horizontally during toe-off. This creates a unique soft landing and firm takeoff sensation. Combined with the Speedboard, it provides responsive energy return while maintaining excellent shock absorption during long runs. Unlike traditional foam that gradually loses cushioning properties, CloudTec elements maintain their structural integrity and performance characteristics throughout the shoe's lifespan. The technology also allows for temperature stability, performing consistently across varying weather conditions where traditional foams might become too firm or soft. This innovative approach prioritizes biomechanical efficiency over maximum cushioning depth, creating a distinctive ride that enhances natural running mechanics.
