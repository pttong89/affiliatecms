Frequently Asked Questions

Are just so so shoes made by Nike or affiliated with major brands?

No, just so so shoes are manufactured by independent Chinese factories and sold through various distributors. They are not affiliated with Nike, Adidas, or any major athletic brand. The design similarities to premium sneakers are intentional to offer comparable aesthetics at budget prices. Unlike established brands such as Allen Iverson shoes with decades of basketball heritage, just so so focuses purely on accessible pricing.

How long do just so so shoes typically last with regular use?

Durability varies significantly based on usage patterns and individual models. For casual wear and light exercise, expect 6-12 months of regular use. Heavy athletic activities or construction work may reduce lifespan to 2-4 months. This differs greatly from premium work footwear like Tecova boots which are designed for years of demanding use. The key is setting appropriate expectations for budget footwear and having backup pairs available.

Do just so so shoes run true to size compared to major brands?

Sizing varies between models, but generally runs close to Nike and Adidas sizing for men's shoes. Women's models tend to run slightly narrow, so consider ordering 0.5 size up if you have wider feet. Children's sizes consistently run small, making a half-size upgrade advisable. Unlike specialized footwear such as Alohas shoes with detailed sizing guides, just so so requires more careful measurement and potentially ordering multiple sizes initially.

Are just so so shoes suitable for serious running or athletic training?

Just so so shoes work well for casual jogging, gym workouts, and recreational sports, but aren't designed for competitive athletics or intensive training programs. The cushioning and support systems don't match specialized running shoes from major brands. However, for budget-conscious fitness enthusiasts doing moderate exercise, they provide adequate performance. Consider them similar to entry-level athletic shoes rather than performance-oriented options like professional APL shoes designed for serious athletes.

What's the return policy when buying just so so shoes online?

Return policies vary significantly by retailer. Amazon offers their standard 30-day return policy with free returns for Prime members, making it the safest purchase option. The official just so so website has stricter return requirements and slower customer service response times. Third-party sellers on eBay, Walmart, and other platforms have individual policies. Always purchase from retailers with clear return policies, especially when trying the brand for the first time. Can just so so shoes be used for hiking or outdoor activities?

Just so so shoes can handle light hiking on well-maintained trails and casual outdoor activities, but aren't designed for serious mountaineering or rough terrain. The blade-type sole provides decent traction on packed dirt and moderate inclines, making them suitable for urban hiking and nature walks. However, for challenging outdoor adventures or professional use, invest in specialized footwear like alligator boots or dedicated hiking shoes. I've successfully used them for day hikes under 5 miles on moderate terrain.

Are just so so shoes available in wide widths or special sizes?

Most just so so shoe models come in standard width only, which can be problematic for people requiring wide or narrow fits. Unlike premium brands that offer extensive width options, just so so prioritizes affordability over accommodation of diverse foot shapes. Some users report success ordering a half-size up for width accommodation, but this isn't guaranteed to work for everyone. If you require specialized fits, consider investing in brands known for width variety rather than budget options. The trade-off between price and fit customization is a key consideration when choosing just so so shoes.
