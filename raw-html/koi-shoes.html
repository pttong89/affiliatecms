Vegan Ethics & Sustainability: KOI's Environmental Impact

100% Vegan Materials and Manufacturing

KOI Footwear maintains a strict commitment to vegan production across their entire range. Unlike many brands that offer vegan options alongside leather products, koi shoes are exclusively cruelty-free. Their primary materials include water-based polyurethane, synthetic textiles, and recycled rubber components for outsoles.

The brand's water-based PU represents a significant improvement over traditional petroleum-based alternatives. This material reduces volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions during manufacturing and offers better biodegradability at end-of-life. However, it's important to note that no synthetic material can match the environmental profile of properly sourced natural materials in terms of complete biodegradability.

Manufacturing and Supply Chain Transparency

KOI's manufacturing occurs primarily in China through third-party facilities, which raises questions about supply chain oversight and worker conditions. While the brand claims ethical factory partnerships, independent verification of these practices remains limited. This contrasts with more transparent vegan brands that provide detailed facility certifications.

The company has partnered with OneTriem to offset carbon emissions from shipping and has implemented tree-planting initiatives. However, their overall environmental rating from Good On You remains "not good enough," primarily due to limited evidence of comprehensive sustainability practices and the environmental impact of synthetic materials production.

Comparing KOI to Other Sustainable Footwear

When evaluated against other ethical footwear brands, KOI occupies a middle ground. They excel in animal welfare but lag behind in comprehensive environmental practices. Brands like Veja and Allbirds demonstrate more holistic approaches to sustainability, incorporating recycled materials, carbon neutrality, and transparent supply chains.

For consumers prioritizing vegan ethics above all other factors, koi shoes represent a solid choice.
