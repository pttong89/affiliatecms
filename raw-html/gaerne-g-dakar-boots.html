Frequently Asked Questions

Are Gaerne G Dakar boots true to size?

Yes, Gaerne G Dakar boots run true to size based on extensive user feedback and my personal testing. The Italian sizing corresponds accurately to standard US measurements. I recommend using Gaerne's official sizing chart on their website for the most precise fit. The boots feature memory foam padding that adapts to your foot shape over the first few rides, ensuring a custom-like fit. If you're between sizes, most riders find their normal size works perfectly due to the adjustable buckle and Velcro closure system.

How waterproof are the Gore-Tex Dakar boots?

The Gore-Tex Performance Membrane in Gaerne G Dakar boots provides excellent waterproofing while maintaining breathability. During my testing through Pacific Northwest rain and creek crossings, they kept my feet completely dry for hours. The seam-sealed construction and quality leather upper work together with the Gore-Tex liner to create a reliable barrier against water ingress. However, like all Gore-Tex products, proper care and occasional reproofing of the leather will maintain optimal performance over years of use.

What's the difference between Gore-Tex and Aquatech versions?

The main difference lies in the waterproof membrane technology and upper materials. Gore-Tex versions use nubuck leather with the proven Gore-Tex Performance Membrane, offering superior waterproofing and breathability. Aquatech models feature microfiber uppers with Gaerne's proprietary DryTech membrane, providing good weather resistance at a lower price point. Based on my experience, Gore-Tex versions excel in extreme conditions and long-term durability, while Aquatech models work well for fair-weather adventure riding and offer better value for occasional use.

Can you walk comfortably in Gaerne G Dakar boots?

Yes, the Gaerne G Dakar boots are surprisingly walkable for adventure boots. The anatomically shaped Gaerne-Ergo+ footbed and flexible sole design allow for comfortable walking during gas stops, sightseeing, and short hikes. The tessellated tread pattern provides good grip on various surfaces. While they're not hiking boots, I've comfortably walked several miles during multi-day adventure tours. The key is the balance between protection and flexibility that Gaerne achieved with their Smart-Dual-Joint ankle system, allowing natural foot movement while maintaining support.

How do Gaerne G Dakar boots compare to Sidi Adventure 2?

Both are excellent Italian-made adventure boots, but they serve different preferences. Gaerne G Dakar boots excel in comfort and all-day wearability, with superior ankle flexibility and a more relaxed fit. They're better for long touring rides and riders who prioritize comfort. Sidi Adventure 2 boots offer more rigid protection and a sportier feel, making them ideal for aggressive off-road riding. In my testing, Gaerne boots were more comfortable for extended wear, while Sidi provided better protection during hard enduro sections. Choose Gaerne for touring comfort, Sidi for maximum protection.
