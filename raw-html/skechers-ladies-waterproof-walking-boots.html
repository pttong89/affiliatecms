Frequently Asked Questions

Are Skechers ladies waterproof walking boots truly waterproof?

Yes, Skechers waterproof walking boots feature advanced waterproof membranes and treated uppers that provide excellent water resistance. Models like the Trego series use waterproof synthetic materials combined with sealed seams to keep feet dry in wet conditions. How do Skechers waterproof boots compare to other hiking boot brands?

Skechers waterproof walking boots offer excellent value compared to premium hiking brands like Merrell or Salomon. What's the best Skechers model for ladies who need wide-fit boots?

The Skechers Relaxed Fit Trego series is ideal for ladies needing wide-fit waterproof walking boots. How should I care for and maintain my Skechers waterproof boots?

Proper care extends the life of your Skechers waterproof walking boots significantly. After each hike, remove dirt and debris with a soft brush and lukewarm water. Allow boots to air dry naturally, avoiding direct heat sources that can damage waterproof membranes. Apply a waterproofing treatment every 3-6 months depending on usage frequency. Clean the insoles regularly and replace them when worn. For stubborn stains, use mild soap and a soft brush. Store boots in a cool, dry place with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape. Regular maintenance ensures optimal waterproof performance and extends boot lifespan considerably.
