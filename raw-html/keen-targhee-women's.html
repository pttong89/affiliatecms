Sizing and Fit Guide: Getting It Right the First Time

The Half-Size Down Rule

Through my testing of multiple pairs and conversations with other female hikers, I've confirmed that KEEN Targhee boots consistently run about half a size large. This isn't just marketing speak—it's a real consideration that affects comfort and performance. When I first ordered my usual size 8, I experienced heel slippage and hot spots during longer hikes. Switching to size 7.5 eliminated these issues entirely.

The generous toe box that KEEN is famous for can mask sizing issues initially. You might think the fit is perfect when standing in the store, but mile after mile of hiking will reveal whether you've chosen correctly. Pay particular attention to how your heel feels in the boot—it should be snug without being tight, with no lifting during the walking motion.
