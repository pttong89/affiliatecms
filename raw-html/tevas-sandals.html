Sizing Strategy for Perfect Fit

Teva sizing runs remarkably consistent across models, but understanding the nuances prevents sizing mistakes. I recommend ordering your normal athletic shoe size for most Teva models. However, if you're between sizes or have wide feet, size up rather than down. The adjustable strap systems accommodate slight size variations, but you can't compensate for sandals that are fundamentally too small.

During seasonal changes, your feet naturally swell, especially during hot weather hiking. I've learned to account for this by ensuring adequate toe space‚Äîabout a thumb's width between your longest toe and the sandal's front edge. The heel should sit comfortably in the heel cup without overhanging the back. If you plan to wear thick hiking socks with your men's sandals or women's models, consider sizing up an additional half size.
