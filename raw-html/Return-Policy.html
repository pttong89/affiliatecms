Return Conditions

✓ Accepted Returns

Items in new, unused condition with original tags attached

Items tried on indoors without signs of wear

Technical gear tested briefly in controlled indoor conditions

Items in original packaging with all accessories and documentation

Defective items regardless of use (covered by warranty)

Personal care items and undergarments for hygiene reasons

Customized or personalized items

Items damaged by misuse or normal wear and tear

How to Return Items

1

Initiate Your Return

Log into your account at explorenationalparks.store and select "Return Items" from your order history, or contact our customer service team at returns@explorenationalparks.store with your order number.

2

Receive Return Authorization

We'll email you a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and prepaid shipping label within 24 hours. This RMA is required for processing your return.

3

Package Your Items

Securely package your items in the original box if available, or any suitable packaging. Include the RMA number and packing slip. Attach the prepaid shipping label.

4

Ship Your Return

Drop off at any authorized shipping location or schedule a pickup. You'll receive tracking information to monitor your return shipment.

5

Processing & Refund

We'll inspect your return within 2-3 business days of receipt and process your refund or exchange. You'll receive email confirmation once complete.

International Returns

International customers can return items following the same process, with these additional considerations:

Shipping Costs

International return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to our error or product defect.

Customs & Duties

Any customs duties or fees incurred during the return process are the customer's responsibility.

Extended Timeline

International returns may take 7-14 business days for processing due to shipping and customs clearance.

Required Documentation

Include customs forms and RMA number clearly visible on the package exterior.
