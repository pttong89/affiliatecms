How should Gucci boots fit for women?

Gucci boots women should fit snugly but not tight, with about a thumb's width of space at the toe. If you're between sizes, generally go with the smaller size for leather boots as they'll conform to your foot shape.

How do you care for and maintain Gucci boots?
