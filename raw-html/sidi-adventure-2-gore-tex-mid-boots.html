Sizing Guide & Fit Tips

Understanding Sidi Sizing

Getting the right size in sidi adventure 2 gore tex mid boots is crucial for comfort and performance. Based on my experience and feedback from numerous riders, Sidi sizing tends to run slightly large compared to typical U.S. shoe sizes. I recommend ordering a half size smaller than your regular footwear, especially if you have narrow to medium width feet.

Sizing Recommendations

For Normal Width Feet:

  • • Order 0.5 size smaller
  • • Consider thick riding socks
  • • Allow for removable insoles

For Wide Feet:

  • • Order true to size
  • • May need to remove arch supports
  • • Consider 0.5 size up if very wide

My Personal Sizing Experience

I typically wear a U.S. size 10.5 in athletic shoes and boots. For my Sidi Adventure 2 Gore Tex Mid boots, I ordered EU size 44 (U.S. 10), and the fit is perfect. There's enough room for thick wool socks during winter rides while maintaining a secure, non-sloppy fit with thinner socks in warmer weather.

The removable arch supports play a crucial role in fine-tuning the fit. If your boots feel slightly tight initially, removing these insoles can provide additional room. Conversely, if they feel too roomy, aftermarket insoles or thicker arch supports can improve the fit. Many riders find this adjustability useful for accommodating different sock thicknesses throughout the year.

Break-in Period Tips

While these boots are comfortable from day one, they do benefit from a brief break-in period:

  • Wear them for short walks before long rides
  • Start with 2-3 hour rides to assess comfort
  • Adjust buckles to find your preferred tightness
  • Consider different sock combinations

For riders considering different color options, sizing remains consistent across variants. Whether you choose the brown colorway or stick with the classic brown Adventure 2 options, the fit characteristics are identical.
