Complete Sizing and Fit Guide

Getting the right size in platform ultra mini UGGs is crucial for both comfort and style. Unlike some UGG styles that notoriously run large, the platform version tends to run more true to size, though there are several factors to consider when making your decision. Based on customer feedback and my own experience with multiple pairs, here's everything you need to know about sizing.

The general consensus among wearers is that platform ultra mini UGGs fit true to size for most foot shapes. However, if you're between sizes or prefer a snugger fit, sizing down half a size often works well. The boots are designed to fit snugly initially, as the UGGplush lining will compress slightly with wear. This compression creates a custom fit over time, so don't be concerned if they feel tight during your first few wears.

Important Sizing Considerations:

  • Wide feet: Consider going up half a size for comfort
  • Narrow feet: Your true size or half size down works best
  • Thick socks: Stick to your true size if you plan to wear thick socks
  • Foot length at size border: Size up for long-term comfort

The ankle opening on platform ultra mini UGGs is more generous than regular Ultra Mini boots due to the platform construction. This means they're easier to slip on and off, which was a common complaint about the original Ultra Mini style. However, this also means less ankle support, so if you have narrow ankles, you might experience some heel slippage initially.

For those considering platform ultra mini UGGs as a gift or ordering online without trying them on, I recommend consulting UGG's official size chart and measuring your feet properly. Keep in mind that UGG sizes can vary slightly from standard US shoe sizing. If you own other UGG products, use those as a reference point, but remember that different styles can fit differently.

One unique aspect of the platform version is how the added height affects the overall proportion and fit. The 2-inch platform doesn't change the internal foot dimensions, but it does alter how the boot looks on your leg. Shorter individuals often find that the platform creates a more balanced silhouette, while taller wearers might find the proportion slightly overwhelming. This is more about personal preference than fit, but it's worth considering when making your purchase decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do platform ultra mini UGGs run true to size?

Most customers find platform ultra mini UGGs run true to size, though some recommend sizing down half a size if you prefer a snugger fit. The platform adds 2 inches of height, but the internal fit remains consistent with regular Ultra Mini sizing. If you have wide feet, consider going up half a size for optimal comfort, especially if you plan to wear thick socks.

Are platform ultra mini UGGs comfortable for all-day wear?

Yes, platform ultra mini UGGs are comfortable for extended wear thanks to their UGGplush wool blend lining and cushioned EVA midsole. However, the 2-inch platform may cause fatigue during long periods of standing or walking on uneven surfaces. They're ideal for moderate daily activities but may not be the best choice for extensive walking tours or standing jobs.

How do you style platform ultra mini UGGs for different occasions?

Platform ultra mini UGGs are versatile and work with leggings and oversized sweaters for casual looks, wide-leg jeans and blazers for elevated casual, or even mini dresses with tights for trendy winter outfits. The key is balancing proportions with the chunky platform by choosing tops with substantial visual weight and avoiding tight-fitting ankle-length pants that compete with the boot opening.

What's the difference between platform and regular ultra mini UGGs?

The main difference is the 2-inch EVA platform sole on the platform version, which adds height and a bolder look. Regular ultra mini UGGs have a flat sole and lower profile. Both feature the same suede upper and UGGplush lining for comfort, but the platform version is heavier and requires more adjustment to your gait.

Are platform ultra mini UGGs worth the investment?

At around $170, platform ultra mini UGGs are worth it if you love the elevated aesthetic and plan to wear them regularly. They're well-made with quality materials and offer unique styling opportunities that regular boots don't provide. However, they're more of a fashion statement than practical winter boots for harsh conditions, so consider your lifestyle needs before investing. For budget-conscious shoppers, there are quality alternatives available that offer similar styling at lower price points.
