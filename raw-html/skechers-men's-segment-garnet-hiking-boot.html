Are Skechers Segment Garnet boots waterproof?

No, the Skechers Segment Garnet boots are not fully waterproof. While the oiled leather upper provides some water resistance for light moisture and brief exposure, they will eventually allow water penetration through seams and the leather itself during extended wet conditions. The boots are better suited for dry hiking conditions or light rain rather than stream crossings or prolonged wet weather hiking. If waterproof protection is essential for your activities, consider looking at other Skechers men's hiking shoes that specifically feature waterproof technology.

How do Segment Garnet boots fit compared to regular Skechers shoes?

The Segment Garnet boots use Skechers' Relaxed Fit design, which provides more room than regular Skechers shoes, particularly in the toe box and overall width. Most users find them true to size, but if you typically wear regular-width shoes, you might find these feel roomier than expected. For those who usually need wide-width footwear, the Relaxed Fit may provide the perfect amount of space. I recommend ordering your normal Skechers size for casual wear, or going up half a size if you plan to wear thick hiking socks regularly. What maintenance do these leather boots require?
