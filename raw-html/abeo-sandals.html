Care and Maintenance Tips

Essential Maintenance Practices

Daily Cleaning

After each use, rinse with cool water and mild soap. Avoid hot water which can damage the BIOsystem materials. I use a soft brush to remove dirt from textured areas, particularly after hiking on dusty trails.

Proper Drying

Always air dry away from direct heat sources. The orthotic materials can warp under excessive heat. I position them in a well-ventilated area with good airflow, typically drying completely within 2-3 hours.

Odor Prevention

Use antimicrobial sprays designed for orthotic footwear. I apply these weekly during heavy use periods, particularly in humid conditions where bacterial growth is more likely.

What to Avoid

Never use bleach, harsh chemicals, or put ABEO sandals in washing machines or dryers. These can destroy the orthotic properties and void warranty coverage.

Storage considerations are often overlooked but significantly impact longevity. I store my ABEO sandals in a cool, dry location with adequate ventilation. Avoid leaving them in hot cars, closed gym bags, or damp basements where mold and material degradation can occur. Using shoe trees or paper stuffing helps maintain shape during extended storage periods.

Rotation between multiple pairs extends overall lifespan considerably. Having two pairs allows each to fully dry and recover between uses, preventing premature wear of the orthotic components. This strategy has proven particularly valuable during extended travel periods where daily wear would otherwise accelerate breakdown.

Warranty Protection

ABEO offers limited warranties on manufacturing defects, but proper care is essential for coverage. Document purchase dates and follow care instructions precisely. I photograph any wear patterns or issues that develop to support potential warranty claims.

Signs indicating replacement needs include: visible compression of the orthotic footbed, significant sole wear affecting traction, strap stretching that compromises fit, or persistent odors that resist cleaning. Generally, with proper care and moderate use, ABEO sandals maintain therapeutic effectiveness for 18-24 months of regular wear.
