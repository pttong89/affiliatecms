Sizing and Fit Guidelines

Proper fit is crucial for both safety and comfort. KEEN utility boots generally run true to size, but their wider toe box design may feel different initially. I recommend trying boots on later in the day when feet are naturally swollen to simulate work conditions.

For optimal fit, wear your typical work socks during sizing. The boot should feel secure around the heel and midfoot while allowing toe movement. There should be approximately thumb-width space between your longest toe and the boot's end. Remember that KEEN boots for women feature gender-specific lasts for improved fit.

Common Sizing Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ordering based on athletic shoe size rather than work boot size
  • Assuming all KEEN models fit identically across different series
  • Not accounting for thick work socks during size selection
  • Ignoring width considerations for optimal comfort
  • Failing to test boots during typical foot swelling times
