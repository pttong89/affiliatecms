Complete Buying Guide: Choosing Your Perfect ECCO Sandals

Sizing and Fit Considerations

Proper sizing is crucial for maximizing the benefits of ECCO sandals. Unlike mass-market brands that prioritize speed of production, ECCO uses European sizing standards and anatomically correct lasts that may feel different initially but provide superior long-term comfort.

Width Considerations

ECCO sandals typically run roomier in the toe box compared to narrow brands like Chaco. If you have narrow feet, consider going down a half size. Wide feet usually find the standard width accommodating.

Length Guidelines

Allow approximately 1/2 inch of space between your longest toe and the front of the sandal. This accommodates natural foot swelling during extended activities and prevents forward slide on descents.
