How should I care for my Skechers waterproof boots?

Proper care extends the life of your Skechers waterproof walking boots significantly. Clean them regularly with mild soap and warm water, avoiding harsh detergents that can damage the waterproof coating. Allow them to air dry completely at room temperature, never near direct heat sources. Apply waterproofing spray every 3-6 months or after heavy use to maintain protection. Remove insoles to clean separately and rotate between pairs when possible. Store in a cool, dry place with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape. Regular maintenance keeps the waterproof membrane effective and prevents premature wear. For women's walking boots Skechers models with leather uppers, use appropriate leather conditioners to prevent cracking.
