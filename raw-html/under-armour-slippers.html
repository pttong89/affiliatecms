Sizing Guide and Fit Tips

Proper sizing is crucial for maximizing the comfort and longevity of your under armour slippers. Through extensive testing with multiple models and sizes, I've developed reliable guidelines that help ensure optimal fit regardless of your foot shape or intended use.

General Sizing Recommendations

Most Under Armour slipper models run true to size, but several factors influence the ideal fit. For casual indoor use, ordering your regular shoe size typically provides appropriate comfort. However, if you plan extensive outdoor wear or prefer wearing socks with your slides, consider going up half a size to accommodate the additional bulk and prevent toe crowding.

The memory foam footbeds in premium models compress slightly over the first few weeks of regular wear. This natural settling process creates a more customized fit but may initially feel snug if you order your exact size. During my testing period, I found that slides initially feeling slightly loose became perfectly fitted after the break-in period.

Width Considerations

Under Armour slippers accommodate average foot widths effectively, but wide-footed individuals should pay special attention to strap positioning. The Ignite Pro series offers the most generous width accommodation, while the streamlined Locker V models may feel restrictive for particularly wide feet. Testing both standard and half-size-up options helps determine the best fit for your specific foot shape.

Seasonal considerations also affect sizing choices. During warmer months, feet naturally swell slightly, making a roomier fit more comfortable. Conversely, winter wear with thicker socks requires additional space. I recommend trying on under armour slippers later in the day when your feet are at their largest to ensure comfortable all-day wear.

The adjustable straps on certain models provide additional customization options. Unlike fixed-strap designs, these allow fine-tuning of the fit across the instep area. This feature proves particularly valuable for individuals with high arches or instep sensitivity. The connection to Under Armour's expertise in tennis shoe fitting is evident in these thoughtful design elements.

Pro Tip: If ordering online, check the specific model's reviews for sizing feedback. Some limited-edition colorways or special collaborations may fit differently than standard versions of the same model.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Under Armour slippers true to size?

Most Under Armour slippers run true to size, but I recommend going up half a size if you prefer a looser fit or plan to wear them with socks. The memory foam footbed compresses slightly over time, so a slightly larger size ensures long-term comfort. During my testing, models that initially felt snug became perfectly fitted after a few weeks of regular wear. Consider your foot width and intended use when making sizing decisions.

Can you wear Under Armour slippers outdoors?

Yes, under armour slippers are designed for both indoor and outdoor use. The durable rubber outsoles provide excellent traction on various surfaces, making them perfect for quick trips outside, pool areas, or casual outdoor activities. I've successfully worn them on camping trips, beach walks, and around resort properties without issues. The quick-drying materials handle moisture exposure well, though they're not designed for extended hiking or rough terrain use.

How long do Under Armour slippers last?

With regular use, under armour slippers typically last 12-18 months. The memory foam footbed may compress after 6-8 months of daily wear, but the overall durability remains excellent. Proper care and rotation with other footwear can extend their lifespan significantly. I've found that alternating between two pairs for different activities helps maximize longevity. The outsole rubber typically outlasts the memory foam, maintaining traction throughout the slipper's useful life.

Are Under Armour slippers good for plantar fasciitis?

Many users with plantar fasciitis find under armour slippers helpful due to their memory foam cushioning and arch support features. However, they're not medical devices, so consult your healthcare provider for specific foot conditions. The Ansa Fix series offers enhanced arch support compared to standard models. During my testing, several friends with foot issues reported noticeable comfort improvements when wearing these slippers for recovery periods after activities.

How do you clean Under Armour slippers?

Clean under armour slippers with mild soap and warm water for best results. Remove the footbed if possible and air dry completely in a well-ventilated area. Avoid machine washing or direct heat, which can damage the memory foam and synthetic materials. For stubborn odors, sprinkle baking soda inside and let sit overnight before brushing out. The quick-drying materials make maintenance simple, and regular cleaning helps preserve the anti-microbial properties. I recommend cleaning them weekly during heavy use periods.
