Do Peter Millar shoes run big or small?

Peter Millar shoes typically run about half a size small, particularly in their sneaker models like the Glide V3 and Camberfly. The Excursionist loafers tend to fit more true to size but may feel snug initially before breaking in. Always check the specific product reviews for sizing guidance, as individual foot shape and width preferences can influence the ideal fit.

How do you clean Peter Millar sneakers?

Peter Millar sneakers, particularly the Glide V3 model, are machine washable. Remove the insoles and laces before washing in cold water, then air dry completely. For suede models like the Excursionist loafers, use a suede brush and specialized suede cleaner. Regular maintenance includes wiping with a damp cloth after wear and using shoe trees to maintain shape. The performance materials are designed to resist stains and dirt naturally, making maintenance relatively straightforward compared to traditional leather footwear.
