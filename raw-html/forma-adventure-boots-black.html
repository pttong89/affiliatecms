Spring represents ideal conditions for these boots. Frequently Asked Questions

Are Forma Adventure boots black truly waterproof?

Yes, forma adventure boots black feature Drytex waterproof technology that provides excellent moisture protection. However, like any waterproof boot, proper maintenance with leather conditioner helps maintain their water-resistant properties over time. The key is allowing them to fully dry between rides and treating the leather annually with quality conditioner.

How do Forma Adventure boots black fit compared to regular shoe sizes?

Forma Adventure boots typically run close to true size, but I recommend ordering one size larger than your regular shoe size for optimal comfort. What's the difference between regular and low Forma Adventure boots in black?

The main differences lie in height and protection coverage. Regular Forma Adventure boots extend higher up the calf, providing more shin protection and a more aggressive adventure look. The low version stops just above the ankle, making them easier to get on/off and more suitable for urban riding or warmer climates. Both offer the same CE Level 2 protection at critical areas like the ankle and toe, but the low version sacrifices some shin coverage for increased mobility and comfort during walking. For touring in hot climates or frequent city riding, the low version often proves more practical.
