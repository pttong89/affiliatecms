Complete Buying Guide

Sizing and Fit Considerations

Getting the right fit is crucial when purchasing CK shoes online. Based on my extensive testing and consultation with Calvin Klein fit specialists, the brand typically runs true to size. However, there are some style-specific considerations to keep in mind. Sneakers tend to have a more generous fit, while dress shoes follow traditional sizing more closely.

During my visits to various Calvin Klein retailers, I learned valuable insights from experienced sales associates. They recommend ordering your standard US size for most styles, but suggest going up half a size for platform shoes or thick-soled sneakers. This advice proved accurate during my testing period, particularly with the women's Alondra platform sneakers.

Professional Sizing Tips

  • Sneakers: Order your regular size - they run true to fit
  • Dress Shoes: Consider your foot width - narrow feet may need half size down
  • Heels: Platform styles may need half size up for comfort
  • Boots: Allow room for socks - consider going up half size

How should I care for my Calvin Klein shoes?

Proper care extends the life of your CK shoes significantly. For leather styles, use quality leather conditioner monthly and protect with weatherproof spray. Sneakers benefit from regular cleaning with gentle soap and water, avoiding harsh chemicals that can damage materials. Store shoes with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture. Rotate between pairs when possible to allow proper drying and reduce wear. Professional cleaning for expensive styles is worth the investment for special occasion shoes.
