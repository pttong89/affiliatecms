Frequently Asked Questions

Are REV'IT Discovery GTX boots truly waterproof?

Yes, the Discovery GTX boots feature genuine GORE-TEX Performance Comfort membrane, which provides excellent waterproofing while maintaining breathability. I've tested them extensively in Pacific Northwest rain, creek crossings, and snow conditions without experiencing any leakage. The GORE-TEX membrane uses microscopic pores that prevent liquid water entry while allowing vapor to escape, ensuring your feet stay dry and comfortable. The seam sealing and construction quality maintain waterproof integrity even after thousands of miles of use. However, proper care and occasional reproofing of the outer leather will maintain optimal performance over time.

How does the BOA closure system work on these boots?

The BOA system uses a precision dial that tightens steel lace cables for a custom fit. Simply twist the dial clockwise to tighten and pull the dial out to release instantly. The system allows for micro-adjustments throughout the day as your feet swell or when wearing different sock thicknesses. The steel cables are remarkably durable and distribute pressure evenly across your foot and ankle. The dial mechanism is designed to withstand harsh conditions and heavy use. If needed, BOA offers replacement parts and warranties their system. The convenience factor is substantial – you can adjust fit one-handed while wearing gloves, making it ideal for quick stops during rides.

Can I walk comfortably in Discovery GTX boots off the bike?

Absolutely. The Vibram sole and OrthoLite insole provide excellent walking comfort, making them perfect for exploring destinations during adventure tours. I've hiked several miles in these boots across various terrain types, from rocky trails to sandy beaches. The flexible construction allows natural foot movement while maintaining ankle support. The multi-density sole provides cushioning for comfort yet remains firm enough for precise bike control. Many riders use them as their primary footwear during multi-day tours, eliminating the need to pack separate walking shoes. The grip pattern works well on wet surfaces, loose gravel, and smooth rock, providing confidence in diverse conditions.

What's the difference between Discovery GTX and Expedition GTX boots?

Discovery GTX boots are designed for touring comfort with moderate off-road capability, while Expedition GTX boots offer maximum protection for serious off-road adventures with taller height and stiffer construction. The Discovery GTX prioritizes comfort and versatility, making them ideal for adventure touring, highway riding, and moderate trail exploration. Expedition GTX boots feature additional armor, taller protection, and more aggressive sole patterns for extreme off-road use. The Discovery GTX offers better walking comfort and lower-profile styling, while Expedition GTX provides superior protection for technical terrain. Choose Discovery GTX for 70/30 road/dirt riding, and Expedition GTX for 30/70 road/dirt adventures. The price difference reflects the specialized construction and additional materials in the Expedition model.

Do these boots run true to size?

Most riders find Discovery GTX boots run true to size for average-width feet. However, if you're between sizes, many customers recommend sizing up, especially if you have wider feet or prefer thicker socks. The BOA system provides some adjustment capability, but starting with proper length is crucial. European sizing tends to be more precise than US sizing, so measure your feet carefully and consult REV'IT's size chart. The boots accommodate standard and slightly narrow feet well, but wide feet may find them restrictive. Consider that proper motorcycle socks add thickness, and feet swell during long rides. If ordering online, many retailers offer easy exchanges to ensure proper fit. The break-in period may affect initial comfort, so judge sizing after 100+ miles of use.
