How should KEEN hiking boots from REI fit?

KEEN hiking boots should fit with about a thumb's width of space between your longest toe and the boot's end. REI recommends sizing up 1/2 size from your regular shoe size to accommodate hiking socks and foot swelling during long hikes. The heel should feel secure without slipping, and there should be no pressure points across the instep. KEEN's signature roomy toe box allows natural toe splaying, which is crucial for comfort on long trails. Always try boots on later in the day when your feet are naturally swollen, mimicking hiking conditions.
