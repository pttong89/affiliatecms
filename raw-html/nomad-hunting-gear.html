Sizing Guide and Fit Analysis

Critical Sizing Information

Based on extensive user feedback and personal testing, Nomad runs small. Order one size larger for pants and two sizes larger for jackets to ensure proper fit with layering.

Why Nomad Runs Small

The sizing discrepancy appears intentional, designed around the assumption that hunters will layer extensively. However, this creates problems for hunters in warmer climates or those who prefer minimal layering. The "athletic" cut prioritizes range of motion over loose fit, which explains the need for size adjustments.

Specific Fit Characteristics

  • Jacket sleeves: Run long when properly sized, accommodate wrist gaiters well
  • Pant inseam: True to size chart when ordering up one size
  • Torso length: Adequate for most body types, may be short for very tall hunters
  • Shoulder room: Critical for bow hunters - the upsize recommendation ensures draw clearance

Personal Sizing Experience

As a 6'1", 205-pound hunter who normally wears Large, I found XX-Large jackets and X-Large pants provided the perfect fit for Wisconsin hunting conditions with merino base layers and fleece mid-layers underneath.

When in doubt, contact Nomad's customer service with your measurements and intended use. Their team understands the sizing quirks and can provide specific recommendations based on your hunting style and climate.
