Maintaining hey dude shoes is refreshingly simple. Remove the insoles and laces, then machine wash on a gentle cycle with cold water and a small amount of liquid detergent. Air drying is essential – never use direct heat sources as they can damage the materials and affect the shoe's flexibility.
