Are Axel Arigato shoes true to size?

For the Marathon Runner and chunkier models, many users recommend sizing up half a size. How do Arigato shoes compare to Common Projects in quality?

Arigato shoes offer comparable quality to Common Projects at a more accessible price point. Both brands use premium Italian leather and skilled European craftsmanship, with Axel Arigato manufactured in Portugal and Common Projects in Italy. The main differences lie in design philosophy and price: Common Projects focuses on ultra-minimalist aesthetics with their signature gold numbering, while Axel Arigato offers more variety with weekly drops and cultural collaborations. In terms of materials and construction, both use high-grade leather and similar sole construction methods. What makes Axel Arigato shoes sustainable?

Axel Arigato demonstrates strong sustainability commitment through multiple initiatives. All leather used is LWG (Leather Working Group) certified, ensuring environmentally responsible tanning processes. The outsoles contain at least 20% recycled rubber sourced from the SEAQUAL INITIATIVE, which transforms marine plastic waste into functional materials. The lining incorporates 29% recycled polyester from used plastic bottles. Manufacturing occurs at small, family-owned factories in Porto, Portugal, supporting traditional craftsmanship while minimizing industrial environmental impact. The brand's direct-to-consumer model also reduces packaging waste and transportation emissions compared to traditional retail distribution. Additionally, the weekly drop system helps reduce overproduction by allowing real-time response to demand rather than seasonal bulk manufacturing.

How should I care for my Axel Arigato leather sneakers?

Proper care extends the life and appearance of your Arigato shoes significantly. For leather versions, use a damp cloth to remove surface dirt, followed by a quality leather conditioner every few months to maintain suppleness. Avoid soaking the shoes or using harsh chemicals. For suede models, invest in a suede brush and protective spray to prevent staining and maintain the nap texture. Always allow shoes to air dry naturally away from direct heat sources. Use shoe trees when storing to maintain shape and prevent creasing. The brand offers a specialized care kit with water-based cleaning solution, microfiber cloth, and double-sided brush designed specifically for their materials. Regular rotation between multiple pairs also helps extend longevity by allowing leather to rest and naturally moisture balance between wears.
