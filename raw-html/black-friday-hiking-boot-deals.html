Size and Return Optimization

Sizing uncertainty kills many potential black friday hiking boot deals purchases. My solution: order two sizes when genuinely unsure, especially during free return periods. This strategy secured limited-time deals that would have disappeared while visiting stores for fitting. The time investment in processing returns (usually 15-20 minutes) pales compared to potential savings of $50-100 on premium boots.

Additionally, consider purchasing boots slightly earlier in the season if your size tends to sell out quickly. Popular sizes in mainstream brands often disappear by mid-Black Friday morning, leaving only uncommon sizes available at advertised prices.

Important Note: Always verify return policies before purchasing. Some black friday deals hiking boots are marked as final sale, eliminating the safety net of easy returns.
