How should I properly break in new Red Wing logger boots?

Breaking in Red Wing logger boots requires patience and systematic approach to avoid injury and ensure optimal fit. Start by wearing boots for 2-3 hours daily during low-impact activities, gradually increasing duration by one hour every few days. Apply mink oil or approved leather conditioner before the first wear and every 3-4 days during break-in. Wear appropriate work socks (merino wool or synthetic blends) to prevent blistering and hot spots. The complete break-in process typically takes 40-60 hours of wear, during which the leather conforms to your specific foot shape. Never attempt to accelerate break-in with heat or excessive force, as this can damage the leather and compromise waterproofing.
