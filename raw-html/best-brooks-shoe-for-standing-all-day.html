Sizing Considerations for Extended Standing

Proper sizing becomes critical when selecting the best Brooks shoe for standing all day. Your feet naturally swell during extended standing periods, and inadequate room can lead to painful pressure points and reduced circulation.

I recommend trying on Brooks shoes in the afternoon when your feet are naturally more swollen, simulating end-of-shift conditions. Consider going up half a size from your normal running shoe size, especially if you'll be wearing compression socks or orthotics.

Professional Sizing Tips:

  • Measure both feet - many people have different sized feet
  • Allow thumb-width space between longest toe and shoe front
  • Ensure adequate width to prevent lateral pressure
  • Test walk for 10+ minutes before purchasing
