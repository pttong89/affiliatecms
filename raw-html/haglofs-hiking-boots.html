How should I size Haglöfs hiking boots?

Proper sizing for Haglöfs hiking boots requires careful attention to their European sizing system and relatively narrow construction. I recommend ordering a half-size larger than your typical athletic shoe size to accommodate hiking socks and potential foot swelling during extended use. The toe box runs narrower than many hiking boot brands, so wide-footed hikers should consider this carefully before purchasing. Always measure feet later in the day when they're naturally swollen, and account for the thickness of your preferred hiking socks. The knitted collar construction provides excellent heel hold, so focus on getting adequate toe room rather than a snug overall fit. Many users report excellent fit with proper sizing, but returns due to width issues are common, making it essential to purchase from retailers with flexible return policies.

The flexible construction that provides exceptional comfort in temperate conditions becomes a liability when maximum warmth retention is essential. However, Haglöfs winter boots specifically designed for cold conditions offer enhanced insulation and weather protection. For winter hiking, I recommend pairing standard models with quality wool socks and gaiters for light snow conditions, but transitioning to dedicated winter footwear for serious cold weather adventures.
