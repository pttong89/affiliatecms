Sizing and Fit Considerations

Icepeak uses European sizing, which generally runs true to size for most hikers. However, I recommend trying them on with your typical hiking socks, as the initial fit can feel slightly snug. The toe box offers good width accommodation, making these shoes suitable for hikers with broader feet who often struggle with narrow-fitting brands. Consider sizing up half a size if you frequently hike steep downhill sections or prefer extra toe room.

Model Selection Guide:
