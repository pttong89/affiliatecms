The Rich Heritage of Via Spiga

Founded in 1985, Via Spiga emerged as a testament to Italian luxury footwear excellence, taking its name from the prestigious Via della Spiga in Milan's Quadrilatero della Moda. This legendary street, literally meaning "Alley of the Ear," forms part of Milan's most exclusive fashion district, setting the perfect backdrop for a brand destined to embody sophistication and style.

During my travels through Milan's fashion quarter, I've walked the very cobblestones where Via Spiga drew its inspiration.
