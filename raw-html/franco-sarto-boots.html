Frequently Asked Questions

Are Franco Sarto boots true to size?

Franco Sarto boots generally run true to size, though some customers find they run slightly large. Many reviewers recommend ordering your normal size first, but if you prefer a snug fit or have narrow feet, consider going down half a size. The brand offers both regular and wide calf options for knee-high styles, ensuring a proper fit for different leg shapes. Always check the specific product reviews for sizing insights on individual models, as there can be slight variations between styles.

What makes Franco Sarto boots comfortable?

Franco Sarto boots are renowned for their comfort due to several key features: cushioned insoles that provide all-day support, quality leather construction that molds to your feet over time, and thoughtful design elements like proper arch support. The brand uses Italian-inspired craftsmanship with attention to both style and comfort. Many models feature flexible soles and ergonomic footbeds that reduce fatigue during extended wear. The leather quality also plays a role, as it breaks in beautifully rather than becoming stiff or uncomfortable.

How do I care for Franco Sarto leather boots?

To maintain your Franco Sarto leather boots, clean them regularly with a soft cloth and appropriate leather cleaner. Apply leather conditioner every few months to keep the material supple and prevent cracking. Store boots with cedar shoe trees to maintain their shape, and allow them to air dry completely if they get wet. For suede styles, use a suede brush to maintain texture and remove surface dirt. Avoid harsh chemicals and always test any cleaning products on an inconspicuous area first. Regular maintenance will significantly extend the life of your boots.
