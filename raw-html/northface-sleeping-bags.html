How do I care for my North Face sleeping bag?

Proper care extends your North Face sleeping bag's lifespan significantly. Store it loosely in a large cotton or mesh bag, never compressed long-term. Wash infrequently using down-specific detergent in a front-loading machine on gentle cycle. Dry thoroughly in a large dryer with tennis balls on low heat for down bags, or air dry synthetic bags. Always ensure complete drying before storage to prevent mold and maintain loft. Spot clean minor dirt rather than washing the entire bag unnecessarily.
