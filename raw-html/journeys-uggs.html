After helping dozens of friends and family members find their perfect fit in journeys uggs, I've developed a systematic approach to UGG sizing that eliminates guesswork. Understanding UGG's Fit Philosophy

UGG boots are designed to fit snugly initially, similar to how a quality leather jacket feels when new. The sheepskin lining will gradually mold to your foot's unique contours, creating a custom fit over the first few wears. This means that what feels slightly tight initially will evolve into a perfect, personalized fit. Based on my extensive experience with multiple UGG styles, I recommend ordering your true shoe size for most styles.

Professional Sizing Tip

When trying on UGG boots at Journeys, your heel should sit comfortably in the heel cup with about 1/2 inch of space at the toe. The boot should feel snug around the sides without any pinching or pressure points.

Style-Specific Sizing Considerations

Different UGG styles at Journeys have unique fit characteristics that I've catalogued through personal testing. The Classic Ultra Mini tends to run slightly more generous than the regular Classic Mini, while the Bailey Bow styles typically require careful attention to width due to their construction.
