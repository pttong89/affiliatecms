Width considerations are particularly important with Michael Kors sandals. Platform and wedge styles generally offer more room in the toe box compared to flat sandals. If you have wider feet, I recommend sizing up half a size in closed-toe styles or choosing sandals with multiple adjustable straps.

Style-Specific Fitting Notes

Care and Maintenance Tips for Longevity

Proper care and maintenance can significantly extend the lifespan of your Michael Kors sandals, protecting your investment while keeping them looking pristine. Through years of testing different care methods, I've developed a comprehensive maintenance routine that preserves both the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity of these designer sandals.

Daily Cleaning Routine

  • Wipe with damp microfiber cloth after each wear
  • Remove dirt from crevices with soft brush
  • Air dry completely before storage
  • Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners

Deep Cleaning Process

  • Monthly leather conditioner application
  • Gentle saddle soap for stubborn stains
  • Metal hardware polish quarterly
  • Professional cleaning for major damage

Storage Solutions

  • Use shoe trees to maintain shape
  • Store in breathable dust bags
  • Avoid direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Cedar blocks for moisture control

Protection and Prevention Tips

Weather Protection

  • Use protective covers for outdoor events

  • Match sandals to activity intensity

How do I clean and maintain my Michael Kors sandals?

For leather sandals, use a damp cloth with mild soap and leather conditioner monthly. For synthetic materials, gentle soap and water work well. Always air dry away from direct sunlight. Store with shoe trees to maintain shape and use protective sprays to prevent stains. The hardware should be cleaned with a soft cloth and metal polish quarterly to prevent tarnishing. Avoid harsh chemicals, bleach, or abrasive cleaners that can damage the premium materials. Regular maintenance extends the lifespan significantly and preserves the aesthetic appeal of the designer elements.
