How should I care for my pink Adidas shoes to maintain their color?

Proper care for pink Adidas shoes involves regular cleaning with gentle, color-safe products and appropriate storage methods. Use a soft brush or cloth with mild soap and lukewarm water to remove surface dirt, avoiding harsh chemicals that might affect the pink pigmentation. Allow shoes to air dry completely away from direct sunlight, which can cause color fading. For suede models like the Gazelle, invest in a quality suede protector spray and brush to maintain texture and color integrity. Store your shoes in dust bags or boxes when not in use to prevent color transfer and material degradation.
