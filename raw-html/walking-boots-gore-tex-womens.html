What's the difference between Gore-Tex and other waterproof membranes?

Gore-Tex remains the gold standard for waterproof-breathable membranes in walking boots for women. How should I care for my Gore-Tex walking boots to maintain waterproofing?

Proper care extends the life of women's Gore-Tex walking boots significantly. Clean boots after each use with lukewarm water and soft brush, removing mud and debris that can compromise the membrane. Always air-dry naturally ‚Äì never use direct heat. Store in ventilated areas and inspect regularly for wear.
