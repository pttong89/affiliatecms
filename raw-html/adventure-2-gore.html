The full-height version provides superior protection for serious off-road riding and adverse weather conditions, while the mid-height version offers easier on/off convenience and reduced bulk for urban riding. Weight difference is approximately 200 grams per boot, with the mid version being more comfortable for walking but offering less overall protection.

What maintenance is required to keep Adventure 2 Gore boots performing optimally?
