Can Keen Troy boots be resoled or repaired?

Unfortunately, keen troy boots cannot be traditionally resoled due to their direct-attach PU construction method. This construction creates a molecular bond between the upper and sole that cannot be separated without destroying the boot. This is a significant consideration for buyers who prefer repairable footwear and represents one of the trade-offs for the advanced comfort features.

However, minor repairs to the upper leather, lace replacement, and insole swapping are possible and can extend the boots' usable life. Some users report success with professional boot repair services for specific issues like torn flex zones or damaged waterproof membranes, though these repairs may affect the boot's original performance characteristics.

This limitation is consistent with most modern work boots that prioritize advanced materials and construction methods over traditional repairability. For professionals who prefer repairable footwear, traditional welted construction boots remain a better choice despite the comfort advantages of the Troy design.
