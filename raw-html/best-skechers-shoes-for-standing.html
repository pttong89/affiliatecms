Can I wash Skechers work shoes in a washing machine?

Yes, most Skechers work shoes are designed to be machine washable, which is a significant advantage for maintaining hygiene in professional environments. Remove the insoles and laces before washing, use cold water on a gentle cycle, and allow air drying completely before wear. Avoid hot water or heat drying, as this can damage the memory foam and adhesives. For heavily soiled shoes, pre-treat stains and consider using a mesh laundry bag for protection. The machine-washable feature makes the best skechers shoes for standing particularly appealing to healthcare workers, food service employees, and others in environments requiring regular shoe sanitization. I typically wash mine bi-weekly and have found no degradation in comfort or structure over multiple cleaning cycles.
