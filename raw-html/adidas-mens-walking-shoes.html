What's the difference between Adidas running shoes and walking shoes?

While many Adidas running shoes work excellently for walking, dedicated walking shoes typically feature less aggressive tread patterns and more emphasis on comfort over speed. Running shoes may include torsion systems that feel restrictive during casual walking.

Based on Reddit community feedback, models without torsion plastic (like Pureboost) feel more natural for walking. However, proven running models like Ultraboost 22 excel in both applications, making them versatile choices for active individuals.

How long do Adidas men's walking shoes typically last?

Based on my testing and user reports, quality adidas mens walking shoes average 600-800 miles of use before significant wear. My Ultraboost 22s showed minimal degradation after 500 miles of varied terrain including rocky trails and concrete sidewalks.
