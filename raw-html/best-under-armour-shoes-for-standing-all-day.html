Under Armour sizing can vary between models, making proper fit crucial for all-day comfort. How long do Under Armour shoes last with daily standing wear?

With daily standing wear (8+ hours), premium HOVR models typically last 12-18 months before showing significant wear, while budget options like the Charged Assert series last 8-12 months. Do Under Armour shoes run small or large for standing applications?

Under Armour shoes generally run true to size, but for standing applications, consider ordering a half-size larger to accommodate foot swelling during long shifts. HOVR models tend to run slightly smaller than Charged models, so sizing up is particularly important for these premium options. Width can be an issue for some users – if you have wide feet or experience pinching, opt for wide width options when available. Always measure your feet later in the day when they're naturally swollen to get the most accurate size for all-day wear.

What's the difference between HOVR and Charged Cushioning technology?

HOVR technology provides energy return by capturing and redirecting impact energy back to your stride, reducing fatigue over long periods. It's ideal for dynamic standing situations with movement. Charged Cushioning offers responsive support through compression-molded foam that maintains shape and provides consistent cushioning throughout the day. It's better for static standing positions. HOVR models cost more but offer superior performance for high-demand environments, while Charged Cushioning provides excellent value for most standing applications. Both technologies significantly outperform traditional work shoe cushioning systems.

Can Under Armour shoes help with plantar fasciitis and foot pain?

The HOVR technology's energy return reduces impact on the plantar fascia, while strategic support elements help maintain proper foot alignment.
