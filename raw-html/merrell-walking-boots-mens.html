Understanding Merrell's Sizing and Fit

Length Considerations

Merrell boots generally run true to size, but allow for thumb-width space between your longest toe and boot end. I recommend trying boots on in the afternoon when feet are naturally swollen, mimicking hiking conditions.

Width Options

The Moab series offers both regular and wide widths, accommodating various foot shapes. Wide-footed hikers will find better comfort in Merrell compared to narrower European brands.

Waterproof vs. Non-Waterproof

Choose Waterproof When:

  • Hiking in wet climates (Pacific Northwest, Appalachians)
  • Stream crossings are common
  • Hiking during rainy seasons
  • Priority is dry feet over maximum breathability

Choose Non-Waterproof When:

  • Hiking in desert or dry conditions
  • Maximum breathability is priority
  • Budget is a primary concern
  • Primarily day hiking in fair weather
