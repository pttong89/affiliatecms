How waterproof are HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX in real conditions?

The Gore-Tex membrane in HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX boots provides excellent waterproof protection for most hiking conditions. What's the expected durability of HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX boots?

Based on my 6-month testing and lab analysis, the HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX boots show good durability up to about 250 miles of regular use. Are HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX suitable for backpacking?

The HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX boots are excellent for light backpacking with packs under 35 pounds, but I wouldn't recommend them for heavy multi-day backpacking. How do HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX compare to other hiking boots?

Compared to traditional hiking boots, the HOKA Anacapa Mid GTX offers superior comfort and lighter weight but less durability and ankle support. They excel against boots like the Merrell Moab 3 in cushioning and against the Salomon X Ultra 4 in comfort. The Anacapa boots are best for hikers who prioritize comfort over ruggedness, making them ideal for maintained trails and day hiking rather than technical terrain or extended backpacking trips.
