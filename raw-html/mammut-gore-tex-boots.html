How should I care for and maintain my Mammut Gore-Tex boots?

Proper maintenance significantly extends the life of mammut gore tex boots. After each hike, remove debris and allow them to air dry naturally – never use direct heat. Clean the uppers with a soft brush and mild soap when necessary. The PFC-free DWR treatment should be reapplied every 20-30 uses or when water stops beading on the surface. Store them in a cool, dry place with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape. Unlike traditional leather boots, avoid over-conditioning, as this can compromise the Gore-Tex membrane's breathability. Regular maintenance keeps them performing like new for years.
