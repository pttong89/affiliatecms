Professional Fitting Tips

Best Time to Measure

  • Measure feet in the afternoon or evening
  • Feet naturally swell throughout the day
  • Wear the socks you plan to use with the shoes
  • Measure both feet (they're often different sizes)

Fit Check Points

  • Thumb width space at the toe
  • No heel slippage when walking
  • Arch support aligns with your foot's arch
  • No pressure points on sides or top

Common Sizing Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ordering your exact usual size without adjustment
  • Assuming all Skechers models fit the same
  • Not considering foot swelling throughout the day
  • Ignoring width requirements for optimal support
  • Not testing with intended sock thickness
  • Rushing the decision without proper research
