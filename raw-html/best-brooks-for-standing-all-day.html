Start by assessing your work surface. Hard surfaces like concrete, tile, or metal flooring demand maximum cushioning, making the Glycerin 21 or Ghost 16 ideal choices. Should I size up in Brooks shoes for work?

Consider going up half a size in Brooks shoes for all-day standing, as feet naturally swell during long shifts. The extra room prevents pressure points and improves circulation.
