How should I care for and maintain my Magellan boots?

Proper maintenance significantly extends boot life and performance. After each use, remove dirt and debris with a soft brush, then air dry completely away from direct heat. Apply waterproof conditioner every 20-30 uses or when water stops beading on the surface. Store boots with cedar shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture. Inspect seams and wear points regularly, addressing any issues promptly. For models with removable insoles, alternate between two pairs to allow complete drying between uses. Rubber components benefit from occasional cleaning with mild soap and water. Following these practices, my oldest pair of Magellan boots has provided over four years of reliable service.
