Sizing, Fit, and Care Guide

Proper sizing and care are crucial for maximizing your ALDO men's shoes investment. Through trial and error with multiple pairs, I've developed a comprehensive understanding of their sizing characteristics and optimal care practices.

ALDO Sizing Guide and Fit Characteristics

ALDO men's shoes generally run true to size in length but tend to be slightly narrower than American brands. If you typically wear Nike or Adidas sneakers, you'll likely need the same size in ALDO dress shoes, but consider a half-size up if you have wide feet or prefer roomier fits.

During my fitting experiences at ALDO stores, I've noticed that their European-inspired lasts create a more tapered toe box compared to American brands. This design choice enhances the sophisticated appearance but may require adjustment for those accustomed to wider shoes.

Sizing Tips from My Experience:

  • • Measure feet in the afternoon when they're naturally slightly swollen
  • • Try shoes with your typical dress sock thickness
  • • Walk around for at least 10 minutes during fitting
  • • Check for heel slippage and toe pressure points
