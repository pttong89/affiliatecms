What Are Platform UGGs and Why They're Taking Over Fashion

Platform UGGs represent the evolution of the classic comfort boot, featuring an elevated sole that typically adds 1.5 to 2.5 inches of height while maintaining the signature sheepskin comfort that made UGGs famous. Unlike traditional UGGs with their flat soles, these elevated versions combine the cozy interior with a chunky platform design that has captured the attention of fashion enthusiasts worldwide.

The platform UGGs trend gained massive momentum in 2022 and shows no signs of slowing down in 2025. Social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram have been flooded with styling videos, and celebrities from Hailey Bieber to Gigi Hadid have been spotted wearing them. The appeal lies in their ability to provide both comfort and a subtle height boost without sacrificing the laid-back aesthetic that UGGs are known for.

What sets platform UGGs apart is their versatility. The ultra mini platform UGGs offer a sleek, minimalist look that pairs perfectly with everything from leggings to midi skirts. Meanwhile, the classic platform versions provide more ankle coverage for those who prefer a traditional boot silhouette with modern elevation.

The construction of platform UGGs involves the same high-quality materials as regular UGGs—premium suede uppers, genuine sheepskin lining, and UGGplush wool blend—but with an innovative EVA or Treadlite outsole that creates the platform effect. This design not only adds height but also provides superior cushioning and shock absorption compared to traditional flat soles.

Fashion experts predict that platform UGGs will continue dominating the comfort footwear market because they address a key consumer desire: looking stylish while feeling comfortable. They bridge the gap between athleisure and elevated casual wear, making them perfect for our increasingly flexible lifestyle demands.

Care and Maintenance Tips for Your Platform UGGs

Proper care and maintenance are essential for maximizing the lifespan and appearance of your platform UGGs. The investment in quality footwear deserves appropriate attention to ensure years of comfortable wear and maintained aesthetic appeal.

Pre-Treatment Protection

Before wearing your new platform UGGs, apply a high-quality suede protector spray. This creates a barrier against water, stains, and dirt that can damage the suede upper. Reapply the protection every 2-3 months or after cleaning. I recommend the UGG Protector Spray, which is specifically formulated for their materials and won't alter the texture or color of the suede.

Daily Maintenance

After each wear, gently brush your platform UGGs with a suede brush to remove surface dirt and restore the nap of the suede. This simple step prevents dirt from settling deep into the material and maintains the boots' appearance. Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, and use cedar shoe trees to maintain their shape when not being worn.

Deep Cleaning Process

For more thorough cleaning, start by removing laces if applicable and brushing away loose dirt. For stains, use a specialized suede cleaner or create a mixture of equal parts white vinegar and water. Apply gently with a clean cloth, working in the direction of the suede grain. The platform sole can be cleaned separately with mild soap and water using a soft brush.

Never submerge platform UGGs in water or put them in washing machines or dryers. The combination of water and heat can cause the sheepskin lining to shrink and the suede to become stiff and discolored. If they become very wet, stuff them with newspaper to absorb moisture and maintain shape while air-drying at room temperature.

Seasonal Storage

During warmer months when platform UGGs aren't in regular rotation, proper storage is crucial. Clean them thoroughly, ensure they're completely dry, and stuff with acid-free tissue paper or cedar shoe trees. Store in breathable dust bags or boxes in a climate-controlled environment. Avoid plastic bags, which can trap moisture and promote mold growth.

Addressing Common Issues

Salt stains from winter sidewalks can be removed with a mixture of water and white vinegar applied gently with a soft cloth. For oil-based stains, cornstarch can absorb the oil if applied immediately and left overnight before brushing away. The platform sole may show wear patterns over time, which is normal and doesn't affect functionality.

Pro Tip

Rotate between multiple pairs if you wear platform UGGs frequently. This allows each pair to fully dry and recover between wears, significantly extending their lifespan.

With proper care, platform UGGs can maintain their comfort and appearance for several years. The key is consistency in maintenance and addressing issues promptly before they become permanent problems. Regular attention to your boots will ensure they continue to provide the comfort and style that made you choose them initially.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are platform UGGs comfortable for all-day wear?

Yes, platform UGGs are designed for all-day comfort. The elevated sole provides excellent cushioning and support, while the signature UGGplush wool lining keeps your feet cozy. The platform design actually distributes weight more evenly than regular shoes, reducing foot fatigue. I've personally worn mine for 8+ hour days and found them incredibly comfortable, though I recommend breaking them in gradually.

How do platform UGGs fit compared to regular UGGs?

Platform UGGs generally fit true to size, similar to regular UGGs. However, the platform sole can make them feel slightly more spacious. I recommend trying your normal UGG size first. If you're between sizes, consider going down half a size as the sheepskin lining will compress over time. The platform doesn't affect the interior fit significantly.

Can you wear platform UGGs in snow and wet weather?

While platform UGGs have better ground clearance than regular UGGs, they're still not fully waterproof. The suede upper can be treated with water-resistant spray for light moisture protection. For heavy snow or rain, consider dedicated winter boots. However, the platform sole does provide better traction and keeps your feet further from wet surfaces.

What's the height difference between platform and regular UGGs?

Platform UGGs typically add 1.5 to 2 inches of height compared to regular UGGs. The Classic Ultra Mini Platform has about a 1.75-inch platform sole, while some styles go up to 2.5 inches. This extra height gives you a subtle boost while maintaining the comfortable, casual aesthetic that UGGs are known for.

How do you clean and maintain platform UGGs?

Clean platform UGGs by first brushing away dirt with a suede brush. For stains, use a suede cleaner or mixture of water and white vinegar. Stuff with newspaper to maintain shape while drying away from direct heat. The platform sole can be cleaned with mild soap and water. Apply water-resistant spray regularly and use cedar shoe trees to maintain shape between wears.
