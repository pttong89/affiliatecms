Frequently Asked Questions

Are ASICS running shoes good for men with flat feet?

Yes, ASICS offers excellent options for men with flat feet. The Gel-Kayano 32 features advanced stability technology that provides support without harsh overcorrection. During my testing with flat-footed runners, the 4D Guidance System effectively controlled excessive pronation while maintaining comfort. The GT-2000 13 offers similar benefits at a lower price point. Both models use graduated support that adapts to your foot's natural movement patterns rather than fighting against them. I recommend visiting a specialty running store for gait analysis to determine if you need stability features, as some flat-footed runners actually have neutral gaits that work best with shoes like the Novablast 5.

How long do ASICS running shoes typically last?

Based on my extensive testing and durability analysis, most ASICS running shoes last 400-500 miles for average runners. However, this varies significantly by model and usage patterns. The Gel-Nimbus 27 consistently exceeded 450 miles in my testing, while the lighter Metaspeed Sky Paris typically shows wear around 300 miles due to its racing-oriented construction. Factors affecting longevity include your weight, gait efficiency, running surfaces, and maintenance habits. Heavier runners or those with inefficient gaits may see shorter lifespans, while lighter, efficient runners often exceed the typical mileage ranges. The high-quality AHAR rubber compounds used in ASICS outsoles generally provide above-average durability compared to competitors.

What's the difference between ASICS Gel-Nimbus and Novablast series?

The Gel-Nimbus and Novablast series serve different purposes in the ASICS lineup. The Gel-Nimbus 27 prioritizes maximum cushioning and comfort, featuring a 43.5mm heel stack with traditional stability geometry perfect for easy runs and recovery days. The Novablast 5 offers more versatility with its 41.5mm stack and energetic FlyteFoam Blast Max midsole that works well for both easy runs and uptempo efforts. During my comparative testing, the Nimbus felt more luxurious and protective, while the Novablast felt more responsive and versatile. The Nimbus costs about $25 more but provides superior step-in comfort and durability. Choose Nimbus for maximum comfort and easy miles, or Novablast for versatile daily training that can handle various paces and distances.

Can I use ASICS racing shoes for daily training?

While technically possible, using racing shoes like the Metaspeed Sky Paris for daily training isn't recommended for most runners. These shoes are engineered for specific performance demands with lightweight construction that sacrifices durability. During my testing, racing shoes showed significantly faster wear patterns and provided less impact protection than training models. The aggressive geometry and firm midsoles can also increase injury risk when used for high weekly mileage. However, the Superblast 2 bridges this gap effectively, offering near-racing performance in a more durable training package. If you want racing-inspired performance for daily use, choose the Superblast 2 over pure racing models. Reserve true racing shoes for competitions and specific speed workouts to maximize their lifespan and effectiveness.

Do ASICS shoes run true to size for men?

ASICS generally runs true to size, but fit varies slightly between models based on their intended use and construction. During my fit testing across multiple models, I found the Novablast 5 and Gel-Nimbus 27 fit true to size with adequate toe box room for most foot shapes. The Metaspeed Sky Paris runs slightly snug due to its performance-oriented construction, so consider sizing up a half size if you prefer more room or have wider feet. The Gel-Kayano 32 offers the roomiest fit in the ASICS lineup. Most runners should start with their normal athletic shoe size, but I strongly recommend trying shoes on later in the day when feet are naturally swollen to simulate running conditions. ASICS also offers wide and extra-wide options in most models for runners needing additional room.
