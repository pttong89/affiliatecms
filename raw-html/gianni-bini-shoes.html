Frequently Asked Questions

Are Gianni Bini shoes true to size?

For wider feet, consider sizing up half a size or look for styles specifically marked as wide width options, which are available in select designs. The Italian leather construction means the shoes will stretch and conform to your foot shape with wear, so initial snugness often resolves within a few wears.

What is the price range for Gianni Bini shoes?

Gianni Bini shoes range from $79 to $159 for most styles, positioning them in the accessible luxury category.
