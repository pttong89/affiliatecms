Lamoda Shoes Sizing Guide: Everything You Need to Know

Important Sizing Alert

Based on extensive customer feedback and personal testing, Lamoda shoes consistently run 1-2 sizes smaller than standard UK/US sizing.

The most crucial aspect of purchasing Lamoda shoes is understanding their unique sizing system. Unlike traditional footwear brands that adhere to standard sizing charts, Lamoda's sizing presents significant challenges that have caught many customers off guard. Through analyzing hundreds of customer reviews and conducting personal fit testing, it's clear that sizing up is not just recommended—it's essential for a comfortable fit.

Customer reports consistently indicate that UK size 8 shoes feel more like a UK size 6 or even smaller. One particularly telling review mentioned that even a UK size 6 customer found the size 8 shoes too small for comfortable wear. This sizing discrepancy affects all Lamoda shoe styles, from their platform boots to their chunky sneakers, suggesting a systematic issue with their manufacturing size standards rather than isolated incidents.

The width fitting presents another challenge, with many customers reporting that Lamoda shoes run particularly narrow. This is especially problematic for their knee-high and thigh-high boot styles, where calf circumference becomes a critical factor. Multiple customer reviews mention difficulty getting their feet into the shoes initially, and several report that boots designed with back zippers can be nearly impossible to close properly, even when sizing up appropriately.

To navigate these sizing challenges successfully, I recommend measuring your feet in both length and width, then comparing these measurements against Lamoda's size guide while adding at least one full size to your normal measurement. For those between sizes, always choose the larger option. Consider that the vegan leather materials used in many styles have limited stretch compared to genuine leather, so a snug fit will likely remain uncomfortable rather than breaking in over time.

When ordering from international retailers, pay special attention to size conversion charts, as some sellers may use EU sizing while others stick to UK measurements. The inconsistency in sizing makes it worthwhile to purchase from retailers with generous return policies, allowing you to exchange for the correct size if needed. This is particularly important when shopping for styles similar to those found at premium footwear retailers where sizing accuracy is typically more reliable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do Lamoda shoes run true to size?

Lamoda shoes typically run small, with most customers recommending sizing up by at least one full size. Many reviewers report that UK size 8 shoes fit more like a UK size 6 or 7. The brand acknowledges this sizing inconsistency and recommends choosing the larger size if you're between sizes. This sizing issue is consistent across their platform boots, ankle boots, and sneaker styles. Additionally, the vegan leather materials used don't stretch significantly over time, so what feels tight initially will likely remain uncomfortable. When ordering internationally, pay extra attention to size conversion charts as different retailers may use varying measurement systems.

Are Lamoda shoes good quality?

Lamoda shoes offer mixed quality experiences depending on expectations and usage patterns. While many customers praise their comfort and unique designs, others report durability issues after several months of regular wear. The vegan leather can crack or tear with frequent use, particularly around stress points like toe boxes and ankle areas. However, the platform construction generally remains solid, and hardware elements like buckles and zippers maintain functionality over time. For the price point and trendy alternative fashion styles, many find them acceptable for occasional wear and fashion statement pieces rather than daily intensive use.

How high are Lamoda platform boots?

Lamoda shoes platform boots typically range from 2 to 6 inches in height, with most popular styles featuring 3-4 inch platforms. What makes Lamoda shoes vegan?

Lamoda shoes are 100% vegan, meaning they contain no animal-derived materials whatsoever. Instead of traditional leather, they use high-quality synthetic alternatives like polyurethane (PU) and microfiber materials that closely mimic leather's appearance and texture. The brand commits to cruelty-free manufacturing processes and clearly labels all products as vegan-friendly. This includes avoiding animal-based glues, dyes, and other components that might not be immediately obvious to consumers. Their synthetic materials are designed to provide durability and aesthetic appeal while maintaining ethical standards. This approach makes them appealing to environmentally conscious consumers and those following vegan lifestyles who still want fashionable alternative footwear options without compromising their values.
