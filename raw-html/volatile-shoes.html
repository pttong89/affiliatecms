Sizing Guide & Fit Tips

Critical Sizing Information

Most volatile shoes run SMALL, especially wedge styles. I strongly recommend ordering at least half a size up from your normal size.

This sizing tendency is consistent across multiple customer reviews and my personal testing experience. The brand acknowledges that sizing can vary between styles, so always check individual product reviews before ordering.

How to Measure Your Feet

  1. Measure both feet - Most people have slightly different sized feet, so measure both and use the larger measurement
  2. Measure in the evening - Feet naturally swell throughout the day, so evening measurements are more accurate
  3. Wear appropriate socks - Use the type of socks you'll wear with the shoes during measurement
  4. Stand while measuring - Weight bearing provides a more accurate foot length and width
  5. Measure from heel to longest toe - This might not always be your big toe

Volatile Sizing Formula

Your Normal Size + 0.5 to 1 full size = Recommended Volatile Size
Example: If you normally wear size 8, order size 8.5 or 9 in Volatile
