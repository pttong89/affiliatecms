Frequently Asked Questions

Which On Cloud shoe is best for women's walking?

The On Cloudmonster 2 stands out as the best overall choice for women's walking. It offers maximum cushioning with 32mm heel height, weighs only 230g in women's sizing, and provides excellent comfort for all-day wear. The CloudTec technology delivers superior energy return while maintaining stability, making it ideal for extended walks, city exploring, and daily activities. For women seeking the ultimate in walking comfort, this model consistently receives top ratings from both experts and users.

Are On Cloud shoes good for women with wide feet?

Yes, several On Cloud models work well for women with wide feet. The Cloudmonster 2 offers the roomiest toe box, while the Cloud 5 provides moderate width accommodation. The Cloudtilt features a slip-on design that adapts to foot shape without pressure points from traditional laces. However, women with significantly wide feet should consider going up half a size or trying the shoes in-store first. The best On Cloud shoes for wide feet typically require careful sizing consideration.

How long do On Cloud women's walking shoes last?

On Cloud women's walking shoes typically last 400-600 miles of walking, which translates to 8-12 months for average daily walkers. The durability depends on walking surface, frequency of use, and individual gait patterns. The CloudTec pods may show wear first, but the overall construction is built to handle extensive walking sessions. In my testing, the Cloudmonster 2 maintained excellent cushioning properties even after 500+ miles, making them excellent value for regular walkers.

What's the difference between On Cloud running and walking shoes?

On Cloud walking shoes feature more cushioning, lower heel-to-toe drop (typically 6-8mm vs 10-12mm), and firmer midsole construction for stability during longer periods of standing and walking. Running shoes prioritize energy return and forward propulsion, while walking shoes focus on comfort, support, and all-day wearability. The best On Cloud for running models often overlap with excellent walking options, but walking-specific models offer superior comfort for extended wear.

Can I wear On Cloud walking shoes for plantar fasciitis?

Many On Cloud models can help with plantar fasciitis symptoms due to their cushioning and arch support. The Cloudmonster 2 and Cloudrunner 2 offer the most support with their structured midsoles and heel cushioning. However, women with severe plantar fasciitis should consult a podiatrist and may need custom orthotics, which are compatible with most On Cloud models. The best On Cloud shoes for standing all day often provide the support needed for foot condition management.
